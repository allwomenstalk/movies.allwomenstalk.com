# 7 TV Characters Id Love to Have in My Life ...

There are plenty of TV characters I’d love to have in my life. Unfortunately for me, they’re just TV characters. Anyway! Sometimes I like to think of what life would be like if some characters were real. So I thought I’d share that with you. Here are seven TV characters I’d love to have in my life.

## 1. Donna Paulsen

Donna Paulsen from “Suits” would be an absolute life saver. She is one of the more organized TV characters that I’ve ever seen. Not only that, but she’s smart, witty, sarcastic, and loyal. Is there any question as to why she made it onto a list of TV characters I’d love to have in my life?! I mean, I don’t need her to organize my entire life (or maybe I do, but that’s not the point) but she’s definitely someone I could hang out with and probably call every so often to help keep me on track.

## 2. Joan Harris

Much like Donna from “Suits,” Joan is a smart, witty, and loyal person while also being very organized and helpful to those around her. She’s noticed as important by those she works with as a woman in the '60s which is a great accomplishment, even if she is just a TV character. Joan just stands out to me above a lot of characters on “Mad Men” and I think she’s one of the few people on the show that I would actually enjoy spending time with (other than Don of course).

## 3. The Doctor

Which Doctor do I mean? Well, all of them! I would love to know every single incarnation of The Doctor from “Doctor Who” (especially the 10th Doctor played by David Tennant, but that’s because I have a crush on him). Despite the fact that some of the incarnations are viewed as better than others, I want to know all of them and I want to go on adventures with all of them! Is it so much to ask that this time traveling alien be real and come take me on crazy adventures through all of time and space!? I don’t think so!

## 4. Schmidt

It wouldn’t be an article by me if I didn’t include “New Girl.” Schmidt quickly gave every other character on “New Girl” a run for their money as my favorite character. He’s so funny and an amazing friend to the people he cares about, what’s not to love?! If I could spend a day with Schmidt, it would be one of the best days ever. With every episode, I see more and more to love about him. He’s an amazing friend to those on the show, so I’d love to have him in my life!

## 5. Samantha Jones

Samantha Jones, from “Sex and the City,” and I are VERY different! I wouldn’t go so far as to say I’m like Charlotte, but that’s probably who I relate to most on the show. The thing is that, unlike Charlotte, I think I would embrace the wild side of Samantha! I love her sassy, tell it like it is attitude. Sometimes you just need someone to snap you out of it and Samantha is definitely that person!

## 6. Charlie Kelly

I love Charlie from “It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia”! Even though his voice may irritate some people and he can be really dumb, he’s just so funny. I don’t know that I could ever go to his apartment because it’s really gross and messy and I probably couldn’t get in on his crazy schemes, but I’d love to hear about them! He just seems like he would be so much fun to hang out with and listen to all the crazy stories he probably has. He’s the person I’d call after a long day of work to cheer me up.

## 7. Andy Botwin

I’d say that Andy Botwin from “Weeds” is the most loyal character on this list. That is the main reason he made it actually. Well that and I think he’s really funny and just want to hang out with him and listen to the weird stories he would tell me. Andy is loyal to a fault. While I think that’s a great thing, I also think someone would have to snap him out of it and tell him to live for himself and since I’m always doing that to people anyway, I think we would make a great pair!

I’m sure there a ton of other characters that would be great to have in my life, but I can only write so much! Which characters do you want in your life? Do you disagree with any of these?
