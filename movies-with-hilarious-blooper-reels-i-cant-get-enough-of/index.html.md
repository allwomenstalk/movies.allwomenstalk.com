# 7 Movies with Hilarious Blooper Reels I Cant Get Enough of ...

There are so many movies with hilarious blooper reels. However, I’ll never, ever get tired of these seven right here. Whenever I need a good laugh, all I need to do is look these up. The crazy part about them is that no matter how many times I watch them, they’re always just as funny as the first time. Check out these movies with hilarious blooper reels! You’ll crack up, trust me.

## 1. Date Night

No matter how many times I watch this movie, I’m left in tears from laughing so much. Steve Carell can honestly do no wrong! He and Tina Fey have amazing chemistry on screen and everything they say is just effortlessly funny. This is my top pick for movies with hilarious blooper reels because it’ll never get old. Watching Steve call James Franco a “whore” over and over again cracks me up. I know I can’t be the only one!

## 2. Bridesmaids

The bloopers for this movie crack me up. Can you imagine if they just let the camera roll and let these hilarious actors improvise the entire movie? I would definitely pay to watch that! For now, this video will have to do. I love seeing Rebel Wilson before she was Fat Amy!

## 3. 21 Jump Street

This movie is gold. It kept me laughing from start to finish. Jonah Hill and Channing Tatum are my new favorite pair to watch onscreen! I am so incredibly stoked that the sequel comes out next summer! I also love that Nick Offerman (Ron Swanson from Parks & Rec) and Jake Johnson (Nick Miller from New Girl) are in this movie. Bonus points!

## 4. Dinner for Schmucks

Anything with Paul Rudd and Steve Carell is golden. I’ll never get enough of this movie because I’ll never get enough of watching the two of them onscreen together. Oh, and Zach Galifianakis, too?! Comedy geniuses. This movie is hilarious but the blooper reel is my favorite part. Whenever I need a good laugh, this is my go-to video.

## 5. Friends with Benefits

How could you ever get enough of Justin Timberlake and Mila Kunis goofing around? This blooper reel is fantastic! I think they have such great chemistry together! If I didn't think Mila and Ashton Kutcher were the cutest thing together, I'd totally ship her with JT (if he wasn't with Jessica Biel, of course.)

## 6. This is the End

I just recently watched this movie and oh my God, I am so happy that I did. This movie was hilarious! The entire group of actors is fantastic. My favorites were definitely Jonah Hill and James Franco. Of course, I can’t forget Michael Cera! If you haven’t watched this movie yet, do it today. Your stomach will be hurting from laughing so much.

## 7. Easy a

I’ll never get tired of this movie. Emma Stone is so flawless! This is the type of movie I can watch pretty much everyday and never get tired of. The writing of it and the plot are already funny, but the cast is what makes it even funnier. It's a movie everyone should watch!

There you have it! These are the movies that have my favorite blooper reels. All I have to do is watch any of these videos and I know I’ll be cracking up. What’s your favorite movie/blooper reel?
