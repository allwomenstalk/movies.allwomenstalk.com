# Season 19 of Dancing with the Stars: Here is Who Won the Mirrorball Trophy!

Season 19 of **Dancing with the Stars** (DWTS) officially came to a thrilling conclusion on Tuesday night, November 25th, on ABC. The competition was fierce, with the final four celebrities giving it their all for the coveted Mirrorball Trophy. If you’ve been following the journey of these talented stars, you know that the finale was a joyous celebration of talent and hard work.

## The Final Four Contestants

The [final four stars](https://movies.allwomenstalk.com/dancing-with-the-stars-season-19-contestants/) who made it to the last stage of the competition were:

- **Alfonso Ribeiro** (Actor)
- **Sadie Robertson** (Reality Star)
- **Janel Parrish** (Actress)
- **Bethany Mota** (YouTube Star)

These four were perfect for the finale; they were a joy to watch every single week, bringing unique energy and growth to the ballroom floor.

## And the Winner Is...

Let’s talk about what really matters: the winners! The **Season 19 of Dancing with the Stars** champions are **Alfonso Ribeiro** and his professional partner, **Witney Carson**! The duo has killed it week after week, overcoming challenges and injuries to prove they truly deserved the win. You go, Alfonso! His win was a long time coming for the _Fresh Prince of Bel-Air_ star, and you can keep up with him on [his official Twitter](https://twitter.com/alfonso_ribeiro)
