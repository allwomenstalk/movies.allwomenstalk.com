# Dancing with the Stars Crowns Their Season 19 Champs ...

Season 19 of DWTS came to an end Tuesday night, November 25th, on ABC! The final four stars were Bethany Mota, Janel Parrish, Sadie Robertson and Alfonso Ribeiro. If you've been keeping up with the show, you know these four were perfect for the finale; they were a joy to watch every single week!

Anyways, let's talk about what really matters... the winners! The season 19 champs are Alfonso Ribeiro and Witney Carson! The duo has killed it week after week despite their occasional injuries and definitely deserve the win. You go, Alfonso!

Since it didn't take two hours to announce the winner, the night was jam packed with awesome performances. Jennifer Hudson, Nick Jonas, and Meghan Trainor hit the stage to perform their hit songs.
