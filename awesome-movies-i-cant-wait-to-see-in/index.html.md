# 8 Awesome Movies I Cant Wait to See in 2013 ...

Do you know how many awesome upcoming movies for 2013 are right around the corner? November is here now and that means that it's almost 2013, which further means that a slew of great upcoming movies for 2013 are coming up! Do you have a list of movies that you want to see? I do, and I'm going to share it below! So get ready and let's take a look at list of awesome movies for 2013!

## 1. The Hobbit: the Desolation of Smaug

I love, love, love the Hobbit. It is not only a book that I've read so much that the cover is worn to almost nothing, but the LOTR trilogy wasn't enough for me! I love all of the movies (in fact, I own all three extended versions), but I am so wicked excited for this 2013 movie blockbuster that I'm going to see it on opening night – midnight showing, baby! Truthfully, I'm excited for all three installments!

## 2. Oz: the Great and Powerful

Another 2013 movie that I can't wait to see is Oz: The Great and Powerful. Now, I am a huge fan of the Wizard of Oz. I think it's such a unique story and one that has been done up in so many ways, but this version touched me. Is this movie on your list? It's definitely a blockbuster for me!

## 3. The Hunger Games: Catching Fire

Oh man, you had to know that no list of awesome upcoming movies for 2013 would be complete without The Hunger Games, right? I cannot, I repeat, cannot wait for this movie to come out! The casting looks amazing and although this trailer here is fanmade, it still looks awesome! The official trailer isn't out, but stay tuned, I'm sure we'll have it soon enough!

## 4. Hansel and Gretel: Witch Hunters

By far, one of my favorite fairy tales is Hansel and Gretel. I don't think that this particular fairy tale has been told enough and while Grimm's wrote it best, I think that this movie looks amazing. It's unique, it's totally different and it's going to be the talk of the town when it comes out! Would you see it opening day?

## 5. Grown Ups 2

Okay, I know what you are going to say – why is this on here? This movie is very personal to me and that's because the cast and crew stayed at a hotel that my office represents. Oh yeah, did I mention that I work for a hotel chain on the side? That's my corporate life and that's why I am so excited about this 2013 Adam Sandler movie! It was filmed in MA!

## 6. The Great Gatsby

Now, I have to admit, I just saw The Great Gatsby starring Robert Redford and Mia Farrow last week but I loved it! That's why this 2013 movie is topping my charts. Tobey MaGuire? I can't wait to see him in this! Leo DiCaprio? He is going to be great! If you haven't seen the trailer, watch it! It's amazing and this is definitely going to be a hit!

## 7. Monsters University

If you ever watched Monster's Inc, this flick has got to be on your list for awesome upcoming movies for 2013! It's going to be super funny, totally awesome and it's a kiddie movie! I can promise, I won't be ashamed of ducking into a theater with this ticket stub in my wallet.

## 8. Iron Man 3

Finally girls, the last 2013 movie that I'm going to talk about is packed with action and lots of RDJ goodness! Iron Man 1 and 2 were some of my favorite movies in the world and I can't wait until Iron Man 3 comes out! What about you, girls?

So, there you have it! All of my top 2013 movies that are not only fantastic, but that I am so excited for! What other 2013 movies are you excited about? Give 'em up!
