# 10 Movies like Bridesmaids Every Chick Flick Lover Will Enjoy ...

Invite your girls for a sleepover and watch these funny movies like the [Bridesmaids](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1478338/). These movies are so much more than just chick flicks, they are movies about strong women, lasting friendship, and girl power. They remind us that if women stick together there is nothing we cannot achieve. Three cheers for female empowerment!

## 1. Bachelorette (2012)

I have yet to watch a movie starring Rebel Wilson that has not made me laugh! Becky (Rebel Wilson) gets engaged and invites her three besties from school to her wedding but none of them have their lives together; one of them is jealous of Becky for getting married first, the second one is a cocaine addict and the third one loves to party! Trouble ensues when they reunite. It’s a fun-filled movie sure to make you laugh out loud.

## 2. 27 Dresses (2008)

If you love wedding movies, and you have not watched this one. You have just hit the jackpot! This is about a girl who has been a bridesmaid in 27 weddings. She is obsessed with weddings and has dreamt about her own perfect wedding and the perfect man since she was a little kid. Things change when she meets a reporter who hates weddings.

## 3. Bride Wars (2009)

This is one of my favourite wedding movies of all time! It is about two inseparable best friends played by Anne Hathaway and Kate Hudson who are perfect for the roles. Since they were kids, they have dreamt together about their perfect wedding, but things turn sour when their wedding dates are accidentally booked on the same day!

## 4. Revenge of the Bridesmaids (2010)

Two best friends discover that their childhood best friend’s boyfriend has been stolen by their frenemy. They both come together to get revenge by becoming the bride’s bridesmaids and stopping the wedding. This is such a funny, light-hearted movie, you will love it!

## 5. My Best Friend’s Wedding (1997)

The gorgeous Julia Roberts and stunning Cameron Diaz star in this movie! Julianne Potter (Julia Roberts) made a pact with her best friend Michael that if neither of them is married at 28, they would marry each other. But three weeks before Juliane turns 28, Michael tells her that he is marrying Kimmy (Cameron Diaz). Julianne realizes she is in love with Michael and decides to sabotage the wedding.

## 6. The Other Woman (2014)

This movie is all about girl power. A group of women comes together to plot revenge on a guy who has been cheating on all of them! This movie has the perfect cast with Cameron Diaz; playing the strong independent woman, Leslie Mann; playing the perfect wife, and Kate Upton; playing the hot girl with the perfect body, and the bad boy played by no other than, the famous kingslayer; Nikolaj Coster Waldau. This is a super hit sleepover movie!

## 7. Girls Trip (2017)

This movie follows a group of women who have grown distant over the years but they plan to reunite and go on an exciting adventure together. We can never have enough of these kinda movies!

## 8. Sex and the City 1 & 2 (2008, 2010)

These movies are an extension of the hit TV series but even if you have not watched the series, you will love the movies! The story of the four chic New York girls continues in the movie where they struggle and overcome obstacles in their family, work and love life while taking luxurious girl vacations. There are rumors about a third film but nothing is confirmed yet.

## 9. Bad Moms (2016)

This movie is not about weddings but it is about a group of women who are trying to figure out the mom life while managing everything else. It is a must watch for all busy moms!

## 10. My Big Fat Greek Wedding (2002)

This movie is about a Greek girl and her big traditional family. She is 30 years old and her family constantly talks about fixing her marriage with a Greek man, but fate had other plans when she falls in love with a man who is definitely not Greek. There is a sequel to this movie that came out in 2016.

I have watched the movies on this list quite a few times with my girls and I always have a super fun time watching it. These are the kind of movies you can watch over and over but never get bored. Which movie sounds most fun? And which scene made you LOL in Bridesmaids?
