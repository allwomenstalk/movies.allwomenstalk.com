# Broadway's "of Mice and Men" is Hitting Theaters Next Month ...

Did you miss your chance to see "Of Mice and Men" on Broadway this past summer? Or feel like watching it over again? Well, here's your chance! "Of Mice and Men" starring James Franco, Chris O'Dowd, and Leighton Meester will screen in international cinemas starting Nov. 6th! It's a limited engagement so make sure you go out and watch it while you have the chance. To find your nearest venue, check out [ntlive.nationaltheatre.org.uk](https://www.ntlive.com/).
