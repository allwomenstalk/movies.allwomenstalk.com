# 7 Mini Series to Put on Your Watch List ASAP ...

I think I have too many many **mini series to binge watch** on my to do list. Except, it IS one of my [favorite things to do](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/of-my-favorite-things-to-do-in-summer/), so can you ever have too many? With long shows, you lose interest or forget about it by the time the last episode is released - I get so impatient waiting to know how the story ends. Miniseries, on the other hand, keep you captivated and interested in the story and the best part is you don’t have to wait for years or even months to know how it ends! Here are the **mini series to binge watch** that should be on your radar.

## 1. BIG LITTLE LIES

Genre: Crime, comedy

Once you start watching this show you are going to be hooked. It is gripping, mysterious and the cast is amazing! Starring Reese Witherspoon, Nicole Kidman, Shailene Woodley and other amazing actors.

## 2. GIRL BOSS

Genre: Comedy

Based on a novel written by Sophia Amoruso, Nasty Gal’s founder. This show is loosely based on her life and how she started her fashion clothing line.

## 3. 13 REASONS WHY

Genre: Drama, Mystery

Based on a novel, this show is eye opening. But grab a tissue box, there’s a fair chance you might end up crying.

## 4. FRIENDS from COLLEGE

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Laugh out loud watching this hilarious show about 6 friends.

## 5. GYPSY

Genre: Drama, Thriller

This show has been on my watch list for a while now and I’m so excited to start watching it finally.

## 6. SHERLOCK

Genre: Drama, Crime, Mystery

A brilliant show. Mystery lovers are going to be obsessed with it.

## 7. PLANET EARTH 1 and 2

Genre: Documentary

This Emmy Award winning show took 5 years to film, and is the first nature documentary series to be filmed in HD, not to mention the most expensive! This show is not just for nature lovers, anyone can watch it and anyone who has falls in loves with it.
