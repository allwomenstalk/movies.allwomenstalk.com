# Netflix Needs to Add These Shows Pronto ...

There are so many TV shows that Netflix should have available for instant streaming. Seriously, they would double in the amount of subscribers if they were to add some classic fan-favorite shows! Of course, I do need to applaud Netflix for recently making Gilmore Girls available (Thank you, Netflix Gods!) and recently announcing that Friends will be available in its entirety starting January 1st. Now, if they were to just add these shows as well, all would be right in the world. Here are some TV shows that Netflix should have:

## 1. The Golden Girls

I know I can't be the only one who's still obsessed with this show. I can't even explain how happy I get when a Golden Girls marathon is on! Betty White, Bea Arthur, Rue McClanahan, and Estelle Getty are comedic geniuses and everyone needs to know it. If Netflix were to make the series available for instant streaming, people would be in for some serious laughs. This is just one of the many TV shows that Netflix should have.

## 2. The O.c

If you still watch reruns of The O.C., it's probably your guilty-pleasure TV show. I mean, Adam Brody, Rachel Bilson, Benjamin McKenzie... what more could you possibly need?! Seriously Netflix, it should be a crime that this series isn't easily accessible to all of us. It would definitely be worth a binge-watching session.

## 3. Veronica Mars

I know I can't be alone in thinking that Netflix's viewership would skyrocket if they put this series on Instant Streaming. It's actually available through the DVD section of Netflix but who even has time for that?! We need the series available for instant satisfaction and while they're at it, Netflix should feel free to add the Veronica Mars movie to Instant Streaming as well!

## 4. Modern Family

How is this not on Netflix yet?! I'm a big fan of the show but I've only seen random episodes from random seasons. I'd love to be able to watch it from season one and work my way through the series. But first, Netflix needs to add it to Instant Streaming! How else are people going to learn about Phil Dunphy's words of wisdom and experience the rest of the family?!

## 5. Community

This is another TV show that I really want to watch from the beginning! It'd be awesome if Netflix added it to their site because who can ever get enough of Joel McHale, Donald Glover, and Yvette Nicole Brown?

## 6. Awkward

This MTV show is ridiculously popular so it's beyond me as to why it isn't available for Instant Streaming! What gives, Netflix?! With all the drama that goes on and love triangles that take place throughout the seasons, we need to be able to binge-watch and obsess over it all. The show's currently on its last season!

## 7. Seinfeld

With Seinfeld's cable and broadcast TV deals expiring this fall, there have been talks about Netflix getting their hands all over it. Personally, I would love nothing more! I'll admit, Seinfeld might not be for everybody but it's definitely for me. Let's hope Netflix gets the deal before Amazon comes after it!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome TV shows Netflix needs to have, like ASAP. Can you think of any other shows you'd love to be able to binge-watch on Netflix?
