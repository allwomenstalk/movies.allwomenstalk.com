# Girls with a GSOH Will Love These Netflix Comedies ...

There’s not a day that cannot be made better with a good laugh. And if nothing has made you smile today, or any other day, there’s always Netflix to turn to. From the slightly quirky to the downright bizarre from sitcoms to sketch shows, these Netflix comedies are sure to tickle your funny bone.

## 1. The IT Crowd

This is a quintessentially awkward British sitcom about the lives of a group of nerdy workers who operate as a company’s IT department. There’s lots of tech-related humor but not ostracizing to people who don’t understand computers!

## 2. New Girl

Zooey Deschanel is at her most quirky and cute in this successful sitcom about a group of 20 somethings living together and trying their best to become fully adult!

## 3. Bob’s Burgers

Bob’s Burgers is probably everybody’s favorite animated show right now, and if you have seen it yet you are lucky that it is available to catch up on Netflix!

## 4. Wet Hot American Summer: First Day of Camp

It’s the addition to the cult classic Wet Hot American Summer that we never knew we needed! Everybody is back, and they are all older, but somehow they seem even fresher and funnier than before!

## 5. Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt

Tina Fey has knocked it out of the park once again with this comedy about a woman who is released from years of confinement and takes on the big wide world of New York City.

## 6. Grace and Frankie

Not since The Golden Girls has there been such a hilarious show about elderly women living together. Jane Fonda and Lily Tomlin are an absolute dream team!

## 7. Peep Show

Another sitcom for those of you who revel in the sarcasm and awkwardness of modern British comedy: a hilariously cynical sitcom about two flatmates who couldn’t be more different.

## 8. BoJack Horseman

A Netflix original series, this is a hilarious adult animation about a horse actor who used to be A-list but whose life has become increasingly more depressing!

## 9. Doc Martin

A charming British drama/comedy set in a quaint seaside town about a Doctor who dislikes most of his patients and faints at the sight of blood!

## 10. Parks and Recreation

Amy Poehler has never been funnier as Leslie Knope, the kind hearted but rather hapless, member of the parks and recreation board in the fictional town of Pawnee, Indiana.

## 11. Portlandia

A really fun series starring Carrie Brownstein and Fred Armisen that pokes fun at the hipster, laid back way of life in Portland, Oregon.

## 12. Arrested Development

Arrested Development was something of a cult hit when it first came out, but since then it has gained a huge following and the most recent season made just for Netflix was so great.

## 13. Archer

Another adult animated series, this time a hilarious send up of the James Bond story, full of sex, drugs and rock and roll!

## 14. Lilyhammer

Any girl who misses the drama and comedy mix of the Sopranos will love Lilyhammer: a series about a former mobster who chooses to move to Norway as part of a witness protection program.

## 15. Louie

Do I really need to write an explanation about why you should be watching Louie? The guy is one of America’s hottest comedians right now; pretty much everything he touches turns to gold of late!

## 16. 30 Rock

The show that sealed the deal for Tina Fey’s status as a national treasure. Relive all of the glory years of 30 Rock and fall in love with all the characters all over again!

## 17. Orange is the New Black

Sure, there is a lot of drama that goes down in OITNB, but there is also a shed load of comedy in every episode too. Sometimes the best humor comes from depressing situations!

Check the Netflix comedy listings too, because there might be a gem there just waiting for you.
