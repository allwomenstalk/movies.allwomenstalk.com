# 7 Movies That Are Turning 10 Years Old in 2014 ...

This list of movies that are turning 10 this year is sure to make you feel super old. No, really. I can't believe it's been 10 years since these movies! I guess it doesn't seem like so long because I still watch these movies constantly (oops). Are you ready to say "Are you serious?!" every five seconds? If so, check out the movies that are turning 10 this year. Comment with your reactions!

## 1. Mean Girls

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/VuAaoS)

Yup, it's been ten years since "fetch" and "grool" became a part of your vocabulary. Can you believe it? Rachel McAdams still hasn't aged - I mean, does she ever?! A lot has happened since this movie. Rachel and Amanda Seyfried have great careers and it doesn't look like they'll be going anywhere anytime soon. Lohan? Eh, I'll let you make your own judgments on her. The list of movies that are turning 10 this year just gets better and better.

## 2. The Notebook

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/the-notebook/id595178029)

How has it been 10 years since one of the most tear-jerking love stories?! I don't know about you, but this movie is still very much a part of my life. I mean, Ryan Gosling? How can you refuse an opportunity to watch him proclaim his love while standing in the rain? Noah and Allie will always be one of my favorite movie couples. I can't believe it's been ten years already!

## 3. Napoleon Dynamite

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/napoleon-dynamite/id270007388)

Whaaaat?! It's been ten years since we all voted for Pedro? Unbelievable. This movie still manages to get some laughs out of me each time I watch it. The cool thing about this movie is that the next generation that watches it will still love it as much as we all did. That's the power of a great, hilarious movie!

## 4. A Cinderella Story

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/a-cinderella-story/id271328451)

No way. Look how young Hilary Duff looks in the trailer! It definitely doesn't feel like it's been so long since this movie came out. Then again, there's been so many sequels to it! Of course, the original beats them all. Hilary Duff and Chad Michael Murray will always be the best

## 5. 13 Going on 30

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/13-going-on-30/id264038287)

I'm never not in the mood to watch 13 Going on 30. It's one of my favorite movies! Jennifer Garner and Mark Ruffalo are great together. Let's not forget that awesome "Thriller" dance scene! I can't believe it's been a decade since then.

## 6. 50 First Dates

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/50-first-dates/id280360127)

Every time I watch this movie I get the Beach Boys stuck in my head. This is such a beautiful romantic comedy! Drew Barrymore and Adam Sandler can do no wrong. No matter how many years go by, this will always be one of my favorite movies! What's your favorite part about the movie?

## 7. White Chicks

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/white-chicks/id271778179)

It's always the right time to watch White Chicks. This movie cracks me up! I know I'm not the only one who still makes references to this movie. At least I hope I'm not.. It's been ten years but no amount of time will make this movie any less funny!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of MANY epic movies that will be turning 10 years old this year. So, let's be honest, how old did this list make you feel? Also, which movie from this list do you STILL find yourself watching?
