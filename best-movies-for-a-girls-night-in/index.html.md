# 5 Best Movies for a Girls Night in ...

Are you on the hunt for the **best movies for a girls' night in**?
Who doesn’t love a night in with the girls? It’s a time to watch classic movies, binge on junk food, and gossip. In my opinion, everyone has a set list of chick flicks that they watch whenever a girls night arises and I’m here to give you even more ideas. This list could be a mile long but I narrowed it down to the top 5 **best movies for a girls' night in**.

## 1. Mean Girls

This is a staple chick flick, as far as I’m concerned it’ll continue being watched for years to come. Halloween costumes are inspired by this movie and it is quoted on a daily basis, “I’m a mouse duh.” That's why it's one of the **best movies for a girls' night in**.

## 2. Clueless

This is a classic in my opinion and I watch it as much as I can, definitely a must for any girls night. It follows a privileged teenager Cher through high school drama and the ups and downs of life.

## 3. 10 Things I Hate about You

Who would pass up watching young Heath Ledger in a movie? In order for her younger sister to date Kat must first have a boyfriend. As an audience we get to see how the younger sister swindles her way into getting a date for prom.

## 4. 27 Dresses

Always a bridesmaid and never the bride? This movie showcases the life of an all time bridesmaid, Jane. If you and your friends love fashion and romance, this is a great
combination of both.

## 5. How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

This is a perfect balance between romance and comedy. It follows Andie, a writer for a magazine, and her journey of losing a guy in 10 days. The plot isn’t as simple as that though, so watch to find out what happens in the end.
