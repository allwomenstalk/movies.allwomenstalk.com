# 5 Best Romantic Christmas Movies to Watch This Season ...

Wondering about the **best romantic Christmas movies**? It's a great time of year to get cozy and enjoy something that puts you both in the holiday frame of mind.

It’s here. It’s finally here! There is just something so magical about this time of year. The holidays bring cheer and love and to celebrate that, we have the top 5 **best romantic Christmas movies** to showcase the wonder of the holidays. So get your hot cocoa ready and prepare to feel all the love.

## 1. Love Actually

Is this even a surprise that it’s on this list? A great ensemble cast with good laughs and great many love stories, this film tugs at your heartstrings during the most wonderful time of the year. What makes this film perfect is the showcasing of multiple couples, proving that love is not one-size-fits-all. This is definitely one of the **best romantic Chrristmas movies**!

## 2. The Holiday

One of my personal all-time favorites, this romantic movie brings to life the possibilities of love that the holiday season promises. Recently heartbroken, two beautiful women (Cameron Diaz & Kate Winslet) find love when they essentially trade lives.

## 3. While You Were Sleeping

Maybe it was the spirit of Christmas causing Lucy (Sandra Bullock) to do a good deed, but a case of mistaken identity takes this movie to next level when the family of the man she saves believe her to be his fiance. Christmas cheer prevails as everything falls into place.

## 4. Serendipity

Christmas! Christmas! Christmas! It’s everything in this heartwarming movie that proves what is meant to happen will happen, especially when it comes to love. John Cusack and Kate Beckinsale meet over and over again in an unexpected series of happy and fortunate events.

## 5. Bridget Jones’ Diary

Who isn’t a fan of the lovable Bridget Jones? She’s quirky, she’s adorable, she’s relatable. She proves that you don’t have to be perfect to find true love, to find your very own Mr. Darcy. And no one can escape ugly sweaters during the holidays.
