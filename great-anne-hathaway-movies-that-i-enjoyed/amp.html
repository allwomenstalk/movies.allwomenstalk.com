7 Great Anne Hathaway Movies That I Enjoyed ...

The Princess Diaries • One Day • Les Miserables • Ella Enchanted • The Devil Wears Prada • More ...

By Michelle

I always enjoy watching Anne Hathaway movies. She’s a very talented actor in my opinion who stars in array of films for every fan out there. What’s more, she’s kind in person and her interviews portray that. One afternoon I was flipping between channels to watch two different films at once. It took me a minute to realize that they both starred Hathaway, hence my desire to watch the two. These are some great Anne Hathaway movies that I like and you will too!

1. The Princess Diaries

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com

When it comes to Anne Hathaway movies, you can’t leave out the classics like the “Princess Diaries” series. I personally loved this story as a kid. Teenage Mia Thermopolis finds out that she is more than the geeky girl at school, she’s a princess. Hathaway does a brilliant job in her first movie ever. The film is funny, quirky and simply lovely. If you haven’t seen it already, you should check it out.

2. One Day

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com

The film “One Day” has a very interesting plotline in my opinion. It tells the story of two people on the course of the same day for multiple years. Somtimes they are together and sometimes they aren’t. It take a little to get used to the format but I quite enjoyed this film.

3. Les Miserables

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com

Hathaway plays Fantine in the 2012 film adaptation of “Les Miserables.” When asked how she prepared physically for the film, Hathaway said she tried to lose weight “as if she was dying.” I admire Hathaway’s desire not to promote extreme weight loss. The story is the classic musical about poverty in France and the revolutionary efforts taken to establish justice. The entire film is a musical with no breaks for dialogue, so be prepared.

4. Ella Enchanted

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com

The storyline of “Ella Enchanted” follows young Ella who has been cursed to do everything she is commanded to do. She ends up falling in love with the soon-to-be king. When the current king, a wicked uncle, learns of this, he plans to have Ella kill the prince to ensure his reign. The trick, she has to do whatever she is commanded. To find out what happens, you’ll have to watch this great, family-friendly film.

5. The Devil Wears Prada

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com

Step into the fashion world in the film “The Devil Wears Prada.” Hathaway stars as a naive young woman, Andy, who has moved to New York City to work as an assistant to the city’s biggest magazine editor Miranda Priestly, played by Meryl Streep. Meet the devil! She’s cynical and manipulative and ruthless. But at least she has good taste.

6. Becoming Jane

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com

“Becoming Jane” tells the biographical story of the young Jane Austen. Known for her writing, the film focuses on the woman behind the pen; a woman who’s loved and been loved. Her relationship with a certain young Irishman unfolds in this movie.

7. The Dark Knight Rises

On iTunes: itunes.apple.com

Who doesn’t love a good Batman movie? The most recent installment, released in 2012, portrays Hathaway as Selina. Adventure. Action and anticipation define this film. You won’t want to miss it!

Anne Hathaway is a very talented actress who has started in a varied collection of films. She’s down-to-earth, authentic and gorgeous. What is your favorite Anne Hathaway film?
