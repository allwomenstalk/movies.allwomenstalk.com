# 10 Most Charming Underdogs on TV ...

Everyone loves an awkward, bumbling unlikely hero, so here’s my list of the loveliest underdogs on TV! Whether they’re a main character or a supporting role, these wonderful personas are constantly trying to be the best they can, much to the disappointment of their co-workers, schoolmates, family members or friends. There are some great underdogs on TV, and you’ll be rooting for them all the way!

## 1. Shannon Beiste, Glee

![Shannon Beiste, Glee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pn/p2/vuh8cg89_600x800.jpg)

Coach Beiste from Glee is the one everyone is afraid of. She’s tough and masculine and doesn’t give a damn about anyone’s emotions - or so they think. As her storyline develops, you see that she is actually a kind, soft, gorgeous person who simply wants acceptance. As you witness her quest for love, you cheer her on and hope that she finds the very happiness that she deserves - she is definitely one of the sweetest underdogs on TV!

## 2. Jerry Gergich, Parks and Recreation

![Jerry Gergich, Parks and Recreation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cz/zd/se5m00tf_449x600.jpg)

I am a huge Parks and Recreation fan, and poor Jerry is definitely one of my favourite characters! He’s the person everyone loves to hate. He just keeps on trying to make friends with the people he works with, despite getting pummelled with abuse and sarcastic comments every minute of the day. Jerry is loveable and kind-hearted, and you’ll definitely be rooting for him the whole time.

## 3. Milhouse Van Houten, the Simpsons

![Milhouse Van Houten, the Simpsons](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/lu/w0uw4orr_600x789.jpg)

Milhouse is such an iconic character, probably because he is constantly trying to awkwardly flirt with Lisa Simpson and keep up with her cheeky brother and his best friend, Bart. Somehow, now matter how awkward and cringe-worthy he gets, you just can’t help but hope he gets the girl and makes something of himself - he’s just so sweet! One thing’s for sure, I’ll never get tired of watching him bumble around mucking things up, yet still being so lovely!

## 4. Walter White Jr., Breaking Bad

![Walter White Jr., Breaking Bad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uo/s4/kh0unazo_600x800.jpg)

Poor Walter White Jr. really gets the rough end of the stick in Breaking Bad. While his parents’ relationship diminishes and his father’s health, safety and sanity goes further and further downhill, he is brushed aside and treated as much younger than he is. Even though he’s still young, he understands what is happening and simply wants to be included. He tries a series of stunts to get attention from the people he loves, but it still doesn’t seem to work. We still love you, Walt Jr.!

## 5. Shirley Bennett, Community

![Shirley Bennett, Community](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/ik/q27r0avf_400x600.jpg)

Shirley is perhaps the most terrifyingly lovely underdog on this list! I say that because she is kind-hearted, loving and sweet, yet she still has the capacity to snap quickly and be icy-cold when she needs to be. That being said, her first instinct is to be the motherly figure to everyone she comes across, even though that is something that is often taken advantage of. Shirley is always looking out for others, even when they’re not looking out for her.

## 6. Vince Masuka, Dexter

![Vince Masuka, Dexter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rp/g1/bg7iy7jd_600x900.jpg)

There probably isn’t a character in any TV show more awkward than Vince Masuka from Dexter! He constantly tries and fails to be the ‘cool one’, often by trying to chat up ladies and make jokes at horrendously inappropriate moments. His hilarious laugh is iconic and sums up his character so well - adorably creepy and totally funny! While he often laughs off snappy comments made to him, all he wants is to be appreciated by the people he works with.

## 7. Glenn Rhee, the Walking Dead

![Glenn Rhee, the Walking Dead](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oo/p8/d31dscbz_600x902.jpg)

Glenn might get the girl and save more lives than most other characters, but he’s still quite under-appreciated by the other people he’s with. He constantly tries to prove himself, and even though he possesses some amazing skills, he’s somehow still always fighting for the same recognition as most of the other men in the group. Not only is he a strong member of the team, he also has a really kind heart which is always important.

## 8. Samwell Tarly, Game of Thrones

![Samwell Tarly, Game of Thrones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/by/sl/z7p5af75_600x901.jpg)

He’s always picked on for his size and intelligence, but all Samwell Tarly wants to do is to prove himself to the other men in The Nights’ Watch. Despite his efforts, he ends up abandoned by them, but not before showing more bravery than all of them put together. Samwell is one of my favourite characters in all of Game of Thrones, because of his charming personality and inspiring persistence. He never gives up on anything just because other people tell him he’s not worthy.

## 9. Amy Farrah Fowler, Big Bang Theory

![Amy Farrah Fowler, Big Bang Theory](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sf/ol/jkr5sdi6_455x637.jpg)

Amy is the nerdy, socially awkward girlfriend of Sheldon Cooper who was introduced to him after Sheldon’s friends found her on a dating website. Amy has avoided any relationships up until Sheldon, but still expresses a genuine want and need for friendship - the very thing she has missed out on her whole life. She finds friendship within Penny and slowly but surely builds up her confidence, making her beautiful personality shine through!

## 10. Adam/Ryan Newman, Wilfred

![Adam/Ryan Newman, Wilfred](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bm/e0/wh0liwb0_435x653.jpg)

The name will be different depending on which series of Wilfred you watch, the Australian one or the American one, but what is still very much the same is the glorious underdog (no pun intended, I promise) nature of the main character. He constantly tries to keep himself out of the spotlight and create a life for himself with his girlfriend, but his “dog”, Wilfred, is constantly getting in the way. Watching Adam/Ryan’s struggles are oh-so-endearing and you’ll find yourself drawn to his awkward character.

Perhaps watching underdogs struggle makes you feel better about your own life, or perhaps you are able to really relate to what they’re going through. Either way, there’s just something about them that is so much fun to watch! Who is your all-time favourite TV underdog?
