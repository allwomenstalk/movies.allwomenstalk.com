# 7 Amazing Musicals I Wouldnt Mind Watching over and over Again ...

There are so many amazing musicals that are repeat-worthy. This list showcases my favorite seven. If I HAD to choose seven amazing musicals to watch for the rest of my life, it’d be these. Check it out and let me know if any of these are also your favorites! I love bonding over musicals.

## 1. Grease

I mean, how could this not be on the list? This movie is timeless! I could watch it every single day without getting tired of it. John Travolta and Olivia Newton John are everything. So, this is my number one pick for amazing musicals that I wouldn’t mind watching time and time again. Anyone who says they don’t like the movie Grease is lying! Or they’re an alien... because it just doesn’t make sense to have anything but love for this musical.

## 2. Mamma Mia!

I saw this on Broadway and was very excited to watch the film adaptation. It was awesome! Amanda Seyfried and Meryl Streep are two of my favorite people in the movie industry so watching them together was epic. The songs are all very catchy and the singing is incredible! My favorite, of course, has to be “Lay All Your Love On Me”! Amanda Seyfried and Dominic Cooper had such great on-screen chemistry.

## 3. Les Miserables

I love this musical so much. The 2012 adaptation is fantastic. Every song that was sung was a good one, but this one is my favorite. I wouldn’t mind having this song on repeat for the rest of my life. Eddie Redmayne and Amanda Seyfried sound amazing together. The fact that all the singing in the movie was done live still amazes me! I was so impressed.

## 4. Hairspray

This was such a fun movie to watch! This clip features Zac Efron, Nikki Blonsky, Elijah Kelly and Amanda Bynes. The cast was fantastic. This song was my favorite to listen to because the scene in the movie is so cute! I wouldn’t mind hearing this and every other Hairspray song over and over again.

## 5. Camp Rock

Yeah, I know. I might’ve outgrown Disney Channel but the soundtrack to this movie is still great! I wouldn’t mind watching the movie whenever it’s on because of the singing! Demi Lovato and the Jonas Brothers sound great. “This is Me” is just one of those songs that you’ll never forget the words to. The song just creeps into my mind at the most random moment and I start shouting out the lyrics!

## 6. Tangled

I’m a sucker for a good Disney animated musical. I mean, how cute was Tangled?! Mandy Moore and Zachary Levi are the voices of the movie and they’re so good! I'm happy I got my niece hooked on the movie! I’m such a kid at heart. I wouldn’t mind watching Tangled everyday. Would you?

## 7. Rock of Ages

This was one of my favorite musicals last year! I was so pleasantly surprised with Julianne Hough’s voice. She sounds great with Diego Boneta. And in case you didn’t notice, Diego is quite the hunk in this movie. I melt each time he starts singing!

Well, there you have it. These seven musicals are my absolute favorites! I'd watch them all everyday if I could. Is that weird? Probably... but can you blame me? Which movie is your all-time favorite?
