# 7 Movies That Will Lift Your Spirits and Motivate You ...

There are a lot of movies that will lift your spirits and motivate you. There are movies that can even make us want to change ourselves and change the entire world too. There are also wonderful films that can make you cry, that can help you vent your frustration and that can enliven you. The films that made this list have the potential to motivate you, to inspire you and to bring you to your happy place. So here are 7 movies that you should watch when you are feeling a little bit down:

## 1. The King’s Speech

On iTunes at: _itunes.apple.com_
This is definitely one of those movies that will boost your spirits and if you haven’t seen it yet, you should go watch it right now. It’s actually the true story of the former King of England, George VI, and it’s about his journey to overcome a debilitating stammer. It will make you realize that even the royals have flaws and it will inspire and motivate you to overcome all the obstacles that you may encounter in your path.

## 2. Up

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/up/id322447599)
This movie is funny, is full of life and this is by far one of the most touching and beautiful films that I have ever seen. It basically shows what the most important things in life are and how to chase our dreams. This is definitely one of those movies that you should watch when you are feeling a little bit down since it will cheer you up in an instant.

## 3. School of Rock

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/school-of-rock/id292794442)
This is a clever, hilarious movie that will make you feel more energized in no time. Just like Dewey says in the movie “You’re not hardcore, unless you live hardcore.”. It’s also packed with classic rock anthems that will improve your mood and keep you entertained.

## 4. Despicable Me

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/despicable-me/id387548805)
Even though this is one of the funniest movies ever, it’s also one of those kids’ films that appeal to adults too. If you are feeling a little bit down, grumpy or extra jaded, just watch it and it will improve your mood. Of course, you could watch Despicable Me 2 to cheer you up some more.

## 5. Legally Blonde

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/legally-blonde/id261128362)
If you haven’t seen this movie, don’t write it off as a chick flick, since it’s hilarious and quite inspiring. It will definitely lift your mood in an instant and it’s perfect for anyone who wants to have a laugh with their friends. You’ll feel like taking on a room full of haters after watching this wonderful movie and you’ll also learn the famous “bend and snap” move.

## 6. Remember the Titans

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/remember-the-titans/id188765164)
This is a very inspiring movie concerning a football team where the most important scenes don’t take place on a football field. It tells a wonderful story and you could even say that it’s a classic since watching all the characters prevail against prejudice. Their learning to work together as a team will motivate you and it will inspire you to be a better individual.

## 7. Cool Runnings

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/cool-runnings/id351314818)
This movie is pure motivation and inspiration and also, it’s a movie that you will want to watch again. It’s about a group of native-born Jamaicans who are trying to train for Olympic bobsledding, it’s actually based on a true story and the raw determination of those athletes is really motivating and energizing.

There are a lot of inspiring and motivating movies out there. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. Do you know any other movies that can boost your mood? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
