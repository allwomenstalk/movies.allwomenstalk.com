# 10 Best Christmas Movies to Watch on the Big Day ...

Are you looking for the **best movies to watch on Christmas Day**? Watching a movie during the holidays is a great idea to bring everyone together. In fact, there’s nothing more entertaining than watching a movie during this season under a blanket and a hot chocolate! This is why I have rounded up the **best movies to watch on Christmas Day**.

## 1. Bad Santa (2003)

In this comedy, a cheater and his friend Willie, who dressed as Santa, try to steal an apartment with his Little Helper. However, they run into problems and find themselves in trouble.

## 2. Four Christmases

This is a super funny movie about a couple who are obliged to visit all four of the divorced parents on Christmas. Unfortunately, this couple finds themselves wondering about their future and their crazy families.

## 3. Home Alone (1990)

Nothing completes Christmas more than this movie that you can watch all the time. An eight year old trouble maker has been left alone in his house on Christmas. Nonetheless, he proves to everyone that he’s a hero and can protect his home against conmen.

## 4. The Nightmare before Christmas

This is the story of Jack Skellington who lives in Halloween Town, but accidently goes to Christmas Town. Jack decides to kidnap Santa Clause in an attempt to hold Christmas Town hostage. This is an animated musical fantasy that you should watch.

## 5. Bridget’s Jones’s Baby

Bridget Jones, at the age of 40, finally decides to focus on her career life. Yet, things don’t work as she planned. Bridget falls in love with two men and then discovers she’s pregnant without knowing the identity of the baby’s father. However, at the end, she receives the best gift ever on Christmas that changes her life completely.

## 6. Arthur Christmas

Arthur is Santa’s little son who enjoys Christmas to the max. Arthur is on a mission to give a gift to a young girl. This is a humorous animated movie that you won’t regret watching.

## 7. About a Boy

There’s nothing that can excite little Marcus more than the value of family and friends on Christmas. Will, who suffered from his father’s death and a desperate mother, meets an irresponsible man, who frees him from agony.

## 8. The Family Stone (2005)

Everett Stone decides to bring his girlfriend, Meredith, to meet his parents on Christmas night. However, Meredith, feeling frustrated, begins to focus unconsciously on an anxious behavior and doubts Everett about their relationship.

## 9. Christmas with the Kranks

The Kranks decide to skip Christmas and go to the Caribbean instead. This was a perfect vacation for the couple until their daughter decided to come home and surprise her parents. The Kranks have been forced to celebrate Christmas at the last minute.

## 10. The Polar Express

The Polar Express is a 3D computer-animated Christmas movie. With its music and story, this movie succeeds in evoking the Christmas spirit. The movie teaches everyone that with faith, you are able to embrace yourself and the whole world.
