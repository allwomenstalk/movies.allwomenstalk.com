# 7 TV Shows with Catchy Theme Songs Thatll Stay in Your Head ...

There are SO many catchy theme songs. As a big TV person, I get hooked on so many shows and songs. I mean, can you blame me? There’s no way I could get all of the catchy theme songs on this list, but I did get seven that I think are worthy of inclusion Check it out and then tell me what you think. What do you sing along to?

## 1. The Big Bang Theory

This song tops my list of catchy theme songs. The show is great! It’s hilarious and actually very educational. The song is no different! Listen to it. I dare you to not have stuck in your head afterwards! Trust me, it's impossible. You'll be running around the house singing out, "and it all started with a big bang!"

## 2. The Office

This show is everything. The theme song has no vocals and it’s still manages to be extremely catchy. I love that about it! It’s such an iconic show and the theme song has become my favorite thing to hear. It's just one of those feel-good tunes. I’ll never get over the series finale (I still cry like a baby each time I see it). At least I’ll always have this instrumental to listen to!

## 3. Scrubs

When I was obsessed with Scrubs, (I still am) my family would get so annoyed with me. I’d walk around just screaming out “no I know, I’M NO SUPERMAN!” Yes, now I see how annoying that might’ve been but no, I’m not sorry. I love this theme song! I think it’s perfect for the show. I actually managed to my sister hooked on the show now so thankfully, I still get to hear the theme song every now and then, except now she's the one singing it out loud.

## 4. Parks & Recreation

Here’s another catchy song that has no vocals. I love how it still manages to sum up the whole show, though. Parks & Rec is one the most binge-worthy shows at the moment! It’s great that it has such a catchy theme song. I always find myself humming along!

## 5. Sons of Anarchy

This show HAD to make the list. It’s my current obsession and I always sing along to the theme song. It’s a fantastic show. The theme song is super catchy and P.S the entire show’s soundtrack is great. I highly recommend this show!

## 6. Dexter

There’s no way Dexter couldn’t make this list. There’s no vocals in the theme song but that doesn’t make it any less catchy. I always hum along when I watch the show! The instruments in it sound so cool together, I’m obsessed! Listen to it and tell me what you think. Is it worthy of being on the list?

## 7. Full House

Here’s an oldie, but goodie. There’s no way to watch Full House without jamming out the theme song. It’s such a feel-good song! I know it’s a good song because it’s been forever since I’ve actually watched Full House and I can still recite the words to this song perfectly. I’ll never forget it!

There you have it. Seven TV shows with awesome theme songs. Don’t get me wrong, the list goes on and on! However, these are the shows I’ve actually watched. Which do you think is the best? What should make the next list?
