# 7 TV Cliques I Wish I Couldve Been a Part of ...

There are so many awesome TV cliques that I wish I could've been a part of. I mean, am I the only one who gets so invested in a show where you actually feel as though those characters are your friends?! I didn't think so. I've probably spent too much time imagining I was a part of these TV cliques. Check them out and then let me know which one you'd love to be a part of!

## 1. New Girl

![New Girl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p2/jx/z04y2x30_490x338.jpg)

No one wishes there was a real-life Nick Miller more than I do. New Girl has such a fantastic cast and the chemistry that those friends have is incomparable. How awesome would it be to be invited to one of Schmidt's ridiculous parties? You also have to love how this clique always keeps it real (i.e. the 'douchebag' jar). The New Girl gang is just one of the TV cliques I wish I could've been a part of!

## 2. Friends

![Friends](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/04/sg/q6pz38rk_600x390.jpg)

Do I even have to explain why the 'Friends' clique made it on the list?! I would pay money to hang out with Rachel, Monica, Chandler, Joey, Ross, and Phoebe for a day! Being a part of this clique means strolling into each other's apartment anytime of the day and spying on "Ugly Naked Guy" together. Yup, that sounds like a good time!

## 3. Saved by the Bell

![Saved by the Bell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m6/rd/vtt6issq_600x402.jpg)

Zack, Slater, Kelly, Lisa, Screech, and Jessie. Or as I like to call them, the dream team. These guys always managed to have a blast and find new adventures - all without sex and alcohol. The show is so different from anything we watch now, it'd be awesome to go back to these "squeaky clean" days and see adventures you could get into with these six!

## 4. How I Met Your Mother

![How I Met Your Mother](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ga/ff/ax462eu7_508x338.jpg)

Being a part of this clique means nonstop laughter. They always offer the best advice and Barney always has an elaborate bet/scheme going on to keep things interesting. Also, you would've know who the 'mother' is a long, long time ago.

## 5. That 70's Show

![That 70's Show](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/qk/nmjdulvz_496x338.jpg)

This group of friends is just too cool. I mean, being friends with Kelso?! What more could you ask for?!

## 6. Freaks and Geeks

![Freaks and Geeks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xv/ji/f89h18ks_600x487.jpg)

Freaks & Geeks is seriously one of my all-time favorite shows. if you haven't watched it, please do yourself a favor and do so on Netflix! I can't really choose who I liked more - the freaks or the geeks. I mean, the freaks were made up of a young James Franco, Seth Rogen, Jason Segel, Busy Philipps and Linda Cardellini. They were the outcasts of the school but at the same, they were the ones you wanted to hang out with. Then, there's the freaks who are just SO cute. I'd be their friend any day!

## 7. Entourage

![Entourage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/xl/xy2mka3v_600x445.jpg)

I have no words to explain my excitement for the upcoming Entourage movie! It has a 2015 Summer release date and it cannot come fast enough! I remember watching Entourage and wishing I could be best friends with all of them, Ari Gold included. I'm so glad that this movie will help everyone with their Entourage withdrawals!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many TV cliques I wish I could've been a part of. Let's be real, we've all watched TV shows and connected with a clique that we known we would've been the perfect fit for! Which TV clique do you wish you could've been a part of?
