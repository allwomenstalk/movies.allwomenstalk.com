# 7 Movies Thatll Change Your Negative View on Love ...

Some of us have no desire to be in a relationship, because we don't want to get our hearts broken. Of course, if you find the right person, you won't have to worry about that. Once you fall in love with him, you'll realize that all of the negative things you used to say about relationships were completely wrong. If you're a cynic when it comes to romance, here are a few movies that will change your negative view on love:

## 1. Trainwreck

Every one of us can relate to Amy Schumer, which is why you need to see her film. In it, her character grows up with a father figure who teaches her that monogamy is unrealistic. That's why she ends up sleeping with random men, smoking pot, and getting drunk constantly. Of course, once she meets Bill Hader's character, she realizes that her father might've been wrong about relationships.

## 2. The Proposal

It's healthy to have career goals that you put before everything else. Of course, it's possible to sustain a love life along with your career. In fact, getting a boyfriend to help you de-stress after a tough workday could be the best decision of your life. Don't believe it? Then you have to see this movie starring Sandra Bullock and Ryan Reynolds.

## 3. The Wedding Singer

Back in the day, every Adam Sandler movie killed. This one is no different. It's about a wedding singer who starts to hate the idea of marriage after getting left at the alter. Of course, the right woman is able to turn all of that negativity around. Who is she? Drew Barrymore, of course.

## 4. The House Bunny

If you think you need to look gorgeous to land a boyfriend, think again. This movie will show you that your intelligence and overall personality are way more important than your looks. The right guy will love you for you, after all. So you don't need to pretend to be someone you're not in order to impress him.

## 5. 17 Again

Do you think that all marriages eventually fall apart? Well, that's what happens at the start of this movie. However, with enough effort, any husband and wife can keep their relationship strong. This movie will prove that some couples are meant to last.

## 6. 50 First Dates

This is another movie starring Adam Sandler and Drew Barrymore. They make an adorable couple, so you're going to love watching this film. It's all about a woman who has an injury that prevents her from forming new memories. Even though it's hard to live a life like that, it doesn't stop her from finding love.

## 7. How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

This movie is about a woman who is trying to make a guy break up with her. However, the guy she chooses to date has a plan of his own. He's trying to make a woman fall in love with him. Even though they have no real feelings for each other from the start, you know that it's going to change.

No, real life isn't exactly the same as life in the movies. However, it's close enough. That's why the messages in these films should inspire you to open yourself up to love. Which one of these movies is your favorite?
