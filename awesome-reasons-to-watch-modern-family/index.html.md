# 7 Awesome Reasons to Watch Modern Family ...

There truly are so many fantastic reasons to watch Modern Family. I haven't been into the show for as long as most people, but I'm so glad I started watcing it. This family is hilarious. Even though outrageous things always happen in the show, there's always something that you can relate to about your own family. The cast in the show is fantastic! The storyline gets better and better with each episode. Check out some of my reasons to watch Modern Family. You won't regret, I promise!

## 1. The Mockumentary Style

After The Office practically perfected the mockumentary genre, I didn't know what to expect from Modern Family. However, the mockumentary style has become one of my top reasons to watch Modern Family. The clever editing of the show is what makes it so incredible. The "interviews" with the family and the character-revealing backstories work so well. It adds a lot to the show, and it definitely wouldn't be the same without it.

## 2. The Kids

Usually, sitcom kids are very dry and predictable; not the ones from Modern Family. They're all so unique and hilarious in their own ways. There's the Dunphy clan that's made up of three children - Haley, Alex, and Luke. They get into trouble all the time and it's hilarious to watch them get out of it. Then there's Manny who's a sensitive romantic. He's got a little brother who I'm sure will grow up to be just like him. Then there's Lily, Cameron & Mitch's sassy daughter.

## 3. Ty Burrell

Ty Burrell plays Phil Dunphy, one of the greatest TV dads. He's definitely the stereotypical goofy and embarrassing dad, but you can't help but love him. He's oblivious to reality at times, making him one of the most comical characters on the show. His open and friendly demeanor show how endearing his character is. He's a good-natured man who loves his wife and kids. Trust me, if you watch the show, you've probably wished Phil was your dad.

## 4. Well-written Tv

The writers of this show are geniuses. Everything is sharp, witty, and hilarious. There's pretty much never a dull moment on the show, which is hard to do. The writers even take their own real life family experiences to create story lines for the show. I think they're doing a fantastic job.

## 5. Sofia Vergara

If you're still not convinced why you should watch the show, watch it for Sofia Vergara. She's absolutely fantastic in it! Her character, Gloria, is a very proud and loving mother. The latina is hilarious (and sometimes insane) and a total pleasure to watch on the show. She's always yelling at her husband, Jay and with her accent, it is so funny to watch!

## 6. Family

Like the show's title suggests, this show focuses on family. They're a huge family, and they all love and support each other. They sometimes have their ups and downs with each other, but it always works out in the end. The show really embodies what today's "modern" family really is about. That's what I think is so great about the show!

## 7. Critical Acclaim

It's not just me who thinks you should watch this show! The first season brought in many awards like Outstanding Comedy Series, Outstanding Writing for a Comedy Series and Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series, among others. That was just the first season! Since then, it's been nominated for many different awards. If it keeps up what it's been doing, they'll be around for a long time.

So, there you have it! These are just seven out of many reasons why you should watch Modern Family. It's become one of my favorite shows. What do you love most about it?
