# 7 of the Best Movie Food Fights Ever ...

I’ve always thought it would be great fun to get into a real actual food fight. Who wouldn’t want to fling food around, slapping other people with it? You’re laughing, aren’t you? There’s just something hilarious about tossing food around, especially in movie food fights. I’m sure that if I was ever in a big time food fight, I would have to head straight to the shower because I don’t like food on my clothes or in my hair. But it would be totally worth it. Since food fights aren’t socially acceptable, check out these ultimate movie food fights instead.

## 1. Blazing Saddles

This comedy is one of my dad’s favorites. The food fight scene certainly adds to the gut busting laughs this movie produces. In the scene, the characters start throwing pies around. As you can imagine, hilarity ensues as people are getting slapped in the face with pies covered in whipped cream. This epic movie food fight only ends when everyone is coated in pie fillings.

## 2. Little Darlings

This classic 1980s movie features a group of kids living it up at summer camp. I never went to sleep away camp as a kid, but I can only imagine the antics that my friends and I would have gotten into if I had. In Little Darlings, the kids at camp get to witness a couple of the girls tossing food into each other’s faces. You are going to be laughing so hard!

## 3. Animal House

With a name like Animal House, a food fight is less than surprising. This National Lampoon movie is funny from start to finish, but when the food starts flying, it gets even funnier. As the fraternity brothers clash with the other students on campus, food flies more than once. Each will make you laugh every time you watch.

## 4. PCU

Here’s another college inspired movie. It centers around protesting, something that many college students get in on when they move away from home. During one particularly memorable protest against the eating of red meat, food starts flying and even coming down from up above. What happens next is so funny that you might have a hard time catching your breath.

## 5. Hook

Here’s a movie with a food fight you can share with your kids. Starring Robin Williams, the movie is a take on the classic tale of Peter Pan. The kids are trying to convince a newcomer that they can eat anything they want by using their imagination. As you can probably predict when a table full of little boys is having a meal, food starts flying. It’s the imaginary kind at first, but pretty soon all the boys can see it. Their joy will make you love this movie even more.

## 6. Whip It

You know that a movie about roller derby girls is going to include at least one fight. The epic food fight that takes place in this movie is no exception. There aren’t as many pies as in other movies, but I will tell you that ketchup covered French fries will be flying all over the place. Toss in a good old fashioned girl fight and this is a scene you won’t soon forget.

## 7. The Great Race

This 1960s masterpiece is full of laughs, but the food fight scene is epic. So much food goes flying all over the place that not an inch of wall space, people or equipment comes out of the fight unscathed. If you want to see hundreds of pies go flying, watch this movie.
