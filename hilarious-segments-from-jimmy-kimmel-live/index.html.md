# 7 Hilarious Segments from Jimmy Kimmel Live ...

If you're a fan of his late-night talk show, then you already know that there are so many hilarious Jimmy Kimmel segments. They're pretty much my favorite part about the show. I love that these segments are done weekly, or at least regularly. Check out some of my favorite Jimmy Kimmel segments and then let me know which ones you love!

## 1. Unnecessary Censorship

I know I can't be the only one who cracks up while watching this segment. I think the title is pretty self-explanatory and it's great! This is a weekly segment, done on Thursday nights. If you miss them live, you can always catch up with them on Jimmy's YouTube channel. How convenient is that? This is just one of the hilarious Jimmy Kimmel segments that I can't get enough of.

## 2. Lie Witness News

It's always so funny to see just how much people want to fit in with the "cool" crowd. In "Lie Witness News," Jimmy has a reporter ask people questions about FALSE events/people, while these people respond with their "honest" opinions. In a recent segment, Jimmy had a SXSW edition where the reporter asked questions about "new, up and coming bands" (that totally didn't exist, by the way), and it's hilarious to see these people's responses!

## 3. Celebrities Read Mean Tweets

This is hands down my favorite segment! It's funny to see celebrities read mean tweets about themselves because most of the time, they'll just have a laugh at their own expense. I love when celebrities don't take things too personally. In a lot of these segments, the celebrities just laugh off the insult. But you've got to admit, some of these are really, really clever.

## 4. Pedestrian Question

"Pedestrian Question" is such a funny segment. Jimmy sends a reporter outside where she asks people a super random question. Jimmy then plays the footage for his audience but pauses it before they give a response. It's the audience's job to decide what their answers were going to be! It's really fun to watch.

## 5. Escandalo

This was a brief segment but it was really funny while it lasted. The cast of Scandal all took part of the Spanish "soap opera," Escandalo. It's really funny to see them poke fun at their hit show. Also, it's hilarious to see the whole cast talk in Spanish! If you haven't seen it yet, what are you waiting for?!

## 6. Confusing Question of the Day

This segment is hilarious. Like Jimmy said, a confusing question will never keep someone from trying to have an answer for it! If you were in this situation, what would you have said?

## 7. I Witness News

"I Witness News" is kind of like "Lie Witness News," but there's just one little difference. In this segment, a celebrity will go out to the public (in an awesome disguise) and ask them about him/herself. In this segment, rapper Drake goes undercover and asks strangers about what they think of Drake's music. He did get a lot of hate but he just brushed it right off and played along! I love how at the end of the video, he took off his disguise and talked to one of his fans.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many hilarious segments from Jimmy Kimmel Live. If you watch this show religiously like I do, what's your favorite segment? Is there one that you just NEED to watch all the time?
