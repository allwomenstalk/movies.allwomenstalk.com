# The Rom\-Com That Best Suits Your Zodiac Sign ...

Whether you want to admit it or not, we all love a good romantic comedy from time to time! They might not be the most realistic or the most relatable, but they sure are fun to watch and can provide the perfect entertainment for a girls' night in or a lazy Sunday under the duvet. There are so many to choose from that it can sometimes be hard to pick! Here is **the romcom that best suits your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You are someone who is passionate about your career and like to see your efforts reflected in everything that you do, so something like The Wedding Planner would be a perfect pick for an Aries!

## 2. Taurus

Deep down, you know you are the smartest, funniest person in the room at all times, much like Emma Stone in the hilarious Easy A! It’s the kind of romcom that actually has some intelligence and mature wit to it too.

## 3. Gemini

You have many different distinct aspects to your personality, which means that something crazy and funny like She’s The Man is great for you. You might not show two such different sides as in the movie, but you can definitely relate!

## 4. Cancer

Nostalgia is something that you value very highly. You like to feel good by looking to the past and feeling like it’s giving you a nice warm hug. Nothing gives you a nice warm romantic comedy hug quite like Bridget Jones’ Diary!

## 5. Leo

You like something that is high tempo, fun, and optimistic and something that really fits the bill when thinking about those requirements is the every popular When In Rome. Who better than Kristen Bell to put a smile on your face?

## 6. Virgo

To All The Boys I’ve Loved Before is a great pick for a Virgo, because it is only just released on Netflix and you are someone who loves to keep up with all the latest trends and fads.

## 7. Libra

You are very swayed by strong and big personalities, so a massive movie star like Will Smith is always going to hit the sweet spot for you. The best choice is definitely Hitch!

## 8. Scorpio

You like a romcom with a little twist, so something like How To Lose A Guy In 10 Days that has layers upon layers and switches it up on the audience will really appeal to your preferences.

## 9. Sagittarius

You have a very short patience limit and a no holds barred attitude, so you will be able to relate hugely to Reese Witherspoon’s character in Sweet Home Alabama!

## 10. Capricorn

You are an undeniable cynic, so when it comes to romcoms, you have to have something with a bit of a biting edge. Look no further than the modern classic 10 Things I Hate About You!

## 11. Aquarius

Sibling rivalry is something that interests an Aquarius greatly, even ones that are only children! With this in mind, 27 Dresses is the perfect romcom recommendation.

## 12. Pisces

You favour something that is sweet but totally escapist, so let’s take it all the way back to the golden days of the 80s and enjoy one of the very best, The Princess Bride!
