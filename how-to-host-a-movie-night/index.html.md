# How to Host an Epic Movie Night ...

Want a girl's night but short on cash? This YouTube video is cute and creative and shows you **how to host a movie night**.

Instead of going to bars or clubs, just take a trip to your local Dollar Tree for food and drinks, why not borrow a movie from the library! Frugal, right?

Watch this video all about **how to host a movie night**.

MissMikaylaG
Published on May 28, 2016

## 1. Invitations

Make creative handmade invitations to pass around. Possibly, create something similar to a movie ticket.

## 2. Make a Snack Table

Get your chocolates, candies, chips, popcorn, soda and bottled water at Dollar Tree. Get authentic cinema popcorn box containers at Dollar Tree also!

## 3. Grab and Go Bags

Have grab and go bags for the food. The bags can be found at Dollar Tree.

## 4. Movie Decision

Finally, select a movie. Mikayla suggests: Singing In The Rain, Grease, Mean Girls and Last Song. Personally, I have only seen Grease. I give Grease a total score of 10 out of 10.

So, the next time you feel the need to socialize but cannot afford expensive outings, simply have a movie night for girl's night.
