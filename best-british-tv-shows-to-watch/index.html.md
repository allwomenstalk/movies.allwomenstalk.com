# 7 Best British TV Shows to Watch of All Time

British TV shows are some of the most entertaining series ever created. If you’ve ever laughed your heart out watching Del Boy and Rodney, Baldrick and Edmund, or René and Yvette, you already know the magic of classic British television. But these iconic series are just the beginning! Whether you are looking for timeless comedies, gripping soaps, or legendary sci-fi, this list of the best British TV shows to watch will remind you of unforgettable gems that are always worth rewatching.

Here is a quick look at why classic British television continues to captivate audiences worldwide:

- **Unmatched Humor:** Sharp wit, eccentric characters, and unforgettable catchphrases.

- **Rich Storytelling:** From historical satire to groundbreaking sci-fi narratives.

- **Cultural Legacy:** Shows that shaped modern television standards for generations.

## 1. Only Fools and Horses

![Only Fools and Horses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kd/ed/xjsxkd2h54c92e93b02eb689490488_250x354.jpg)

**Price:** $110.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
“This time next year, we’ll be millionaires!” Remember that legendary quote? I don’t know if this show made it to every official list of the most popular British TV shows, but it absolutely belongs at the top. I’ve watched it countless times, and whenever local channels rerun it, I end up watching it all over again. What could be funnier than two working-class flea market traders in Peckham? Two flea market traders who constantly manage to land themselves in absurd trouble! Whether you stream it online or grab the DVD set, do whatever you can to experience this hilarious comedy series that remains one of the finest [classic TV shows](https://movies.allwomenstalk.com/) ever produced.

## 2. Doctor Who

![Doctor Who](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jg/mf/l9ig7qom54c92e94b9ff6712301304_250x323.jpg)

**Price:** $37.59 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I didn’t connect with this show right away because the early props and scenery looked budget-friendly, but once I gave it time, I realized how genuinely fantastic it is. For anyone new to this universe, the series follows an eccentric Time Lord traveling across space and time in a special machine called the TARDIS, which famously resembles a blue British police phone box. You can check out official details on the [BBC Doctor Who homepage](https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b006q2x0). This iconic program earned a place in the Guinness World Records not only as the longest-running sci-fi series in television history, but also as one of the most successful English TV series to watch.

## 3. EastEnders

![EastEnders](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/pc/n9dge6q654c92e95b5043917880397_250x369.jpg)

**Price:** $24.94 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This long-running soap opera focuses on the dramatic daily lives of residents in the fictional London borough of Walford. Having earned multiple BAFTA awards and numerous honors for most popular drama series, it stands as a cornerstone of British television. If you enjoy rich character drama and want a classic, enduring soap opera to dive into, this series offers endless entertainment. Learn more about the cast and episodes directly on the official [BBC EastEnders page](https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b006m86d).

## 4. Men Behaving Badly

![Men Behaving Badly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d6/aw/gp9uwnyr54c92e96ad039707269503_250x267.jpg)

**Price:** $73.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Gary and his zany flatmate Tony are two of the most absurd, messy guys you will ever meet on screen, and their partners Dorothy and Deborah have to constantly endure their antics. While it might not be the most refined series on British television, its irreverent slapstick and witty banter make it undeniably funny for anyone exploring nineties British comedy series.

## 5. Blackadder

![Blackadder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mf/xi/q42ul2w354c92e97abea0590839019_250x333.jpg)

**Price:** $42.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Speaking of brilliance in comedy, Edmund Blackadder is an absolute masterclass in television satire. Widely ranked among the greatest British TV shows of all time, it showcases Rowan Atkinson transforming from his famous silent comedy persona into the cunning, scheming Blackadder. Much like the hilarious schemes seen in [Only Fools and Horses](#1), Edmund's get-rich-quick plots constantly backfire, leaving him tangled in hilarious dilemmas across different historical eras.

## 6. ‘Allo ‘Allo!

![‘Allo ‘Allo!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ax/u9/j4bckorr54c92e98c9da5321920476_250x309.jpg)

**Price:** $175.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Watching this sitcom as a kid was a memorable experience, but rewatching the full series as an adult reveals just how clever the writing truly is. Set in occupied France during World War II, the show centers on café owner René Artois, an unlikely hero juggling the Resistance, German officers, and unexpected romantic entanglements. It takes historical drama and turns it into pure farce, earning its spot among excellent British TV series you should watch at least once.

## 7. Pride and Prejudice

![Pride and Prejudice](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lo/y1/qjy8gdpu54c92e9a001ab119676994_250x353.jpg)

**Price:** $17.97 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
We saved one of the grandest classics for last! If you adore Jane Austen's masterpiece, this heartwarming 1995 BBC adaptation is essential viewing. Jennifer Ehle and Colin Firth deliver stunning performances as Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy, setting the gold standard for period dramas.
