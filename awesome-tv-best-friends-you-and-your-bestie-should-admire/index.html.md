# 9 Awesome TV Best Friends You and Your Bestie Should Admire ...

We use TV as inspiration for everything, and that includes admiring some of the greatest TV best friends you and your bestie should look up to. From bromances to mother-daughter besties, TV shows have covered everything. These TV best friends are a testament that BFFs come in all different shapes and sizes. And no matter what, your best friend will always be there for you.

## 1. Blair Waldorf & Serena Van Der Woodsen

![Blair Waldorf & Serena Van Der Woodsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023151_6894_600x901.jpg)

Blair and Serena might go in the books as being some of the greatest TV best friends out there. When the show started they weren’t exactly the greatest duo, but their friendship endured through thick and thin. Blair and Serena were always there for one another. Through heartbreaks, scandals, and everything in between. They knew the importance of being there for your bestie.

## 2. J.D. & Turk

![J.D. & Turk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023221_3421_600x802.jpg)

J.D. and Turk might have the best bromance ever. They work together, hang out together, and have decided that they are basically married. J.D. and Turk would literally do anything for one another. They are proof that your best friend is just as important as your significant other.

## 3. Sherlock Holmes & John Watson

![Sherlock Holmes & John Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023243_1321_600x900.jpg)

Anyone who has watched Sherlock on BBC can tell that Sherlock and John are the most dysfunctional best friends ever. And that is fine. You and your best friend don’t have to be this picture perfect duo. I wouldn’t go as far as holding a gun up to your bestie’s head, but you can still be dysfunctional. As long as your and your best friend will always support each other, who cares if you are polar opposites?

## 4. Cory Matthews & Shawn Hunter

![Cory Matthews & Shawn Hunter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023305_1394_600x897.jpg)

I grew up loving Cory and Shawn and they are definitely two of the greatest TV best friends out there. They shaped what I thought about friendship and taught me that friends stay together through everything. They stand up for one another and help each other through any issue they face. Not to mention, they should make you laugh when you need it most.

## 5. Troy Barnes & Abed Nadir

![Troy Barnes & Abed Nadir](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/03023731_0656_600x900.jpg)

My best friend and I have never gone as far as to make a magazine about how good of friends we are, but Troy and Abed have. They love to show off their friendship to others. Which is a great thing. If you have a best friend, why not flaunt it to the world?

## 6. Arthur & Merlin

![Arthur & Merlin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023419_8567_600x900.jpg)

The worst thing in the world is trying to hide a secret from your best friend, and Arthur and Merlin prove that you shouldn’t have to. (SPOILERS) After trying to keep a huge secret from Arthur, Merlin finally tells him the truth. By being honest with his best friend, he strengthens their friendship. You can’t fully be yourself with your best friend if you have secrets between you two.

## 7. The Sex & the City Girls

![The Sex & the City Girls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023512_8105_405x550.jpg)

Who said you are only allowed one best friend? Carrie, Samantha, Charlotte and Miranda are proof that your closet gal pals are truly your soul mates. Boys may come and go, but your girls will stick with you no matter what. You and your besties should learn that you should be able to talk about anything, and I mean anything, with each other.

## 8. Golden Girls

![Golden Girls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023535_3510_600x901.jpg)

If there is ever any proof that friendships last a lifetime, it is the Golden Girls. Your best friends will be with you for a while, so make sure that you have a relationship strong enough to last a lifetime. Life experiences only bring you and your girls closer together, until the point that you are a senior citizen and fabulous as ever.

## 9. Rory & Lorelai Gilmore

![Rory & Lorelai Gilmore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20023557_3574_600x910.jpg)

Why can’t your best friend be your mother? Rory and Lorelai had one of the greatest TV best friend relationships ever. They could confide in each other, fully be themselves, and talk super fast with each other. That is why I love Gilmore Girls so much. It shows that best friends don’t have to be the same age. They can be years apart and they can be related. Best friends come in all different shapes and sizes.

Your best friend is the most important person in your life. They will stand with you through all of the good times and bad times. These TV best friends are perfect to admire because they show you how many different types of best friends there are. What did you think about these TV best friends? What are some other great TV best friends? What do you think makes these TV best friends so great?
