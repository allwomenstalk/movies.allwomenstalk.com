# 7 Memorable Gossip Girl Moments I Will Never Get over ...

If there's one thing we have an endless supply of, it's memorable Gossip Girl moments. I remember binge-watching the series on Netflix just a few months ago and wow. That's really all you can say when watching GG! I was so invested in all of the characters (Chuck & Blair especially), but can you blame me? These characters were nothing at all like me, but I still felt connected to all of them. Check out the memorable Gossip Girl moments that I'll never get over!

## 1. Blair Loses Her Virginity

Ah. The place where it all started. Sure, Blair didn't lose it in a "romantic" way but the love story that followed was epic. Let's not forget that crazy Burlesque scene where Blair danced for Chuck. I really think this was a defining moment for their relationship. I don't know about you, but I'll forever ship "Chair". This is one of the most memorable Gossip Girl moments.

## 2. Jenny Loses Her Virginity

I'll never get over this moment. Jenny Humphrey and Chuck Bass?! No. I just can't wrap my head around it. Even though I know Chuck was extremely sorry for what he did, I hated him for that episode. When Blair found out, it was heartbreaking. The way she "banned" Jenny from New York though? That's the power of Queen B, ya'll.

## 3. Blair's Princess Wedding Ruined

Blair's first wedding was like a dream come true (for her). She was going to become royal! Who doesn't want that? Chuck. But he wasn't the one who ruined Blair's wedding. It was Gossip Girl! Gossip Girl sent out a video of Blair confessing her love for Chuck. There went her happy vows.

## 4. The Revealing of Gossip Girl

SPOILER ALERT! If you don't know who Gossip Girl is yet, don't read this. The series finale revealed that our very own Dan Humphrey was Gossip Girl. Once they revealed everything, it kind of made sense. But still, I don't know if I was happy with the reveal of GG. Were you?

## 5. Blair Loses Her Baby

Oh my God. Remember that tragic car accident Chuck & Blair were in?! My heart was in pieces that episode. I was so happy when Blair was pregnant and even though they never said who was the father, I knew Chuck would be a great dad. Unfortunately, she lost her baby! This clip makes me so sad.

## 6. Chuck Wears a Onesie

Trendsetter? Chuck wore the onesie before it was all the rage! When I saw this, I didn't really know how to feel but because I love Chuck so much, I approve of this. Chuck can do no wrong!

## 7. Series Finale

To sum it all up, the series finale is one I'll never get over. It was just so perfect! Jenny Humphrey was back, Dan was marrying Serena, and Blair and Chuck were happily married with the cutest little son. It was just a great way to end the show. It really had such a fantastic run. These characters are everything!

There you have it! Gossip Girl will forever be on my list of favorite TV shows. It's amazing from start to finish. So, my advice to you, if you have a Netflix account, start binge watching NOW. You will not regret it. What's one moment from GG that you'll never get over?
