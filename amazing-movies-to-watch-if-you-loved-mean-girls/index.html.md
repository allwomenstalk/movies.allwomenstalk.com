# 13 Amazing Movies to Watch if You Loved Mean Girls ...

Mean Girls is a movie that everyone, regardless of age or gender, absolutely adores. Of course, there are other films that are just as entertaining. So if you don't feel like rewatching Mean Girls for the tenth time, here are some other movies you should check out:

## 1. It’s a Boy Girl Thing

Ever wonder what it would be like to be a boy for a day? Well, in this film, a girl and a guy switch bodies and have to pretend to be the other person until they can figure out how to set things right again. If you somehow haven't seen Freaky Friday, then you should check that out too, because it has a similar plot.

## 2. John Tucker Must Die

If you liked Mean Girls, because of all the catty behavior, then you'll love John Tucker Must Die. It's about a group of girls who aim to destroy a cheater's life. Wouldn't you do the same?

## 3. Sixteen Candles

This is an old movie that you'll love as much as your parents do. It's about a teenager who is struggling to survive in high school. She's only sixteen, but all she wants is a real relationship.

## 4. What’s Your Number

Chris Evans, Chris Pratt, and Martin Freeman are all in this movie, so why wouldn't you watch it? If you don't get distracted by the eye candy, then you'll enjoy the plot. It's all about a woman who wants to find the love of her life. It's every girl's dream, after all.

## 5. She’s the Man

This film is from back when Amanda Bynes was in her prime. She goes off to college and dresses up as her twin brother in order to help him out of a jam. What could possibly go wrong?

## 6. The House Bunny

If you loved Mean Girls, because Lindsay was transformed from a nerd to a hottie, you'll love The House Bunny. It's about a retired Playboy bunny that befriends a group of nerdy college girls and turns their lives around.

## 7. Miss Congeniality

You've probably seen this before, but there's no harm in watching it again. It's about a tough lady who is forced to go undercover as a beauty queen. She hates dressing up and acting elegant, but it's something she has to do to keep her job.

## 8. Easy a

In this film, Emma Stone plays a teenager who pretends to sleep around. It's a modern twist on The Scarlet Letter, so if you enjoyed the book, you'll enjoy the message in this movie.

## 9. A Cinderella Story

Do you miss Hilary Duff? Then you should watch this movie where she falls in love with her Prince Charming. Of course, nothing comes easy, so there's a lot of bumps along the way.

## 10. Sydney White

Here's a unique take on the story of Snow White. It shows Amanda Bynes in college, struggling to fit in. However, she ends up getting kicked out of the sorority she tries to join, and spends her time with seven misfits instead.

## 11. Bride Wars

If you love weddings, but are sick of love stories, then you should watch this movie that focuses on two female friends. They both get engaged around the same time, so they fight to create the superior wedding. Will their friendship survive, or is their pride more important?

## 12. When in Rome

Have you ever thrown a coin into a fountain to wish for love? In this movie, the opposite occurs. A woman steals some coins out of a fountain, which causes the men who threw them to fall in love with her.

## 13. 13 Going on 30

Sick of being a kid? In this movie, a 13 year old wishes that she could be a grown up and magically wakes up as a 30 year old. It doesn't take her long to realize that being an adult isn't all it's cracked up to be.

There's no reason to watch the same movie over and over when there are hundreds of other amazing films out there. What other movies do you love that remind you of Mean Girls?
