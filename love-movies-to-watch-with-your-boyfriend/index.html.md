# 29 Love Movies to Watch with Your Boyfriend ...

Finding a great love movie to watch with your boyfriend while you are both curled and cuddled up on the couch can be hard! Us girls like to watch true blue chick-flicks, while guys like action. So, what love movies are good to watch with your guy? Are you worried about choosing the wrong 'movie night' movie? Well, take the guessing out and take a look at my top 29 romantic movies to watch with your boyfriend!

## 1. I Love You, Man

I love, love, love this love movie! It's not only totally funny, but it's a movie that guys will love too! Paul Rudd is one of my favorite actors and when you add in Jason Segel, you've got a movie that is super funny with awesome humor packed inside of it! It's light, it's airy and he'll love it!

## 2. The Break-up

Another love movie that any guy would love to watch with you is The Break-Up! Not only do you get to see Jennifer Aniston naked (for the guys!), but you'll see just how much you and your boyfriend can relate to their relationship. When I first saw this movie, I laughed so hard I almost cried! It's emotional, it's funny and it's super fun to watch!

## 3. Couples Retreat

This Vince Vaughn classic is absolutely one of my favorites. It's a love movie all about four different couples. It dives deep into their relationships and it takes them all on a 'couples retreat' so that one of the couples can 'fix' their relationship. It's lighthearted, super funny and you'll love it!

## 4. Date Night

Tina Fey is absolutely one of my favorite actresses. Not only does she really lighten up the screen, but she's super funny too! I actually totally relate to this love movie because not only does it feature a couple who have been married for a while and are comfortable with one another, but it features them trying to break out of that comfort zone and really try to find themselves again. Watch it, girls, and you'll love it!

## 5. Love & Other Drugs

What love movie to watch with your boyfriend has Anne Hathaway naked through most of it? A movie that has Jake Gyllenhaal naked through it too! This quirky, funny love movie is so good that both you and your boyfriend will absolutely love it!

## 6. Just Married

I think that the late Brittany Murphy was one of the best actresses out there. Not only was she super quirky, but when paired with Ashton Kutcher? The combination can't be beat! This movie is a love story, but with a twist. It's all about honeymooners and what they go through when they are newlyweds. If you want a movie to watch with your boyfriend that is super funny and romantic, this is it!

## 7. What Happens in Vegas

Oh Ashton, you did it again! This is another quirky, hilarious love movie that is not only great to watch with the guys, but you can watch it with the girls too! Cameron Diaz is absolutely beautiful and this movie is very 'Something About Mary' like. She is sassy, funny, flirty and totally different!

## 8. Bridesmaids

Who doesn't love a love movie with a wedding in it? I do! But this movie is not focused in on the bride, but rather on the maid of honor and everything that she has to go through. It's hilarious ladies. If you haven't seen this movie, you are missing out completely! I've watched this movie at least ten times and it gets better each time!

## 9. Friends with Benefits

Who doesn't love Mila Kunis? I loved her in 'That 70s Show' and I love her in this! Personally, I think that the spark between Justin Timberlake and Mila is completely undeniable! They are friendly, they are cute and this movie is the ultimate, funny movie that guys and girls will totally love!

## 10. The Vow

Okay girls, so this one is more for us than for a guy, but it is still a great love movie to watch with your boyfriend … if he likes something sappy and something totally chick-flickish. This movie is all about making someone fall in love with you twice. It's sad, it's sweet, it's heartfelt and it's one of my favorite movies!

## 11. Forgetting Sarah Marshall

If you are looking for a funny movie that is sure to turn him on to chick-flicks, you've got to check this one out! It's got all sorts of crude humor and it's lighthearted! I love this movie. Not only did it make me laugh so hard, but I had to watch it over and over again just to see every little in-joke!

## 12. P.S I Love You

This is one of the classic love movies to watch with your boyfriend and although it's a little older, it's definitely a favorite of mine. What movie could be better with the mixture of actors and actresses that they have tucked away in this movie? I love the concept, I love the storyline and I love the plot. It's a great find!

## 13. Nick and Norah's Infinite Playlist

I am all about Michael Cera! I loved him in Juno and he is awesome in this movie too. Not only is this movie super funny, but it's also a great way to really see inside the true 'high school' relationships and what goes on. Also, the soundtrack? It's amazing! You should definitely grab this DVD, curl up with your guy and watch it!

## 14. The Proposal

Now, this is a movie that I stuck on there because I absolutely love Sandra Bullock. I think that she is so talented and beautiful, plus she has great chemistry with hot Ryan Reynolds. Plus the film has Betty White in there! What movie could be bad with Betty White? It's a little corny and totally predictable, but it's still one of my favorite movies.

## 15. Along Came Polly

You're probably wondering why I am adding another Jennifer Aniston movie, right? Well girls, she's truly a great actress (as long as she plays in mostly the same role) and I think that this was ideal for her. It's quirky, it's funny and it's not all that predictable! I loved it and I think that your boyfriend will too!

## 16. Notting Hill

I am a sucker for anything with Julia Roberts in it. Step-Mom? That's one of my favorite movies! This love movie is all about a really well-known actress falling for a bookstore owner. It's so cute, super romantic and while your boyfriend might not be a huge fan, it is still boyfriend-friendly!

## 17. Easy a

This love movie is the ultimate one to watch with your boyfriend. Emma Stone is not only beautiful in it, but she's wicked funny! It's got a great story line (around the same lines of The Scarlett Letter) and it's hilarious. I promise, you probably won't stop laughing throughout the whole movie.

## 18. Bride Wars

Another great love movie to watch with your boyfriend has got to be two girls fighting over their dream wedding. Even if you aren't at the proposal stage yet, seeing these girls transform and watching their stories are both great ways to really appreciate your friends … and to realize that you shouldn't get married in the same month!

## 19. Definitely, Maybe

This is another oldie but a goodie! I love this movie because it's such a classic and it's got a rich and great storyline. Be aware, it's a true love story and while funny at parts, it's definitely a chick-flick, so make sure you leave that part out when you try to sell your boyfriend on watching this.

## 20. Confessions of a Shopaholic

I love this love movie because it's not only super real (oh yes, I've spent way too much money on my credit cards before), but it's a great love story that comes out of debt. It's all about a girl who is obsessed with labels and starts to work at a money-saving magazine. She falls for her boss, gets caught in a lie and then he falls for her. It's cute and super funny!

## 21. American Beauty

If you are really looking for a movie that is romantic in a morbid way to watch with your boyfriend, this is the movie. It's got beauty in it, it's got a strange relationship in it, a marriage, a creepy guy video-taping a plastic bag and it's truly a must-see romantic movie.

## 22. (500) Days of Summer

This is absolutely a beautiful movie and it's a great movie that will pull a guy in too. It's all about true love, relationships and Zoey looks absolutely amazing in it! If you want a romantic movie that is going to keep your boyfriend interested, this is the one!

## 23. The Holiday

The Holiday is absolutely a favorite movie of mine and it's one of the few chick flicks that guys can relate to, too! It's a movie that has some beauties in it, has some fabulous guys and the plot is awesome. It's all about two girls who are looking to switch houses for a while, just to get away.

## 24. How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

What love movies list to watch with your boyfriend would be complete without this particular movie? Not only is this movie super funny, but you wouldn't think that it is a love story at all!

## 25. Never Been Kissed

Oh, Drew Barrymore -- you are amazing! This movie is all about her going back to high school and trying to fit in to become the popular girl -- and for her to seduce her teacher! This is a great, feel-good movie that has ups and downs!

## 26. 10 Things I Hate about You

This is an oldie but such a goodie! It features a young Julia Stiles and a beautifully young Heath Ledger. This is a story where he is paid to date her -- and yet she hates him, until she falls for him. SUCH a good movie!

## 27. 50 First Dates

How would you like to have to make your girlfriend fall in love with you every single day, over and over again? That's exactly what this movie is all about! Adam Sandler is amazing and Drew Barrymore is so precious in this movie!

## 28. Juno

While you might not think that a pregnant teenager movie is going to be a love story, it is! I love the jokes in this movie too and Ellen Page? She is absolutely fantastic! This is a must-watch movie with your boyfriend!

## 29. My Best Friends Wedding

Finally, another old movie but a fantastic one! This one is all about Julia Roberts being in love with her BFF, but her BFF is getting married! How interesting right? Watch it and tell me how much you love it!

So girls, there is my top 29 love movies to watch with your boyfriend when the weather isn't cooperating and you are looking for a movie to enjoy together. What other love movies are out there that you'd watch with the boy?
