# Do You Want to Be in a Movie?

Allwomenstalk is a proud supporter of the Last Empress of Fashion - a movie coming in 2019 with a story about a remarkable woman, Mila Anufrieva.

You can read our interview with the director Gala Pawl here: [Movies](https://bilbr.com/the-last-empress-of-fashion/)

But, we have some more exciting news for you dear readers.

EXCLUSIVE TO ALLWOMENSTALK

Any Allwomenstalk reader who supports the movie gets a GUARANTEED appearance in the movie.

The movie is going to contain a section featuring people from all over the world. If you have an opinion on fashion, what fashion means to you, or why you think the world needs fashion contact, and you want to appear in the movie please contact neecey.b@[allwomenstalk](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/) and I will pass your details on.

This is a fabulous opportunity to be involved in an awesome project and in return, you will receive an IMDB credit in the listing alongside Mila and fashion celebrities.

If you wish to donate to the making of the movie, or support the project in some way, again, contact me at neecey.b@allwomenstalk.com.

Just think how exciting it will be to see your name on iMDB and the kudos of showing your friends and family your spot in the movie.

Can't wait to hear from you.
