# 7 TV Shows to Binge Watch While Theyre on Hiatus ...

If you’re a TV fanatic, summer hiatuses are the worst, but fortunately there are a ton of TV shows to binge watch while they’re on hiatus. You may just be looking for a TV show to pass the time, but you may be surprised to find that one of these TV shows to binge watch will become your new favorite!

## 1. Parks and Recreation

Now that they’ve announced that next season is Parks and Recreation’s last season, there is no better time than the present to catch up with everyone’s favorite comedy. Personally, I always say that Parks and Recreation is one of the most perfect TV shows to binge watch because there aren’t too little or too many episodes to catch up on. Catch up with Leslie, Ann, and the rest of the gang. It might’ve taken you a few years, but you won’t regret starting this comedy.

## 2. Girls

Catch up with Girls as Lena Dunham and co. film season four. Yes, the show receives a lot of backlash, but in my opinion, most of that backlash in unwarranted. I always say that it’s a show worth going into with an open mind. Whether you’re in your twenties or your fifties, you will find yourself relating to the girls in so many ways.

## 3. Inside Amy Schumer

Inside Amy Schumer is a quiet comedy gem. I binge watched this show on a whim in less than twenty-four hours, and it was one of my favorite shows I’ve ever binge watched. It’s perfect if you need a little pick-me-up, or simply want a little laugh. Amy Schumer is so hilarious that you may even find yourself addicted to her show afterwards.

## 4. Playing House

If you’re not watching Playing House, you’re missing out on one of the funniest shows on TV right now. It’s only one season in, but it only just went on hiatus. It’s about two childhood best friends, and if I explain any further I’ll ruin the show. Spend your next lazy day with Playing House, believe me, you won’t regret it.

## 5. The Mindy Project

I’m not sure what planet you live on if you haven’t at least tried to watch The Mindy Project yet. This show will suck you in in a matter of episodes. If you haven’t watched it yet, it’s a mix of an outlandish sitcom and a romantic comedy, and I guarantee that binge watching it will get you addicted to this show!

## 6. Workaholics

Workaholics is another show that is perfect to binge watch. It’s not too short, but it’s not too long either. It’s perfect if you want a mindless comedy to watch after a long week at work. While it may be slightly mindless, the guys who work on that show are comedic geniuses, both on and off screen, and are worth checking out.

## 7. Sex and the City

Being too young to watch Sex and the City when it originally aired on TV, I started binge watching it last summer. Following that, I binge watched the Carrie Diaries and both movies. While it may not be a show that we’re waiting to come back from a hiatus, aren’t we all sort of waiting for the third movie announcement, even if we won’t admit it?

What’s your favorite show that’s on hiatus right now? I’m always looking for new shows to binge watch as well, so let me know in the comments! Right now, I really love Playing House, so if you haven’t checked it out yet, you must!
