# 7 Reasons Why Youd Want Marshall Eriksen as a Best Friend ...

Over the years, we've all come to love Marshall Eriksen. I mean, how could you not, right? He's totally got major BFF potential and I mean, look at him, he's such a cutie! In How I Met Your Mother (HIMYM), Marshall is always there. Sure, it's after he cracks a few jokes at someone else's expense, but that's what's funny about him! I love love love Lilly and Marshall, I think they're perfect together! Check out the reasons why you'd want to have Marshall Eriksen as a best friend.

## 1. He's a Foodie Just like You

![He's a Foodie Just like You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ck/hn/nh/b9qdndmm_500x333.jpg)

We all need that friend that we can pig out with. Marshall Eriksen is that friend. He understands how delicious food is and will be your accomplice when you need to eat your stress away. Isn't that really all we could ask for in a person? You'll never be eating alone again!

## 2. He'll Go on a Road Trip with You

![He'll Go on a Road Trip with You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/rc/ol/b692pa5k_480x360.jpg)

Road trips can go two ways. It can be the worst experience ever with the hours just dragging on and on OR, with the right person, it could be the most fun you'll ever have. Marshall knows how to have a great time on a road trip! He'll jam out to silly songs with you and make time go by quickly. Who knows, you might even want to take a detour just to be able to spend more time with him! I know I'd love going on a trip with him!

## 3. He's Got Killer Dance Moves

![He's Got Killer Dance Moves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/q9/rl/iz444nm1_500x335.jpg)

Wanna go out and impress the whole place with some dance moves? Take Marshall as your partner. With a drink in hand, he's got all the moves. He'll be the one making YOU look good! It's either great or embarrassing, you decide.

## 4. Coordinating Outfits, Anyone?

![Coordinating Outfits, Anyone?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/nm/vs/b1cvu8zx_500x281.jpg)

What's the cutest way to show off your friendship? Coordinating outfits! Marshall is all about making other people happy, even if it means wearing a moose sweater. What would your matching BFF sweater be? It'd have to be something creative yet totally embarrassing at the same time!

## 5. Selfie Nation

![Selfie Nation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/yg/d3/0ekmcexs_480x360.jpg)

Are you a part of the selfie nation? Well so is Marshall! How fun would it be to have a BFF that loves documenting fun moments just as much as you do? He'd be the perfect selfie partner, and let's be honest, you can never go wrong with a good selfie. Plus, look at that adorable smile! I'm sure we'd all love to have that saved on our camera roll.

## 6. He's Great at Pep Talks

![He's Great at Pep Talks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fh/z6/nx/ao14y8qb_500x343.jpg)

It seems like Marshall always knows what to say. He might get knocked down a couple of times, but he always gets right back up. He'll help you do the same! Okay, fine, his pep talks don't seem all that helpful but he does try. That's all we can ask from him. Keep up the good job, Marshall!

## 7. He'll Never Let You Give up

![He'll Never Let You Give up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/pt/vs/d647twpb_530x361.jpg)

Marshall truly is the whole package. If he sees that you're not giving it your all, he'll tell you. He'll push you to be your best, even if that means throwing something at you and screaming out, "NOT GOOD ENOUGH!" Hey, it's coming from the heart, right?

There you have it! Marshall Eriksen is the best friend someone could have. What's your favorite thing about him?
