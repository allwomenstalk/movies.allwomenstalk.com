Get Pumped for These Upcoming Movie Releases You Can’t Miss

Suicide Squad • American Ultra • The Secret Life of Pets • London Has Fallen • Batman Vs. Superman: Dawn of Justice • More ...

By Vladlena

Calling all movie lovers! The upcoming cinema schedule is packed with blockbusters you won’t want to miss. In the following months and carrying over to the next year, you’ll be seeing quite a few must-see movie premiers hitting the theaters. So get ready to start saving, planning and ultimately binging on some of the most anticipated films! To give you a taste of what to expect, here are a few movie releases to take note of.

1. Suicide Squad

Jared Leto, Will Smith, (Official Site) Cara Delevingne, Jai Courtney and more are starring as imprisoned supervillains in the upcoming superhero film based on the DC comics. A secret government agency recruits them as a squad to execute a dangerous mission that can potentially result in their deaths. Knowing this, Expect high-octane sequences and intense character dynamics that will keep you on the edge of your seat throughout the entire runtime. It's an adrenaline-fueled experience that DC fans have been waiting for!

Release date: August 6, 2016

2. American Ultra

Craving a little comedy with a dash of action in your life? This summer you’ll get just that and more. Mike Howell is a hard-core stoner but unaware that he is in fact a government agent. Once activated, he becomes a liability and is then targeted for extermination, but to everyone’s surprise he proves that they might have trained him a little too well to get rid of him that easily.

Release date: August 21, 2015

Quick Watchlist for the Season:

  • High Octane Action: Suicide Squad & London Has Fallen
  • Family Friendly Fun: The Secret Life of Pets
  • Comedy Gold: American Ultra & Daddy’s Home

3. The Secret Life of Pets

The previous animation may be targeted toward the younger demographic, but who says you can’t enjoy it? The plot of this movie revolves around Max, a favorite household dog, who chooses to join forces with a sloppy mutt, Duke who is brought home by his owner. Together they put their differences aside just so that they can defeat a white rabbit named Snowball and his army, who are set on taking down all the happy pets and their owners.

Release date: 2016

4. London Has Fallen

London Has Fallen, (Official IMDb) a sequel to Olympus Has Fallen, was written by Creighton Rothenberger, Chad St. John and Katrin Benedikt. This American action thriller is set to tell the story of the President of USA, a Secret Service lead agent and an MI6 agent as they fight to protect the leaders of the Western world from a terrorist attack during the funeral of a British Prime Minister. We expect nothing less than lots of action and lots of suspense!

Release date: January 22, 2016

5. Batman Vs. Superman: Dawn of Justice

The battle that we have all been waiting for is almost upon us! In a world with a forceful vigilante and sacrifice-it-all savior, there can only be one. Unfortunately, as the two begin to battle one another, mankind struggles to find the hero it really needs and another threat arises to put everyone in danger. So who do think will win in a battle of Batman vs. Superman?

Release date: March 25, 2016

6. Daddy’s Home

Given Will Ferrell’s legendary track record for creating comedic masterpieces, there is no doubt that his latest project will be another major hit for audiences everywhere. In his upcoming project, Will Ferrell plays an uptight radio executive who battles a much cooler biological father for the affection of his stepchildren. A much needed family comedy will be arriving just in time for the holidays!

Release date: December 25, 2015

7. The Finest Hours

The Finest Hours tells the story of a coast guard who risks his life and makes a daring attempt to rescue a group of stranded sailors, who are rapidly sinking on an oil tanker off the coast of Cape Cod during a blizzard in 1952. Yet as he goes out into his suicide mission, he leaves a significant other behind. With romance, suspense and thrill, this seems like a promising film.

Release date: January 29, 2016
