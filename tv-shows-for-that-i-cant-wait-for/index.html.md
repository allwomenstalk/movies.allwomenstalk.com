# 7 TV Shows for 2013 That I Cant Wait for ...

There are so many TV shows for 2013 that I am absolutely thrilled about! Whether the TV shows for 2013 are in mid-season now or they are brand new, I can't wait to tune my DVR to record! By far, 2013 is going to be the hugest year for all sorts of brand new TV shows that are going to be incredible. So, whether you are a TV junkie like me or you are just getting into some of these shows, let's take a look at some of the best TV shows for 2013 that are coming up!

## 1. Zero Hour

This is one of the hottest TV shows for 2013 that so many people are looking forward to! It's starts on Valentine's Day (so you'll be watching right?) and it's all about a treasure map! It's almost like National Treasure in a TV show – how great is that?

## 2. The Carrie Diaries

If you liked Sex and the City, you're going to LOVE this TV show for 2013. It's a prequel to Sex and the City and it takes place when little Carrie Bradshaw was in high school. It's one of those series that is going to be fantastic and really fill out the back story of how Carrie got to be who she is!

## 3. The following

Okay, I admit, I am not the hugest Kevin Bacon fan, but this show? It looks amazing! It takes place about 8 years after a serial killer is arrested but someone seems to be pulling a copy-cat job and following right in the killers footsteps. Kevin Bacon plays the FBI agent that actually comes out of retirement to investigate this particular killer. Thrilling!

## 4. The Americans

I have heard so much about this awesome series and truthfully, I think that it is going to be amazing! It's all about spies and Russia and it almost matches along with Homeland. If you like Homeland, you'll love The Americans, girls! Watch it and tell me what you think!

## 5. The Goodwin Games

So I actually love any TV shows about siblings. I have a brother of my own and it's really cool to see siblings band together. Brothers and Sisters? That was one of my favorite shows! This one actually looks pretty good and it's just basically about siblings banding together in order to carry out their father's instructions – because he has passed away. It's mid-season right now and I can't wait for 2013 when it comes back!

## 6. How to Live with Your Parents

When you lived with your parents, you probably remember how hard it was, right? If not, don't worry, because this show will bring you right back there! A single mom and her daughter actually move back in with her folks and this show captures everything. It's funny, quirky and totally awesome!

## 7. Cult

I am actually one of those people that are pretty obsessed with learning about cults. I think they are so interesting and – different. This awesome show is all about a journalist that actually follows a crazy cult following and documents it. It's going to be good girls, try watching it!

So, this is my TV list for 2013, what's yours? Do you have any favorite shows that are coming back for 2013? Sons of Anarchy and The Walking Dead are some of my favorites – along with Scandal!
