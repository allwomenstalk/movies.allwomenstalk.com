# 7 Amazing Anime Shows Teenage Girls Will Love ...

I can’t imagine surviving my teenage years without all the amazing anime shows I’ve watched. There is a whole world of anime shows out there that many teenage girls have yet to discover. If you’re on the hunt for great-quality anime shows, check out these!

## 1. Fruits Basket

Fruits Basket was my first anime and, of all the anime shows I’ve watched since then, it remains my favorite! Fruits Basket has just the right balance of fantasy, romance, comedy and action. To this day I can’t find a flaw with Fruits Basket, other than the fact that the series was so darn short! You can find Fruits Basket online at Hulu, so get started watching now!

## 2. School Rumble

School Rumble is yet another anime series I’d highly recommend! This series ran for two seasons, and is about a crazy love triangle! All of the characters are lovable, and go on adventures you’ll love watching! You can find School Rumble on Hulu, so what are you waiting for?

## 3. Ouran High School Host Club

Teenage girls go crazy over the characters on Ouran High School Host Club! With suave and hilarious male characters, and a heroine you can’t help but love, Ouran High School Host Club is an incredible anime series! Unfortunately, it only ran for one season but that means you can binge watch this fantastic romcom right now on Netflix!

## 4. B Gata H Kei- Yamada’s First Time

Yamada’s First Time is such a great show simply because the main character is so very awkward! It’s hard not to fall in love with this character after one episode, trust me! Yamada is a girl trying to become a woman, step by step starting with her first kiss. Her journey is wild and, well, awkward! Start watching this series on Netflix today!

## 5. Okami-san and Her Seven Companions

Okami-san and Her Seven Companions is another hilarious anime series, available on Netflix. This show will have you laughing so hard that you cry! The main character, Okami-san, is a bit standoffish, but she’s as great a friend as they come! The cast dynamic is the best of the best, and you’ll love the characters so much you won’t want to stop watching!

## 6. Vampire Knight

Anime series aren’t all romcoms; no, there’s a darker side, too. One dark, mysterious anime series teenage girls love is Vampire Knight! I just started watching the show recently, but I’m already hooked! There’s endless intrigue, and a constant sense of danger! If you’re more inclined to supernatural dramas, check out Vampire Knight on Hulu!

## 7. Spice and Wolf

Again, the characters on Spice and Wolf make the series phenomenal! Spice and Wolf is a show about a merchant named Kraft Lawrence and Holo, who looks like a 15 year old girl but is actually a 250 year old wolf deity! It’s a fantasy, yes, but more than anything it’s about the relationship between Kraft Lawrence and Holo. Their adventures and friendship make the show really worth watching! So why not look it up on Netflix today?

I’m a complete anime freak, and I’m not afraid to admit it! If you’re a teenage girl who’s a fan of anime, or who is maybe a bit hesitant to explore the world of anime, I strongly recommend watching these shows! What is your favorite teenage-friendly anime show?
