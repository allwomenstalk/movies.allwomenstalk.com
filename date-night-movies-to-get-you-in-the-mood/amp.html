15 Date Night Movies to Get You in the Mood ...

By Sici

I don’t know about you, but I don’t think there is any date night more classic than simple dinner and a movie. You get to enjoy good food, food entertainment and good company, what could be better than that? Of course, any good date should end with a good time in the bedroom, so ideally you should pick a film to watch together that will start to stir up those sorts of feelings and set the mood for some sexy fun later on! Here are fifteen great date night movies to get you in the mood.

1 The Little Death

This is a really quirky Australian film that follows four couples as they explore different stages of their relationship. Plenty of sexy scenes included!

***

The film masterfully blends humor and sexuality with a raw and honest look at the complexities of intimacy. As the couples delve deeper into their secret fantasies and desires, audiences are treated to a cathartic experience that is both thought-provoking and highly entertaining. Directed by Josh Lawson, this film shies away from mainstream clichés and offers a fresh perspective on what it means to connect with one's partner. It's a perfect pick for a date night that'll have you laughing and possibly blushing, setting the stage for a memorable evening.

2 Cruel Intentions

This trashy teen classic is not only tons of fun, but is really does have a sexy undercurrent running through it from start to finish, not to mention that iconic kiss between Selma Blair and Sarah Michelle Gellar!

3 Y Tu Mama Tambien

Sex, drugs, and all different kinds of music are on the menu in this saucy Spanish language movie about two young men who take an older woman on a road trip.

4 Basic Instinct

Sharon Stone is unashamedly sexy in this early 90s classic, and that sexiness has the power to reach through the screen and effect the people watching!

***

Sharon Stone's portrayal of Catherine Tramell, a manipulative and seductive crime novelist, became an instant symbol of sensuality, keeping viewers on the edge of their seats. The titillating game of cat and mouse she plays with detective Nick Curran, portrayed by Michael Douglas, is charged with erotic tension that only amplifies the film's allure. The movie's infamous interrogation scene alone is famed for its daring boldness. Settle in for a night where the thrills are as potent as the romance, ensuring both pulses and hearts race in unison.

5 Blue is the Warmest Colour

A wonderful and dramatic French-language film that explores the lesbian relationship of two young women. You will never look at spaghetti the same way again!

***

Directed by Abdellatif Kechiche, Blue is the Warmest Colour is an elegant portrayal of passion and vulnerability that will undoubtedly stir deep emotions. Adèle Exarchopoulos and Léa Seydoux deliver intimate performances that capture the intense connection and evolving dynamics of their characters, Adele and Emma. The authenticity of their relationship is palpable, making it a striking backdrop for a romantic evening that revels in the raw exploration of love and self-discovery.

6 On the Road

Kristen Stewart does some of her sexiest work to date in this film version of the the classic literary work. Lots of sexual freedom and experimentation!

***

Stewart portrays the liberated Marylou, journeying with Dean and Sal as they traverse the country. With intensity in every scene, her portrayal captures the essence of Beat Generation spontaneity. It's not just the sensual energy she brings, but also an adventurous spirit that'll inspire viewers to throw caution to the wind. Perfect for couples seeking to ignite a fiery passion, the film delves into themes of love, self-discovery, and living life to its fullest with a backdrop of stunning American landscapes.

7 9 ½ Weeks

This sexy movie gives you food for thought, literally! One of its most classics scenes involves character exploring the many uses for honey and milk on a kitchen floor!

8 Secretary

This is one for people who want something a little more risqué! A sexy movie about a secretary who indulges in the world of S&M with her boss!

***

The film Secretary stars Maggie Gyllenhaal and James Spader in a daring dance of dominance and submission. As the somewhat quirky and socially challenged secretary, Gyllenhaal's character finds liberation and empowerment in her unconventional relationship with her demanding boss (Spader). The film tastefully explores themes of consent, desire, and personal transformation, making it a provocative choice for couples looking to explore the passionate side of their relationship with a touch of sophistication. Just be warned—it's spicy enough to set the screen on fire, so handle with care!

9 The Dreamers

A film that feels very much like a love letter to French cinema, it follows a love triangle in Paris that includes steamy romps in bubble baths. The only thing, there is a slight dash of incest about the triangle!

10 Dangerous Liaisons

Who doesn’t love a sexy period drama? This one starring the likes of Uma Thurman, Michelle Pfeiffer, and Glenn Close with certainly get you hot under the collar!

***

"Dangerous Liaisons" is set against the opulent backdrop of 18th-century French aristocracy. The film weaves a tantalizing web of intrigue and seduction that will have you and your date wrapped in its spell. With virtuoso performances that simmer with passion and deceit, every scene drips with lavishness, tempting the senses. As the game of love and betrayal unfolds, you'll find yourself ensnared in the complex emotional entanglements. Let the candlelight reflect off your screen—this film promises an evening where love is a game, and the stakes are as high as the drama is thick.

11 Shakespeare in Love

Another period drama with a a sexy vibe, Gwyneth Paltrow has never been better than in this, her Oscar-winning leading performance.

***

Gwyneth Paltrow stars alongside a dashing Joseph Fiennes, playing the fictionalized, charming version of a young William Shakespeare. The chemistry between the two is palpable, sparking across the screen in a blend of passionate romance and witty Elizabethan banter. Shakespeare in Love cleverly entwines history with a twist of fantastic fiction, making it not only humorous but also intriguingly seductive. It invites you into a lush portrayal of London's theater world where love, like the vibrant poetry within, knows no bounds. Perfect for a date night, it wraps you both in the allure of a bygone era sprinkled with the magic of love's first blush.

12 Love & Basketball

With some killer 90s R&B and hip-hop as its soundtrack, this iconic love story between two friends and basketball players is insanely sexy at times!

***

With some killer 90s R&B and hip-hop as its soundtrack, this iconic love story between two friends and basketball players is insanely sexy at times! The chemistry between Sanaa Lathan and Omar Epps is undeniable, making every glance and shared moment electric. Their journey from childhood friends to star-crossed lovers is as heartfelt as it is passionate, blending sports and romance in a way that few films accomplish. Whether you're a basketball fan or just in it for the romance, this movie’s emotional highs and lows will keep you hooked and truly set the mood for your date night.

13 Fifty Shades of Grey

Well, obviously! The bondage action might not be to everyone’s taste, but you can’t deny that the sexiness of Fifty Shades is more than enough to get your blood pumping.

14 Unfaithful

A love triangle involving Richard Gere in his prime? Yes, thank you, sign me up!

***

"Unfaithful" spins the sultry tale of a suburban wife, played with depth by Diane Lane, as she tumbles into a torrid affair with a charming French book dealer. Her escalating infidelity sets the stage for an intense, passionate drama, laden with suspense and moral quandaries. Amidst the deceit, Gere's nuanced performance as the betrayed husband magnifies the emotional stakes. This steamy flick promises to ignite the sparks on date night, wrapping you both in a world of temptation and the thrilling danger of forbidden desires.

15 The Thomas Crown Affair

A super seductive movie. Anything story that involves sex on a grand marble staircase is worth a watch, don’t you think!?
