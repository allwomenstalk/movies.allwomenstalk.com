# A Beginner Friendly Way to Start Watching Maze Runner Type Movies

“The Maze Runner” is one of the hit movies for teens and young adults that continues to captivate audiences. I took my teenage daughter to see it and we both loved it. Based on the popular novel by [James Dashner](https://www.jamesdashner.com), the [movie adaptation](https://www.imdb.com/title/t1790864/) is a must-watch. Whether you have already read the book or are simply searching for the best **maze runner type movies** to add to your watchlist, this film does not disappoint.

## 1. It’s Dystopian Fiction

Dystopian fiction is incredibly popular, setting characters in challenging social or political conditions where they must overcome immense obstacles. “The Maze Runner” fits perfectly into this genre, sharing the same high-stakes energy as other hit franchises like “The Hunger Games” and “Divergent.” If you enjoyed either of those, you are bound to love this film. If you want more recommendations, explore our guide on [thrilling dystopian movies to watch next](https://movies.allwomenstalk.com/thrilling-dystopian-movies-to-watch-next).

Other classic dystopian series to check out include:

- The Hunger Games
- Divergent
- The Giver

## 2. Thomas is Very Cute

Let’s be honest: having a charismatic lead character makes any movie more enjoyable. [Dylan O’Brien](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm3729721/) is fantastic as Thomas, bringing the character to life with his expressive dark eyes and athletic presence. He looks the part of a determined runner, carrying the iconic **maze runner backpack** as he navigates the dangerous paths of the Glade. Beyond his good looks, he is a remarkably talented actor who carries the emotional weight of the story effortlessly.

## 3. The Mystery

“The Maze Runner” keeps you guessing from start to finish. It is the kind of puzzle-box film where you are constantly trying to piece together what is actually going on. Even better, the story features genuine twists and turns that most viewers won't see coming. There is nothing quite like the satisfaction of thinking you have a movie figured out, only to be completely surprised by a clever plot development.

## 4. Teresa’s Spunk

Teresa is a standout character and a personal favorite. As the final arrival in the Glade, she is the only girl in a community of teenage boys, yet she immediately holds her own. While there are no cheesy **maze runner pick up lines** exchanged in this high-stress environment, her sharp wit and resilience bring a few welcome moments of humor to the story. You will quickly grow to love her bravery and refusal to back down.

## 5. It’s Edge of Your Seat Action

There are absolutely no dull moments in this film. It is packed with intense sequences that will keep you on the edge of your seat. You will find yourself actively rooting for Thomas and his companions as they sprint through shifting stone walls. If you love stories that pull you into the action and keep you invested in the characters' survival, you will love the heart-pounding pacing of this adventure, especially as they try to solve the [intricate mystery](#3) of their confinement.

## 6. It’s a Movie You’ll Want to Watch Again

This is definitely a film that warrants multiple viewings. Because the action is so fast-paced and the environment is so detailed, you are guaranteed to spot new clues and background details the second time around. You might even find yourself so captivated by the film's visual style that you start looking up creative **maze runner tattoo ideas** after the credits roll. Very few films manage to maintain their thrill and rewatchability so well.

## 7. There’s More to Come

Fortunately, the adventure does not end here. While the first film provides a satisfying climax, it also sets up a much larger world, leaving you eager to find out what happens next. If you cannot wait to see how the story unfolds, you can dive straight into the original novels. You can learn more about the book series on [James Dashner's official website](https://www.jamesdashner.com) or check out our list of [must-read dystopian books](https://books.allwomenstalk.com/dystopian-novels-to-add-to-your-reading-list) to see how it compares to other classics.
