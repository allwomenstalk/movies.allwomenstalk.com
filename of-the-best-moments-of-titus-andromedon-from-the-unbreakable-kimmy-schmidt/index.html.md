# 15 of the Best Moments of Titus Andromedon from the Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt ...

​If you’ve never seen “The Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt”, do me (and yourself) a favor: sit down and binge all three seasons on Netflix right this second. It is such a breath of fresh air. And you'll get to see the **best moments of Titus Andromedon.**

The humor is similar to that of “Parks and Recreation” and “The Office”, and the characters are some of the greatest, albeit strangest, people you’ll meet. Titus Andromedon is the gay roommate of the titular character, and he brings the most laughs. He’s an actor struggling to make it big in New York City, as well as searching for love, and having his heart stuck in the nineties. Here are some of the **best moments of Titus Andromedon.**

## 1. Titus Sings Peeno Noir (Season 1 Episode 6)

Caviar. Midsize Car. Pat Benatar. What do these things have in common? Why, they’re all lyrics to Titus’ breakout hit “Peeno Noir” of course! I dare you not to have it stuck in your head. This is one of the all time **best moments of Titus Andromedon.**

## 2. Titus Meets Linda (Season 3 Episode 12)

According to Titus, Linda is a name for middle aged Human Resources representatives, not babies! So, when Titus meets baby Linda, his world is spent spinning.

## 3. Titus Goes Viral (Season 1 Episode 12)

One of the first things we learn about Titus is his desire to be famous. His hard work finally pays off at the end of Season One, when he (accidentally) becomes a viral superstar.

## 4. Titus Becomes a Backup Singer (Season 3 Episode 4)

While looking for odd jobs, Titus finds a listing for a backup singer. He is happy to use his talents and earn some cash. Even if the songs are a little…different.

## 5. Titus Watches Kimmy’s Court Case on the Computer (Season 1 Episode 11)

When Titus finds out that Kimmy is a Mole Woman, he is very sympathetic to her case. Or, very excited to watch her case is a better way to put it. He goes to the library so that he can watch the court trial live and takes a journey.

## 6. Titus Puts on a Musical (Season 2 Episode 3)

In Season Two, we find out that Titus has lived some very interesting past lives. He decides to put on a musical about the life of Muraskai, a geisha, despite major backlash and protest, and starring himself, of course!

## 7. Titus Auditions for a Spiderman Musical (Season 1 Episode 4)

When “SpiderMan Too: 2 Many Spidermen,” a Broadway musical holds open auditions for the “2 many Spidermen”, of course Titus auditions. Hilarity obviously ensues.

## 8. Titus Channels His Inner Beyonce (Season 3 Episode 2)

Titus sees his boyfriend with another man, and reacts the way anyone would: by channeling Beyonce and Lemonading, of course!

## 9. Titus is Reunited with His Wife (Season 2 Episode 1)

At the end of Season One, we are briefly introduced to Titus’ wife, Vonda. At the beginning of the next season, we get to know their story and Vonda a little better.

## 10. Titus Decides to Live as a Werewolf (Season 1 Episode 8)

Titus is cast as a werewolf at an interactive restaurant and while donning his costume in public, he discovers something very interesting - he is treated better as a werewolf!

## 11. Titus Joins the Choir (Season 3 Episode 5)

When Kimmy and Titus start attending church, Titus decides to join the choir. His loud personality in a group setting is always very entertaining.

## 12. Titus Comes out as Black (Season 2 Episode 10)

Titus decides to help his boyfriend, Mikey come out as gay to his family. However, when his boyfriend’s family is way more accepting than expected, Titus is disappointed that the theatrical coming-out he planned for Mikey went unused. So, Titus decides to come out, too. As a black man.

## 13. Titus Tries to Seduce Brandon (Season 1 Episode 5)

When Kimmy’s fellow Mole Woman, Cyndee, visits New York, Titus notices that her boyfriend, Brandon, seems to be gay. He makes it his mission to seduce Brandon and prove that he is gay.

## 14. Titus Goes Viral (Again!) (Season 3 Episode 13)

Remember when Titus sang backup for the conspiracy theorist? Well, it may have not have been as crappy of a gig as he thought. In fact, he is able to sing lead vocals for a new viral song.

## 15. Titus Auditions for a Gum Commercial (Season 2 Episode 12)

Titus visits Mikey when he’s sick in bed, and catches the sickness from him. He doesn’t let this deter him, and goes to his audition for Trident Gum, despite the fact that he’s a little bit delirious.
