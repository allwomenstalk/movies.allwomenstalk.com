# Funniest Sitcoms to Watch on Netflix ...

Want something to watch? here are some of the **funniest sitcoms on Netflix**! Get ready to binge!

## 1. THAT 70S SHOW

6 Teens during the 70s. smoking, drinking and just having fun. 10/10

## 2. RULES of ENGAGEMENT

Show about 6 friends. 2 are married, 2 are engaged and 1 is a bachelor. 10/10

## 3. FRIENDS

Classic sitcom. 6 friends making it in the big city

## 4. BABY DADDY

3 bachelors finally got their own apartment. until one of them gets a girl pregnant who ditches them with the baby

## 5. YOUNG and HUNGRY

Young chef trying to make it big gets a chef job with a young tech millionaire and begins to fall in love

## 6. The RANCH

Ex NFL player Colt Bennet returns home to his family’s farm and causes trouble. 10/10

## 7. NEW GIRL

A 20s girl just got dumped by her hippie boyfriend. she needs a place to live and 3 guys let her live with them. 8/10
