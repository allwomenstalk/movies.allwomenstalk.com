28 Most Adorable on Screen Couples ...

John Krasinski &amp; Jenna Fischer on "the Office (US)" • Lucille Ball &amp; Desi Arnaz on "I Love Lucy" • Scott Michael Foster &amp; Spencer Grammer on "Greek" • Douglas Booth &amp; Miley Cyrus in "LOL" • Kiera Knightly &amp; Matthew Macfadyen in "Pride &amp; Prejudice" • More ...

By Sabrina

On screen couples are sometimes more adorable than the real things! These on screen couples might bring tears to your eyes or a smile to your face, but either way they remind you of all the adorable moments they shared on screen!

1. John Krasinski & Jenna Fischer on "the Office (US)"

Via fischinski.tumblr.com
The Office is an adorable, quirky comedy all on its own. Adorable on screen couples like Jim and Pam take it to the next level!

2. Lucille Ball & Desi Arnaz on "I Love Lucy"

Via Kayla's I Love Lucy Page
What TV couple is more adorable than the invincible I Love Lucy pairing? Both on and off the screen, this couple warms my heart!

3. Scott Michael Foster & Spencer Grammer on "Greek"

Via images2.fanpop.com
The leads on this show, Cappie and Casey, are nothing short of adorable!

4. Douglas Booth & Miley Cyrus in "LOL"

Via sad & rad
One of Miley’s less popular films, LOL, happens to have one of the most adorable on screen couples I know of! Best friends turned boyfriend and girlfriend, Kyle and Lola’s relationship is sweet and classic.

5. Kiera Knightly & Matthew Macfadyen in "Pride & Prejudice"

Via wallpaperscraft.com
Speaking of classic, Pride & Prejudice is a story every girl loves! Who doesn’t want a Mr. Darcy to treat them the way Matthew Macfadyen treats Kiera Knightly in this adaptation of the book?

6. Blake Lively & Penn Badgley on "Gossip Girl"

Via njbc-clube.tumblr.com
Gossip Girl is one of the most popular teenage shows in history! And with amazing on screen couples like Serena and Dan, it’s no wonder why!

7. Ben Savage & Danielle Fishel on "Boy Meets World"

Via Flip through your favorite CHILDHOOD ...
Boy Meets World definitely brings back memories of my childhood! And I always dreamed of being in a relationship even half as sweet as Cory and Topanga’s!

8. John Stamos & Lori Loughlin on "Full House"

Via 25.media.tumblr.com
Another 90’s couple is from Full House! Jessie and Rebecca clicked from the beginning until the end!

9. AnnaSophia Robb & Austin Butler on "the Carrie Diaries"

Via cwtv.com
AnnaSophia Robb’s character, Carrie is certainly lucky to call a piece of eye candy like Austin Butler (Sebastian) as her man!

10. Troian Bellisario & Keegan Allen on "Pretty Little Liars"

Via TV series :)
Spencer and Toby’s relationship on Pretty Little Liars is effortlessly adorable!

11. Jason Segel & Alyson Hannigan on "How I Met Your Mother"

Via People
How I Met Your Mother is a modern classic in the world of TV! Lily and Marshall are practically household names now, thanks to the great chemistry created on screen by these two actors.

12. Zooey Deschanel & Joseph Gordon-Levitt in "500 Days of Summer"

Via ontheroadagainn.tumblr.com
500 Days of Summer is such a fun, sweet movie, with two adorable actors! Zooey and Joseph play off of each other so well in this film that you almost wish they were a real couple!

13. Mark-Paul Gosselaar & Tiffani Thiessen on "Saved by the Bell"

Via Zack and Kelly growing up ...
If a 90’s girl said she never had a crush on Zack Morris, I would be surprised! Zack was a total dreamboat, and Saved by the Bell fans were undoubtedly jealous of Kelly Kapowski!

14. Ku Hye Seon & Lee Min Ho on "Boys over Flowers"

Via Boys Over Flowers, Episode 1
Boys over Flowers is one of the most popular K dramas ever made, and with the adorable pairing of Jan-di and Jun-pyo, it’s obvious why!

15. Jennifer Aniston & David Schwimmer on "FRIENDS"

Via Friends
Friends developed a sort of cult following, and for good reason! Viewers got involved in and captivated by the show’s messy, intriguing relationships like Ross and Rachel’s!

16. Dan Stevens & Michelle Dockery on "Downton Abbey"

Via Downton Abbey Freak :P
This ITV period piece took the world by storm! Part of its success should be accredited to the well-developed on screen relationships, such as the one involving Matthew Crawley and Lady Mary.

17. Mandy Moore & Shane West in "a Walk to Remember"

Via 初恋的回忆 海报
There’s a chance that just as you read the title “A Walk to Remember” you had to swallow some tears! That’s only because Mandy Moore and Shane West did such an incredible job bringing Nicholas Sparks’s characters Jamie and Landon to life!

18. Ginnifer Goodwin & Josh Dallas on "Once upon a Time"

Via Let Me love You
There's no love story more classic than the one of Snow White and her Prince Charming. In Once Upon A Time, the storybook characters are given a unique, modern spin that only makes the couple better! It also doesn't hurt that these two are lovebirds in real life!

19. Patrick Dempsey and Ellen Pompeo on "Grey's Anatomy"

Via E-fa-fa
Grey’s Anatomy is a renowned medical drama. The drama part is accomplished by Dr. Meredith Grey’s relationship with Dr. Derek Shepherd!

20. Ellie & Carl in "up"

Via Quotes
Animated they may be, but Ellie and Carl’s relationship is by far one of the sweetest in cinematic history! Elie Docter and Jeremy Leary did a wonderful job making us fall in love with Ellie and Carl’s brief but touching romance.

21. Rupert Grint & Emma Watson in "Harry Potter"

Via Memorable Movies
Ron and Hermione are a force to be reckoned with when together, and certainly complete one another.

22. Alex Pettyfer & Gabriella Wilde in "Endless Love"

Via Endless Love
Both Alex and Gabriella are gorgeous youths who play young, reckless lovers in “Endless Love”!

23. Molly Ringwald & Michael Schoeffling in "Sixteen Candles"

Via Movies
Sixteen Candles is certainly an 80’s classic! What would the world be without Samantha and Jake?

24. Lauren Graham & Scott Patterson on "Gilmore Girls"

Via Luke & Lorelai Married on ...
Gilmore Girls is one of my favorite television shows of all time! When Luke and Lorelai finally got together, everything seemed right with the world!

25. Tom Hanks & Robin Wright in "Forrest Gump"

Via Love Affairs
Tom Hanks could not have been more perfect for the role of Forrest Gump, but the film wouldn’t have been complete without his on screen childhood friend and love interest, Jenny.

26. Ryan Gosling & Rachel McAdams in "the Notebook"

Via fassyy.tumblr.com
Another one of Nicholas Sparks’s best-sellers, The Notebook is known for its sweet, passionate on-screen couple. The roles of Noah and Allie could not have been casted better!

27. Johnny Depp & Winona Ryder in "Edward Scissorhands"

Via Sign up | Tumblr
It was nearly impossible that their love would last, but the relationship between Edward and Kim is known as one of the sweetest ever!

28. Leonardo DiCaprio & Kate Winslet in "Titanic"

Via Twenty-Five Period Dramas That Make ...
The young, burgeoning actors, Leonardo and Kate, truly showed the world what they were made of when they wove a tragic tale of love and loss!

The list of adorable on screen couples goes on and on! Which couples are your favorite on this list? What other on screen couples make your heart melt?
