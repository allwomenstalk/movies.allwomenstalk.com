# 7 Reasons Why You Should Give Teen Wolf a Chance ...

You might be skipping out on Teen Wolf because you have had enough of the supernatural craze, but let me reassure you this is not a typical werewolf TV show. The storyline revolves around an awkward teenager who has been turned into a wolf, and now he has to face his enemies while still living as a ‘regular’ teenager. Before you completely dismiss the thought of watching it, give Teen Wolf one chance!

## 1. The Humor

One of my top favorite things about Teen Wolf is the humor that is incorporated in the script even during some of the most intense moments. The bromance between Stiles Stilinski and Scott McCall really gives the show an edge and makes it more relatable for teenagers. The dynamic between the characters is truly something that you have to see for yourself, so give it a go!

## 2. The Storyline and Mythology

The plotline of Teen Wolf is not like any other. The writers incorporate some heavy mythological ideas and tie them perfectly together into one entity. The latest season takes a turn for Asian Mythology and explores the idea of kitsune, which is often described as a trickster spirit. So if you are a big fan of mythology, this is something you will definitely enjoy!

## 3. The Cast

Teen Wolf is known for its ridiculously good-looking cast. Every member of the team has his or her own unique personality that shines even through the screen. The lineup of the cast includes: Tyler Posey, Dylan O'Brien, Crystal Reed, Holland Roden, Tyler Hoechlin, and Arden Cho. Trust me, it won’t take long before you fall in love with every character.

## 4. Suspense

The Teen Wolf team knows how to work its magic because every episode just fills you with suspense! There are cliffhangers left and right and there are clues that just lead to bigger and more mind-blowing events. One thing I know for sure is that you will not get bored of the show and you will get sucked in into the Teen Wolf craze in a matter of a couple of episodes.

## 5. Strong Female Characters

Not only does Teen Wolf have strong and fearless male characters, but there are also female characters that steal the spotlight. For example, Crystal Reed and Holland Roden, who play Allison and Lydia, are not portrayed as weak and helpless damsels in distress, but instead they are presented as strong female leads that can take charge. The show just had a new addition of a strong female character to the show by the name of Kira, played by Arden Cho.

## 6. Quality Special Effects

Teen Wolf never fails to impress with its special effects and overall production. With a show that has plenty of supernatural occurrences it is crucial to make everything seem believable, and Teen Wolf does an outstanding job! Fighting scenes seem so real and intense and the transformations to werewolves are just breathtaking.

## 7. You Are Always on Your Toes

As I have said before, this show always has something under its sleeve. Sometimes you might think you know where the plotline is going but later find out that you were completely off. The writers are really clever with how they present their ideas because every episode is unbelievably unpredictable.

Teen Wolf is a gem when it comes to television. There is suspense, romance, horror, comedy, you name it and it has it! Will you be taking a bite of Teen Wolf?
