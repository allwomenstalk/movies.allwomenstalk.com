# The Best Ever Shopping Scenes in the Movies Every Shopaholic Will Love ...

If you love shopping, you appreciate the fun ways it is portrayed in the movies. Some will be stereotypical, some will be funny and some will poke fun but it’s entertaining to spot yourself, if you can.

## 1. Superbad

The hilarious shopping scene between Jonah Hill and Michael Cena will be familiar to all of us. “Are these jeans too tight”? Yes, absolutely!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/superbad-unrated/id323044694)

## 2. What Women Want

Back in the days when we still though Mel Gibson was a good guy, he took part in a cute prom dress shopping montage!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/what-women-want/id209114165)

## 3. Mallrats

There is a particular scene in this comedy classic that perfectly encapsulates what it’s like to visit a mall, especially navigating the intricacies of the food court!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/mallrats/id740561396)

## 4. Sweet Home Alabama

Sure, this scene is kind of cheesy, but who wouldn’t want to have a private shopping session in a fabulous diamond ring store!?

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/sweet-home-alabama/id187699130)

## 5. Serendipity

This entire movie is basically just a compilation of great in-store scenes between John Cusack and Kate Beckinsale.

Buy at: _itunes.apple.com_

## 6. You’ve Got Mail

The battle between small independent book store and large corporation book store makes for some fun shopping scenes going between the both of them.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/youve-got-mail/id284580850)

## 7. Marie Antoinette

You haven’t been shopping until you have gone Marie Antoinette style shopping; shoes and cake for everyone!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/marie-antoinette/id284600613)

## 8. Mean Girls

Who can forget that delicious revenge scene when Rachel McAdams’ character is finding it hard to fit in to her favorite size dresses!And of course - "Get in loser. We're going shopping!"

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/mean-girls/id208511170)

## 9. Sex & the City

Of all the great shopping scenes in Sex & The City, I actually love the simple drug store trip that Carrie and Miranda take to find Halloween costumes.

Buy at: _itunes.apple.com_

## 10. Bridesmaids

Nobody who has seen Bridesmaids will ever be able to forget the eventful bridesmaids dress shopping trip that ended up in some very sticky and smelly places!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/bridesmaids-unrated/id460403139)

## 11. Confessions of a Shopaholic

This movie perfectly encapsulates the thrill and potential danger of being first in line for a designer sample sale!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/confessions-of-a-shopaholic/id307069284)

## 12. Clueless

Not even a designer shopping spree can wake Cher up from her romantic daydreams and troubles.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/clueless/id300913833)

## 13. Home Alone

The classic bag scene from Home Alone is always a crowd pleaser. It's one of my favorite moments in the movie!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/home-alone/id344796733)

## 14. Big

Who can forget the classic shopping scene in Big that culminates in the iconic human piano moment!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/big/id435015758)

## 15. The Sweetest Thing

We all love a good montage, and the funny outfit montage that the ladies of The Sweetest Thing take part in is a highlight of the movie.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/the-sweetest-thing/id263832467)

## 16. Breakfast at Tiffany’s

You will be hard pushed to find a more iconic window shopping scene than Holly Golightly’s wistful morning browsing in Breakfast at Tiffany’s.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/m6ImrN)

## 17. Pretty Woman

This double whammy scene combination gives the audience a lot of pleasure, with Julia Roberts first being kicked out of the store and later on going back to rub their noses in it! "Big mistake!"

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/pretty-woman/id492172140)

## 18. Crazy, Stupid, Love

Ryan Gosling takes Steve Carell’s character on a shopping trip to the mall in an attempt to update his wardrobe and change his romantic fortunes.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/crazy-stupid-love/id457658502)

## 19. Elf

Don’t make the same mistake that Buddy makes by buying sexy lingerie for your dad. Oh so awkward!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/DYuLka)

## 20. The Hot Chick

Check out this movie for a bizarre trip to a local voodoo shop to buy some earrings that will turn a hot chick in to a hairy man!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/the-hot-chick/id188702765)

## 21. Jingle All the Way

Jingle All the Way perfectly captures the absolute frenzy that is trying to get the sought-after toy in the run up to Christmas!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/jingle-all-the-way/id270048567)

## 22. Planes, Trains and Automobiles

Arguably the best display of show curtain ring salesmanship in the history of cinema!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/planes-trains-automobiles/id314510037)

## 23. Joe versus the Volcano

What more could you want then a young, handsome Tom Hanks going shopping for tuxes and trunks!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/joe-versus-the-volcano/id891799274)

## 24. Muriel’s Wedding

Wedding dress shopping mixed with an ABBA soundtrack, who wouldn’t love that!?

Buy at: _itunes.apple.com_

## 25. A Very Brady Sequel

Because no good shopping trip is complete without a big production song and dance number!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/a-very-brady-sequel/id306472876)
