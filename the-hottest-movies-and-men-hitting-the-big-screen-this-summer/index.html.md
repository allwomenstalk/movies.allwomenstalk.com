# The Hottest Movies and Men Hitting the Big Screen This Summer ...

You look forward to summer for a lot of reasons! Summer vacations, beach days, blockbuster movies... and the [hot guys](https://love.allwomenstalk.com/hot-guys-which-one-is-your-type/) who are in them! Here’s a list so you know what Hollywood babes you can expect to see on the big screen Summer 2015!

## 1. Chris Pratt

![Chris Pratt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/rq/wot33fjp55832b95b496d835907320_480x540.jpg)

Movie: Jurassic World
Not only is Jurassic World a fantastic film, but it also features the gorgeous Chris Pratt! He strikes the perfect balance between adorable and manly, with his kind eyes and chisled jawline. In his latest role, he is a courageous and chivalrous heartthrob!

## 2. Quincy Brown

![Quincy Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vb/hd/u48zvi9w55832be800633888252842_500x657.jpg)

Movie: Dope
Dope is shaping up to be one of the most uplifting movies of the summer (a pleasant break from the overabundance of action movies!) It also features a stellar cast! Part of that cast is the stunning Quincy Brown! Look at that smile-tell me your heart isn't melting!

## 3. Channing Tatum

![Channing Tatum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/aq/k9oh02qh5584b5590680b819544504_600x849.jpg)

Movie: Magic Mike XXl
Looks like this summer is going to be hot for more than one reason! Apparently, one Magic Mike wasn't enough! Channing Tatum is taking to the screen-and taking off his clothing-once again this summer in Magic Mike XXI!

## 4. Byung-hun Lee

![Byung-hun Lee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mm/lz/p482s6ha5584b543395ca558712080_500x750.jpg)

Movie: Terminator Genisys
I don't know much about the movie yet, but I know Byung-Hun Lee has certainly terminated my heart! With his smouldering gaze, I know Terminator Genisys will capture my attention from beginning to end!

## 5. Michael B. Jordan

![Michael B. Jordan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oj/jh/n4c411a455832c6e57450118294246_600x800.jpg)

Movie: Fantastic Four
Who doesn't have a crush on Michael B. Jordan? He's positively dreamy! And in Fantastic Four, you'll get to see a different side of him-the I-can-save-the-Earth-and-look-great-too side! I'm confident the film will be a hit, but even on the off chance that it isn't, you still gained something from watching it, didn't you?

## 6. Garrett Hedlund

![Garrett Hedlund](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t1/d5/yche2zzg5584b527f0e62028947205_499x750.jpg)

Movie: Pan
I'm a huge fan of any rendition of Peter Pan! I'm particularly excited for this film to be released, because I know it will show a different side of the villian we all grew up with: Captain Hook. And, of course, it doesn't hurt that the dashing Garrett Hedlund is playing the young pirate!

## 7. Chris Hemsworth

![Chris Hemsworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ip/hz/lik1oawo5584b50417df4495045592_600x903.jpg)

Movie: Vacation
Last, but not least by any means, is Chris Hemsworth. I don't know about you, but I thank the heavens for him and his brothers! Not only is this Aussie devilishly handsome, he's also a charming and talented actor, which is why I'm certain Vacation will be a hilarious hit!

If your summer consists of staycations and mini-breaks from the hustle and bustle of reality, take advantage of the time to see some of these blockbuster hits! I promise you'll enjoy them, if not for their plots at least for their cast!
