# 7 Adorable Disney Characters ...

Let’s face it; Disney has produced some of the most adorable characters of all times. I for one find myself constantly in a state of aww (yes aww, not awe) over the movies because the characters are just so darn precious!

## 1. Dug from up!

![Dug from up!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/09/1_dug-from-up_500x600.jpg)

Dug is my personal favorite adorable Disney character. I own a stuffed animal version of him, and I pretty much freak out whenever I watch Up!, but just for him! He is so cute and so loving it is impossible not to like his character. If only they could invent a way dogs could talk to us! POINT!

## 2. Dory from Finding Nemo

![Dory from Finding Nemo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/09/2_dory-from-finding-nemo_554x430.jpg)

The short-term memory impaired fish is another one of Disney’s most adorable characters. Her goofy personality shines through the entirety of the movie and you just can’t get enough of her. She really makes Finding Nemo come together to be the amazing movie it is!

## 3. Gus Gus from Cinderella

![Gus Gus from Cinderella](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/09/3_gus-gus-from-cinderella_500x375.jpg)

Though he is not a completely essential part of Cinderella, he left his mark. The adorably confused mouse pulls at your heartstrings because he is just so helplessly clueless. You can’t help but smile at him!

## 4. WALL-E

![WALL-E](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/09/4_wall-e_810x528.jpg)

Come on now! He doesn’t even speak and he is so precious. The little robot stranded on earth is the cutest little thing. The whole movie you just root for him to overcome the naturally clumsy disposition he has. And we can’t even forget the gentle nature he has, protecting and cherishing what all of us would consider to be junk. Ah! I’m not even watching the movie, but just writing this makes me want to give WALL-E a big hug!

## 5. Winnie the Pooh

![Winnie the Pooh](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/09/5_winnie-the-pooh_1024x768.jpg)

Winnie the Pooh is not only one of the most adorable Disney characters, but he is also one of the most classically cherished. The story of Pooh has been around for a long time and the interpretation of the bear has always been big hearted and kind. He is such an accepting character, which is something that everyone should be anyway; we just have an adorable Disney bear teaching us!

## 6. Marie from the Aristocats

![Marie from the Aristocats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/09/6_marie-from-the-aristocats_600x710.jpg)

One of the lesser-known Disney characters, Marie, is still as adorable as the rest. This kind but feisty kitten definitely knows the way of being a lady. One of my favorite quotes is actually from her character; «Ladies don't start fights, but they can finish them!» Her sassy attitude undoubtedly makes her adorable!

## 7. Dopey from Snow White and the Seven Dwarves

![Dopey from Snow White and the Seven Dwarves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/09/7_dopey-from-snow-white-and-the-seven-dwarves_500x366.jpg)

Everyone knows Dopey, which means everyone knows that Dopey is one of the most adorable Disney characters ever. He is preciously clueless, but has the biggest heart. His loyalty to Snow White is so delightful to see. It makes you wish you could have a version of Dopey in your own life!

Disney’s movies are full of adorable characters; these are just a few of my favorites. Writing this was making me reminisce about all of the old and new Disney movies filled with characters that you can’t help but connect with. Who are some of your favorite adorable Disney characters?
