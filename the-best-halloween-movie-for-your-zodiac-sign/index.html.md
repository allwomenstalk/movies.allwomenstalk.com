# The Best Halloween Movie for Your Zodiac Sign ...

With October in full swing, we are getting closer and closer to that haunted holiday, Halloween! If you are the kind of person who likes to celebrate this holiday, then you will probably be putting together a list of spooky movies to watch to get in the mood for Halloween. There are plenty of classics to choose from out there, but you need to make sure that you have one particular title on your list in order to keep the stars aligned! Here is the best Halloween movie for your zodiac sign.

## 1. Aries

Halloweentown

You want to be on theme, but if you’re being honest, you don’t actually enjoy any of the really scary stuff! This Disney Channel original classic is the perfect alternative to horror.

## 2. Taurus

It’s The Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown

You like the classic side of pop culture, and it doesn’t get more classic than Charlie Brown! This is an animated Halloween adventure that will definitely put a smile on your face and get you in the mood for spooky celebration.

## 3. Gemini

Practical Magic

You want something that you can settle down with and watch with your BFFs, and Practical Magic is probably one of the best gal pal Halloween movies of all time! Nicole Kidman and Sandra Bullock playing witchy sisters; what more could you possibly want?

## 4. Cancer

Corpse Bride

You are a hopeless romantic so Halloween isn’t always your favourite of the holidays, but you can enjoy a dose of emotional romance in Tim Burton’s Corpse Bride. Don’t be fooled by the fact that it’s a cartoon!

## 5. Leo

Beetlejuice

You need something dramatic to match your sense of humour and your extreme confidence, and Beetlejuice is the perfect choice! He’s just as much the life of party as you are!

## 6. Virgo

Hocus Pocus

A straight up classic, of all time, not just Halloween! Virgos are interested in acclaimed and successful things, and Hocus Pocus is certainly that.

## 7. Libra

Casper

Casper is a wonderful Halloween movie for a Libra who is classically sensitive and intelligent. There are more layers to the story than just a spooky house filled with a few ghosts. It’s actually really emotional in places!

## 8. Scorpio

The Craft

You need something a little edgy to match your unconventional vibe, and The Craft is definitely that. It’s an angsty teenage supernatural drama that packs a lot of punch!

## 9. Sagittarius

The Nightmare Before Christmas

Like a true Sagittarius, you can never make up your mind, so watching this Tim Burton classic is perfect because you can a Halloween movie and a Christmas movie all in one!

## 10. Capricorn

Ghostbusters

Whether you want to watch the 80s original or the recent all-star female fronted remake, the quirky humour of Ghostbusters is what a Capricorn needs.

## 11. Aquarius

The Addams Family

All the different members of the Addams Family can represent an Aquarius on any given day; it’s almost like the film is a representation of all of your quirky split personality sides!

## 12. Pisces

The Little Vampire

A wonderful little movie about two best friends who would do anything for each other; something that you can relate to a lot!
