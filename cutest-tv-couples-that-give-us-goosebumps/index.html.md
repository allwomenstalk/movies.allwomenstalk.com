# 7 Cutest TV Couples That Give Us Goosebumps ...

Call me silly, but I'm a sucker for the cutest TV couples. My own love life is good, but there's just something about seeing romance unfold on screen that makes my heart soar. Romance is a big part of our lives “offline”, so it's understandable that we'd enjoy it on TV. It's one of the few times we can see the nice guy (or girl) finish first rather than last; for those of us who were the shy kid in the corner, this might be the only time. Regardless of how it happens, it's always good to see characters we've come to know and love find happiness. To that end, here are some of the cutest TV couples I've seen. I'm sure you can think of more. Caution: this post contains spoilers! Read at your own risk!

## 1. Blaine and Kurt-Glee

![Blaine and Kurt-Glee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/de/xq/m83jhu6b_570x858.jpg)

These two are one of the cutest TV couples, if only for how they filled a hole in each others' lives. Kurt was Blaine's first love and encouraged him to follow his dreams. Blaine showed Kurt that it's okay to be different when he needed it the most and organized one of the best proposal scenes I've ever seen. Being engaged so young will be a challenge, but they were made for each other and will find a way to make it.

## 2. Lisa Simpson and Nelson Munst

![Lisa Simpson and Nelson Munst](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/er/ek/mvl3sx2q_500x499.jpg)

I know they're an unlikely couple - he the class bully, she the brainy feminist - but that's what made them so cute. They went back and forth and even made a brief go at a relationship until they finally realized that they were not meant to be together. “Smell ya later”- a line for the ages.

## 3. Mitch and Cam-Modern Family

_ I haven't seen Modern Family very much, but I'm putting them on this list because their joint-proposal was beyond sweet. What's even better is that it was completely different from what either partner planned, but it still made for one of the sweetest proposals on TV. Read more about it here: tv.yahoo.com_

## 4. Lady Mary and Matthew Crawley-Downton Abbey

![Lady Mary and Matthew Crawley-Downton Abbey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/nu/sp27tdpv_600x900.jpg)

To say they got off to a rocky start would be an understatement. Even though she didn't like him at first, it was obvious that these two would end up together. They may not have had much time as man and wife, but he will always be the one who melted Lady Mary's heart.

## 5. The Bones Couples (as a Group)

![The Bones Couples (as a Group)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wy/pl/kp7i875m_511x640.jpg)

There are so many great couples from this show that listing them separately would probably take up too much bandwidth. Bones and Booth, Cam and Arastoo, me and Vincent (in my dreams, maybe), Angela and Hodgins -their evolving relationships have always been a big part of the show's popularity. Some people thought Booth and Bones getting married would ruin the show, but I don't care. As long as I can see Booth and Hodgins shirtless, I'm happy.

## 6. Cristina and Owen-Grey's Anatomy

![Cristina and Owen-Grey's Anatomy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fp/fd/y44ch9ww_480x480.jpg)

Married or not, these two can't stay away from each other. I never understood what it was that linked them so quickly, but I guess you don't have to put a name on something for it to be real. They may be too much alike, but it's obvious they love each other. It's going to be really sad when Cristina leaves for good.

## 7. Ted Mosby and Robin Scherbatsky-How I Met Your Mother

![Ted Mosby and Robin Scherbatsky-How I Met Your Mother](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jo/eo/tpx796tb_471x707.jpg)

It was obvious from the beginning that Ted and Robin were going to end up together. Sure, Barney put together a kick-ass proposal, but I never understood what it was that brought them together. While I wanted to throw things at the screen when Robin announced they got divorced when the entire last season led up to their wedding, I wasn't surprised. Robin and Ted went back and forth so many times, but the fact that Ted showed up at her window with the blue French horn decades later shows that they were meant to be together.

It doesn't really matter if you're single or married, gay or straight, male or female; anyone can enjoy a well-crafted love story. These couples - whether it works out or not - put a unique spin on things. What about you? Who are some of your favorite TV couples? Are there any characters who aren't couples, but should be? Which ones have I left out? Discuss!
