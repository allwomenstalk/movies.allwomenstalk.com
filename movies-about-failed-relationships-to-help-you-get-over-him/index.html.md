# 7 Movies about Failed Relationships to Help You Get over Him ...

Unfortunately, just because you're in love with someone doesn't mean that you should spend the rest of your life with him. Sometimes, your paths are going to separate. Even though it's what's best for you, it's hard to handle, which is why you should watch these movies about failed relationships to help you get over your ex:

## 1. Chasing Amy

Ben Affleck plays a comic book artist who falls in love with a woman who turns out to be a lesbian. However, the story is way more complicated than that. There are a lot of twists and turns, but even when things seem to be going well between the pair, it's obvious that it's not going to work out in the end. This movie will show you that you're not always meant to date the person you've fallen in love with.

## 2. Forgetting Sarah Marshall

Here's a comedy with Jason Segel, Kristen Bell, Russell Brand, Mila Kunis, Bill Hader, and Paul Rudd. It's about a man who gets dumped by the girl of his dreams and goes to Hawaii to try to get over her. However, she ends up on the same island and makes it incredibly difficult for him to move on. Don't worry--there's a happy ending.

## 3. The Breakup

If you love Jennifer Aniston or Vince Vaughn, you should enjoy this movie. It wasn't their most popular one, but it's certainly worth watching. It's about a couple who has a nasty breakup and aims to hurt each other. It'll show you that no one wins when you try to get revenge.

## 4. The Wedding Singer

This movie is about two people who are engaged to the wrong person. It'll help you see that the person you've spent years dating might not be the one for you. Of course, someone else even better is out there waiting to find you.

## 5. (500) Days of Summer

Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Zooey Deschanel make an adorable onscreen couple, but that doesn't mean that they should spend the rest of their lives together. Sometimes, relationships are only meant to last for a few months or years. The voiceover at the start of the movie warns you that there won't be a "traditional" happy ending. However, the last scene is comforting for anyone who has just suffered through a breakup.

## 6. John Tucker Must Die

This movie is about a guy who cheats on multiple women at a time. Sure, he's attractive, but he's a player. If you want to watch a movie with girl power, this is the one for you. It'll help you realize that you should never settle for a cheater, no matter how nice he appears.

## 7. Up

Okay, this relationship didn't exactly fail, but it can still help you find a way to live without your ex. Everybody knows about the heart wrenching scenes where the old man falls in love with a woman, marries her, and then watches her die. Well, while he's miserable without her, he finds a way to be happy when he meets a young boy. Yes, the movie is sad, but it'll make you realize that life is still worth living without the person you love by your side.

It's hard to get over someone that you loved dearly. However, it's entirely possible and these movies could help ease your pain. What other movies about failed relationships are out there?
