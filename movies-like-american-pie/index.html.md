# Wild Movies like American Pie You Should Watch before Leaving for College ...

American Pie is a teen sex comedy. It follows the story of a group of teenagers who want to lose their virginity before leaving for college. It is a very relatable movie, especially for young adults. There are about four movies in the original American Pie series including the first one that came out in 1999. These series are full of wild, hilarious fun and rated R for strong sexuality, language, and drinking. If you want to watch more movies like American Pie, this list has some amazing options.

## 1. American Pie – Presents Series (2005-2009)

These movies are spin-offs of the American pie franchise. Band Camp came out in 2005, the Naked Mile came out in 2006, Beta house came out in 2007, and the Book of Love came out in 2009.

## 2. Project X (2012)

Three unpopular high school boys decide to throw a huge party to boost their social status but things go out of control when the news about the party spreads and their house is flooded with crazy party people. The movie is full of drama, sex, and drugs.

## 3. Euro Trip (2004)

After graduating from high school, Scott makes a spontaneous plan to go to Europe to meet a girl he thinks is ‘the love of his life’. The movie is hilarious, it exaggerates stereotypes about Europe in funny ways and has been shot in beautiful European landmarks that will take your breath away.

## 4. Superbad (2007)

The movie stars Jonah Hill and Michael Cera, two of Hollywood’s most well-known young comedy actors. A movie about friendship, with witty dialogue, and vulgar comedy that will be loved by young adults.

## 5. Accepted (2006)

A high school graduate is rejected from all the colleges he applies to. To not disappoint his parents, he decides to open a fake college, the South Harmon Institute of Technology. With the help of his friends, he creates a website and hires someone to pose as the dean so that his parents believe that the college is real. Trouble follows when other rejects show up at the college through the active college link to gain admission resulting in some funny circumstances and eventually, the movie ends in a positive inspiring note. The movie stars Justin Long, Jonah Hill, and Blake Lively.

## 6. The Girl Next Door (2004)

This movie is really popular among teenage guys. It follows the story of an average high school student who is next-door neighbors with a porn star. Every teenage boys' dream right? This is an unexpected, refreshing and sweet teen love story starring Elisha Cuthbert and Emilie Hirsch, the actor from ‘Into the Wild’.

## 7. Road Trip (2000)

Road Trip is a teen comedy about crazy college experiences and stereotypes. A fun-filled movie about guys on a road trip getting into trouble and making the audience laugh at their funny antics. This movie is directed by Todd Phillips, the director who is responsible for giving us the record-breaking hit comedy, The Hangover! So you know this movie will not disappoint in the fun department.

## 8. Sex Drive (2008)

Ian is in high school who wants to lose his virginity. A woman that he met online asks him to come to Knoxville to hook up. He and his two best friends steal a car and go on the road trip of a lifetime to find his dream woman. Just like the other movies, this movie is about a fun journey with friends filled with all kinds of craziness, and funny adventures whilst learning a lot about life and real love in the process.

## 9. Scary Movie (2000)

This horror-comedy movie is a parody of many famous horror movies including I know what You Did Last Summer, Scream, etc. starring Regina Hall and Anna Farris. A group of teenagers is being stalked by a creepy maniac who they killed a year back. This movie will make you laugh out loud, and is a perfect movie to watch at a sleepover with friends.

## 10. Wet Hot American Summer (2001)

No better way to end this list other than with this comedy classic. Wet hot American summer is a parody of many wild high school camp movies. The movie has witty and humorous dialogue and smart wordplay. It recreates the plots of high school camp movies from the ’70s and ’80s while making it super funny and fresh. The movie stars Elizabeth Banks, Paul Rudd, and David Hyde Pierce.

These movies like American Pie are hilarious and enjoyable. They remind you to lay back and have some wild fun with your friends.
