# 7 All Time Favorite Disney Shows ...

I strongly believe that it is impossible for anyone to not have at least a few favorite Disney shows. I don't know about you, but I grew up with Disney. My all time favorite Disney shows even include Playhouse Disney! Back then I could watch it shamelessly. Ah, those were the times.

## 1. That's so Raven

![That's so Raven](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/07212806_6334_600x900.jpg)

I used to be so jealous (and probably still am) of how Raven Symone got a show named after her. The show was so hilarious and it made me wish that I could look into the future too. Can't blame a kid for being naive, can you? It's really one of my generation's all time favorite Disney shows.

## 2. Even Stevens

![Even Stevens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20111330_8980_300x400.jpg)

I think lots of people forgot that Shia LaBeouf started out on Disney. Pre-Transformers Shia LaBeouf was so adorable with that curly mane. The show even came with its own movie, at a time when Disney channel original movies were actually something to look forward to, unlike 'Teen Beach Movie.'

## 3. Lizzie McGuire

![Lizzie McGuire](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20111234_5841_445x540.jpg)

Part Hilary Duff, part cartoon figure equals Lizzie McGuire. This was probably one of the more relatable shows Disney has ever produced, at least for girls. It's not entirely relatable though (i.e. Lizzie McGuire the Movie) because if it was, it wouldn't be Disney. But I enjoyed watching it anyways.

## 4. The Suite Life Series

![The Suite Life Series](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25003309_9870_600x930.jpg)

This was a series, the first being 'The Suite Life of Zack and Cody' and the second being 'The Suite Life on Deck.' I watched 'The Suite Life of Zack and Cody' recently and I just realised how small they actually were. They were so cute! There was Ashley Tisdale before 'High School Musical' and guest appearances from young Selena Gomez and Victoria Justice, which made me squeal. There's just something about blasts from the past that arouses me.

## 5. Madeleine

![Madeleine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/07213042_2787_465x500.jpg)

Told you there's going to be some Playhouse Disney involved. How can anybody not go awww at the little French girl in her yellow dress? And her puppy! What I also really like about it was that they try to teach you simple French words subtly, unlike in-your-face Spanish on Dora the Explorer. I remember watching it after coming home from school everyday. My lunch time cartoons included PB&J Otter and the Three Siamese Cats as well.

## 6. Phineas & Ferb

![Phineas & Ferb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20110949_6690_600x800.jpg)

This is one of the more recent cartoons that I actually like. I love the plot. Two kids creating new inventions everyday of their summer, which never lasts long enough to get 'busted' by their older sister, Candace. Great stuff. Though I feel as if Disney is paying less attention on their Disney Channel TV series nowadays, which makes me feel really sorry for the children in this generation.

## 7. Kim Possible

![Kim Possible](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/07213125_4471_600x960.jpg)

Cheerleader turned secret agent who's even got a catchy name! Disney seemed to create lots of female starring shows like Totally Spies and the Disney Princesses. One of my friends had that Kim Possible ringtone as his own. It was so awesome. And the coolest Disney villain ever would probably be Shego.

Disney was such a huge part of my childhood that sometimes people thought I was from America because of the way I talk. Obviously, that comes from watching too much Disney when I was a kid. Now it's just nice for reminiscing. How 'bout you? What reminds you of your childhood?
