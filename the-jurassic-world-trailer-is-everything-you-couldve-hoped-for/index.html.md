# The Jurassic World Trailer is Everything You Couldve Hoped for ...

The trailer for Jurassic World, featuring Chris Pratt, was released Tuesday morning, November 25th. The plot of the movie (the fourth installation in the franchise) centers on “twenty-two years after the events of Jurassic Park, Isla Nublar now features a fully functioning dinosaur theme park, Jurassic World, as originally envisioned by John Hammond.”

The trailer has everything you need to hold you over till the June 2015 release: regular dinosaurs, aquatic dinosaurs, Chris Pratt, and the promise of a very interesting plot line.
