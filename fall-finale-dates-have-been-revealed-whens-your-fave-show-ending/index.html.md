# Fall Finale Dates Have Been Revealed: when's Your Fave Show Ending? ...

It's such a sad time when a TV show's season comes to an end. I mean, what's there to do until next season (besides re-watching all the seasons AGAIN)? TV networks have released their schedules and revealed the fall finale dates for all your favorite shows! Check them out here:

ABC
**Castle:** Monday, Dec. 8 at 10 pm
**Grey's Anatomy:** Thursday, Nov. 20 at 8 pm
**How to Get Away With Murder:** Thursday, Nov. 20 at 10 pm
**Nashville:** Wednesday, Dec. 10 at 10 pm
**Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.:** Tuesday, Dec. 9 at 9 pm
**Once Upon a Time:** Sunday, Dec. 14 at 8 pm
**Resurrection:** Sunday, Dec. 14 at 9 pm
**Revenge:** Sunday, Dec. 14 at 10 pm
**Scandal:** Thursday, Nov. 20 at 9 pm

The CW
**The 100:** Wednesday, Dec. 17 at 9 pm
**Arrow:** Wednesday, Dec. 10 at 8 pm
**The Flash:** Tuesday, Dec. 9 at 8 pm
**Jane the Virgin:** Monday, Dec. 15 at 9 pm
**The Originals:** Monday, Dec. 8 at 8 pm
**Reign:** Thursday, Dec. 11 at 9 pm
**Supernatural:** Tuesday. Dec. 9 at 9 pm
**The Vampire Diaries:** Thursday, Dec. 11 at 8 pm

FX
**Sons of Anarchy:** Tuesday, Dec. 9 at 10 pm

Fox, CBS and NBC haven't released their schedules yet but check back for an update! When's your favorite show's finale?
