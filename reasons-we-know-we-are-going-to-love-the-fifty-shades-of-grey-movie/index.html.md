# 7 Reasons We Know We Are Going to Love the Fifty Shades of Grey Movie ...

Are you as excited as I am about the Fifty Shades of Grey movie? Believe it or not, I am just now reading this trilogy. I know. It’s as if I have been living underground to have missed out on them. And as much as all of us ladies love the books, I know that the the Fifty Shades of Grey movie is going to be even better.

## 1. The Chemistry Will Be Sizzling

One of the things I cannot wait for in the Fifty Shades of Grey movie is to see the chemistry between the characters of Christian and Anastasia. Although we may or may not love the actor and actress that have been chosen for these characters, I for one can see the potential for some sizzling chemistry. In fact, I have read that part of the reason those specific individuals were chosen was the chemistry between them. I look forward to seeing the air spark between these two with a mere look at one another. The question isn’t if there will be chemistry but how deliciously we can enjoy it.

## 2. Inspiring Ideas for Our Bedrooms

Let’s come clean here, ladies. The Fifty Shades of Grey trilogy gave us ideas beyond anything we could have ever dreamed of. At least I know it did for me. It will be even more inspiring when we can visually see it play out. If you are lucky, your man might go see it with you and you can have a whole new window of options to discuss.

## 3. The Love Element

Most of us ladies are a romantic at heart. We are a sucker for a story of deep and sacrificial love. There was no doubt to me early on that Christina deeply loved Anastasia. It was almost painful for me to wait for him to discover it. But when he did, it was an amazing turning point in the story. Soon, we can watch this play out on the big screen.

## 4. Fantasy Food

Yes, ladies, this will give us fantasy material for the next bazillion or so years. Reading the trilogy gave many women the courage to talk to their husbands about things that they found themselves interested in trying. Watching this movie has got to feed the fantasies even more. I think many women will find themselves going home all revved up for sex with their man. After all, the best fantasies are the ones we play out with the one we love.

## 5. The Unique Nature

Entertainment Weekly said that Fifty Shades of Grey was “in a class all by itself.” They couldn’t be more accurate. This delicious combination of erotica with a passionate love story has cast a spell on women around the world. Not only was this a unique new class of reading but it will be a unique class of film. This certainly won’t have any competitors at the box office.

## 6. Christian Grey is Downright Sexy

I have to admit, I am one of those that was not rooting for Charlie Hunnam. I actually had a different actor in mind for the character of Christian. But regardless, I think that Charlie is going to do just fine. If you have any doubt, do a little research on YouTube for his previous sex scenes. You will need no further proof.

## 7. We All Want to Be ANASTASIA

Okay, we may not want to be dominated as outlandishly as she was, but now come on, ladies. We wouldn’t have this fiercely passionate love affair with Fifty Shades of Grey if we didn’t envy Miss Steele. We want our man to be as crazy about us as Christian is, although we could probably do without the crazy in the head element. We want someone who loves us as deeply and will drop everything to shelter and protect us. And we want someone who knows their way around a bedroom.

Talk to me, ladies. What is it you are most looking forward to in the Fifty Shades of Grey movie? What do you love most about this addictive love story?
