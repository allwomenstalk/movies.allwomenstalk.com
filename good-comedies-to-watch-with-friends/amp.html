7 Good Comedies to Watch with Friends ...

Your Highness • The Pacifier • The Naked Gun • Idiocracy • Grandma’s Boy • More ...

By Jelena

I absolutely love comedies and, when it comes to watching them with my friends, I have to agree there is simply no better way to spend a lazy evening. Now, when it comes to good comedies to watch with friends, I can’t really say I prefer this or that- Anything goes as long as we’re all having fun. So, with that being said, I’m giving you the list of movies I consider Good Comedies to Watch with Friends:

1. Your Highness

Price: $16.99 at amazon.com
If you’re looking for some new good comedies to watch with friends you definitely can’t go wrong with Your Highness! I’ve watched it alone and had a lot of fun but I’m sure the experience would be much better if I had some friends watching it with me. I mean, there’s nothing worse than turning around trying to do the “Hahaha, did you see that?” move only to realize there’s nobody in the room. There is no nudity but a pretty decent amount of foul language is to be expected so, make sure you watch it with people who appreciate that type of humor.

2. The Pacifier

Price: $11.99 at amazon.com
Watch this cool action/comedy if you like Vin Diesel and would love to see him playing a slightly different role. There are a lot of funny scenes, hilarious dialogues, witty one-liners and, regardless of how strange it sounds, not even one car gets trashed! It’s one of those family comedies so you can let your younger siblings watch, too.

3. The Naked Gun

Price: $16.99 at amazon.com
You got to love Leslie Nielsen! I think I’ve watched every single one of his movies although I must admit Officer Frank Drebin is and always will be one of my favorite characters. Now, if you’re familiar with The Naked Gun Trilogy you will notice that I wasn’t very specific, the reason for that, of course, being my inability to decide which one to suggest. All three of them are great so maybe you could get together a bit earlier, organize something like a movie night and watch them all? Now, that would be awesome!

4. Idiocracy

Price: $7.49 at amazon.com
What would you do if you woke up one day only to realize that you’ve been in hibernation for 500 years and that a lot has changed? And by that I mean really a LOT! Well this old but pretty funny comedy is about to show you one undoubtedly funny but rather scary future in which the people have become so dumb they can’t even tie their own shoe laces!

5. Grandma’s Boy

Price: $7.49 at amazon.com
Good comedies to watch with friends are pretty hard to find these days so I simply must travel back in time to 2006 and suggest this classic no-brainer. I know, I know, movies like this are pretty dumb and probably not something you’d want to watch over and over again but hey, watching a totally “dumb” movie here and there can be a lot of fun!

6. The Proposal

Price: $12.49 at amazon.com
This movie is so damn funny I had to watch it three times! It’s not a standard ha-ha-ha comedy but more of a weird-situation-turns-into-a-love-story type of movies – all in all a very good thing to watch with your girl friends. No drugs, alcohol, foul language or any of that stuff which pretty much means nothing to blush about, although you should be careful with that soda as there are a few hilarious scenes that might send it “down the wrong hole”.

7. Monty Python and the Holy Grail

Price: $6.99 at amazon.com
Speaking about good comedies to watch with friends – have you seen this one? I watched it so many times and I could watch it again anytime! Yeah, yeah, you are right- it’s, like, ancient but they sure don’t make them like this anymore and I’m sure even the younger generations will find this interpretation of historical facts totally hilarious!

Well, those were my suggestions on Good Comedies to Watch with Friends so if you have anything to add, feel free to do so anytime. The ones I’ve listed here are pretty old so if you know of a few new but good comedies to watch with friends, I definitely want to hear about them. When was the last time you had a good laugh, anyways?
