# 7 Amazing Movies Based on YA Novels for Teen Girls ...

The book is usually better than the movie, but that doesn't mean you shouldn't watch them at all. You might end up liking the ending of the film better or finding a hot actor that you can crush on. Here are a few amazing movies based off of young adult novels that you should really watch at least once:

## 1. The Hunger Games

Everyone knows the name Katniss by now. If you don't know much about her story, except for the fact she volunteers for something, then you need to see the film. It won't disappoint.

## 2. The Maze Runner

It's not exactly the same as the book, but what movie is? At least this film has hot stars in it, like Dylan O'Brien and Thomas Brodie-Sangster. They make the movie worth watching.

## 3. Divergent

We all wish we could embrace our badass side, which is why this story is so much fun to watch. You're going to come out of this movie wishing you had a tattoo.

## 4. The Chronicles of Narnia: the Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe

Animal lovers should watch this film, just to see the adorable lion. Of course, the story isn't bad either.

## 5. The Book Thief

This movie just might make you cry. It's about a little girl living in Germany during World War II.

## 6. The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones

Do you like fantasy? Then you'll love this film, which is about a woman who has to help protect the world from demons.

## 7. Twilight

Don't groan at this suggestion. If you haven't seen the film yet, then you should watch it, just so you can understand the references.

## 8. Paper Towns

Are you a fan of John Green? Then you should watch this movie, which contains Nat Wolff and Cara Delevingne.

## 9. Harry Potter

If you haven't seen all of these yet, what are you waiting for? And if you have seen them, why aren't you watching them again?

## 10. Alice through the Looking Glass

You've seen Alice in Wonderland before, and now it's time for you to watch Through the Looking Glass. They're both by the talented Lewis Carroll.

## 11. Pride and Prejudice and Zombies

This sounds like a ridiculous concept, but it's actually a pretty fun film to watch. See for yourself.

## 12. The Princess Diaries

Did you know that this was a book? You should re-watch the movie, and then check out the written version.

## 13. Percy Jackson and the Olympians

If you want to read a series, then you should start this one. There are plenty of books that'll keep you interested from start to finish.

## 14. Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them

If you're a fan of Harry Potter, then you need to watch this movie. Enough said.

## 15. The Fault in Our Stars

Here's another amazing movie based on a John Green novel. Make sure you have a tissue box nearby before you press the play button.

## 16. The 5th Wave

The beautiful Chloë Grace Moretz is in this movie. She makes it worth watching.

## 17. Vampire Academy

If you miss the days when vampires were all anyone talked about, then you should check out this movie. It'll give you your vampire fix.

You don't have to be a kid in order to enjoy these films. What's your favorite young adult novel? What about your favorite young adult movie?
