# These Are the Funniest Movies to Put a Smile on Your Face ...

Whether you've had a long day at work or you just need a bit of cheering up, there's nothing like having a good laugh at a light-hearted movie to let it all out! Keep reading for the Top 10 hilarious moves that will put a smile on your face in no time 😂

## 1. Bridesmaids

Competition between maid of honour and a dominating bridesmaid threatens to upend the life of an out-of-work pastry chef as they battle it out to prove who's the bride's best friend!

## 2. The Proposal

A pushy boss forces her young assistant to marry her in order to keep her visa status in the US and avoid deportation to Canada. They'll have to not only convince her deportation officer that they're totally in love but also his endearing family for a weird but wonderful weekend away with them in Alaska!

## 3. How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

A cocky advertising executive makes a bet that he can make a woman fall in love with him within 10 days. Similarly, she comes up with a similar plan for an article on classic ways to turn guys off - feeling confident she can drive him away within 10 days!

## 4. Little Miss Sunshine

A family of misfits, determined to get their young daughter into the finals of a beauty pageant, takes a cross-country trip in their VW bus. With everything going wrong along the way, the Hoover family struggle to keep it together in order to be at the beauty pageant on time for Olive to compete!

## 5. Miss Congeniality

In this hilarious comedy, an unattractive FBI agent must go undercover in the Miss United States beauty pageant to prevent an unknown group from bombing the event. This means she must undergo a major makeover not only in terms of her appearance but also her manners and attitude!

## 6. Pitch Perfect

Anna Kendrick stars in this snappy comedy as a rebellious student who becomes the unexpected secret weapon for her college's all-femaile a capella group! Who will take out the ultimate a capella competition that all groups have been preparing for?

## 7. Easy a

A clean-cut high school student relies on the school's rumour mill to advance her social and financial standing. She thinks she's well on the way to making herself more popular amongst her peers, but what she actually gets is far more than what she bargained for!

## 8. Dodgeball

A group of misfits enter a Las Vegas dodgeball tournament in order to pay off the debt they owe to save their cherished local gym. Can they win the $50,000 grand prize they need or will it be taken from fitness giant and major competitor 'Globo Gym'?

## 9. What Happens in Vegas

Two strangers who drunkenly got married in Vegas fight over a huge jackpot after having their annulment denied by a judge. Will both of them be able to put their differences aside and focus on making their marriage work - without the temptation of the huge amount of cash they won?

## 10. I Love You Man

A guy who doesn't have many friends struggles when he overhears his fiancé talking to her best friends about who will be his Best Man. Secretly, he decides to set out on a mission to find the perfect 'Best Man' for his wedding!
