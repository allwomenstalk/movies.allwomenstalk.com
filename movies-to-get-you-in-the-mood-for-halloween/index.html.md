# 22 Movies to Get You in the Mood for Halloween ...

It's the time of year when you're hunting for **movies to get you in the mood for Halloween.** Are you otherwise ready for Halloween? The trick & treating, the dressing up, and the pumpkin patches and corn mazes are all so much fun! But, let’s not forget the movies that get us excited for Halloween.

Whether you’re a kid or an adult Halloween will always be a favorite holiday. I love binge watching all things to get me in the mood for Halloween and I always love watching all the classic movies that I remember watching when I was younger.

Check out the following 22 **movies to get you in the mood for Halloween.**

## 1. Hocus Pocus

After three centuries, three sister witches Winifred, Sarah and Mary are resurrected in Salem, Massachusetts on Halloween night and now it is up to Allison and Max, and the help of an immortal cat Thackery Binx, to steal the witches book of spells to stop them from becoming immortal. This is one of the best **movies to get you in the mood for Halloween**.

## 2. Halloweentown

After discovering that she is a witch, Marnie Piper is transported to Halloweentown where ghosts, ghouls, werewolves and other creatures live.

## 3. Twitches

Alex and Camryn were separated at birth, but they soon run into each other and reunite on their 21st birthday. That's when they discover their unusual powers and that they have to fight the force of darkness to save their kingdom, Coventry.

## 4. Sleepy Hollow

Ichabod Crane was sent to Sleepy Hollow to investigate the decapitations of 3 people. This one is really spooky!

## 5. Evil Dead

5 friends are in a remote cabin in the woods when they find a book of the dead which leads them to unwillingly summoning a bunch of demons.

## 6. Mama

A young couple takes in their 2 nieces, but they soon begin to suspect that the supernatural spirit called Mama is attached to their family.

## 7. The Visit

Two siblings, Becca and Tyler, become frightened by their grandparents' strange behavior while visiting them on vacation.

## 8. Cult of Chucky

Chucky returns to terrorize Nica, meanwhile Chucky wants to settle scores with his old enemies with the help of his former wife. If you loved Child's Play, you'll love this one!

## 9. Hush

Maddie, a deaf writer who retreated into the woods to live a solitary life, must now fight for her life in silence when a killer appears at her window.

## 10. Stephen King's IT

Seven pre-teen outcasts fight an evil demon who poses as a child-killing clown. Both the older and the newer versions are scary, so take your pick!

## 11. Halloween

After murdering his sister on Halloween night 1963, Michael Myers escapes from a mental hospital.

## 12. Don't Breathe

Three friends think they'll get away with breaking into the house of a rich blind man, but they don't get what they expect.

## 13. Prom Night

Donna's prom is supposed to be the best night for her and her friends, but a sadistic killer from her past has other plans for them.

## 14. The Exorcist

A girl is possessed by a mysterious entity, so her mother seeks help of two priests. This one will keep you up all night!

## 15. The Conjuring

Ed and Lorraine Warren, both of whom are paranormal investigators, are summoned to help a family who is terrorized by a dark presence in their farm house.

## 16. Gerald's Game

While away in their remote lake cabin trying to spice things up in their marriage, Jessie has to fight to survive when her husband dies unexpectedly and she begins to hear voices and see strange visions.

## 17. A Nightmare on Elm Street

Nancy and a group of her friends are haunted in their dreams by a serial killer who goes by the name Freddy Kruger. You'll be afraid to go to sleep after watching this movie.

## 18. Split

Three girls are kidnapped by a man who is diagnosed with 23 distinct personalities. This one is eerie.

## 19. Annabelle

John Form had the perfect gift to give his expecting wife, a vintage doll, but shortly after, they started to experience supernatural occurrences that seem to be attached to the doll.

## 20. Friday the 13th

A group of camp counselors are stalked and murdered by an unknown assailant while trying to reopen Camp Crystal Lake which years before, was the site where a child drowned.

## 21. The Babysitter

An evening takes an unexpected turn for a young boy when he decides to sneak downstairs and spy on his babysitter.

## 22. Jigsaw

Bodies have been turning up all around the city and the victims had met a gruesome demise. As the investigation starts, evidence points to one person. That person is John Kramer, who is known as Jigsaw, and who has been dead for years.

I hope you all have a Happy Halloween.
