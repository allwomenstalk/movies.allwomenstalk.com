# Top 10 Car Movies to Impress Him with on Date Night ...

Is the guy you’re dating a fan of movies and fast cars? If he loves sharing special moments over a bucket of popcorn and he also loves the sound of a car engine revving, then combine two of his passions with a night in watching **car movies to impress him on date night**.

Stock up on the essential must-know movies involving racing cars, convertibles and high speed chases for ultimate girlfriend points! Here are the top 10 **best car car movies for date night** that will make him putty in your hands before you know it.

## 1. Fast & Furious

If he loves hot cars and hot women, it’s likely that he’ll be a big fan of The Fast and the Furious film series. And there are 9 films in total, plus 2 short films so there will definitely be more dates on the cards. This is one of my favorite **car movies to impress him on date night**!

## 2. Gone in 60 Seconds

This is a classic 70s movie about an insurance investigator who is also a professional car thief by night. The film ends with one of the best car chase scenes across Southern California from Long Beach to Carson.

## 3. The Italian Job

Here's one of the best retro car movies of all time! And it's a lso a superb comedy that boasts a seriously cool soundtrack. If you want to hit it off with a guaranteed iconic man flick, this is the one. There is also a 2003 remake if you’re planning on hooking a second date.

## 4. Drive

This is a great independent film for those indie boys who just can’t cope with mainstream. Starring Ryan Gosling and Carey Mulligan, the movie is about a stuntman and criminal getaway driver who land a professional driving contract.

## 5. Bullitt

The ultimate must-watch movie for Steve Mcqueen fans (the ‘King of Cool’). If you don’t have an appetite for noise, screeching tyres and noisy engines, this one isn’t for you. But petrolheads will definitely enjoy watching it.

## 6. Days of Thunder

Here's an awesome film starring Tom Cruise that will bring back fond memories of the 90s. A classic American sports action movie, this one is complete with a love story and a plot of good old fashioned, wholesomely, competitive racing.

## 7. Starsky & Hutch

The original was outstanding, but who doesn’t love the modern remake with Ben Stiller and Owen Wilson. Everyone’s favourite ‘Kings of Comedy’ will have you giggling away with your beau. Laughing releases endorphins to have you both in a great mood for a 10/10 date!

## 8. Taxi Driver

A neo-noir psychological thriller film directed by Martin Scorsese and starring a young Robert De Niro, Taxi Driver is sure to please. If he’s a serious film fan, this one’s a winner and he’ll be very impressed with your choice of Friday night flick.

## 9. Kill Bill

Soundtrack, check. Wicked Tarantino cinematography, check. Martial arts and kick ass fight scenes, check. And cool convertibles too? Plus if Uma Therman and Lucy Liu don’t empower you as a woman, then nothing will.

## 10. Back to the Future

Back to The Future is the childhood favourite of every 80s and 90s kid and it combines the fantasy of time travel with a futuristic race car in the form of the iconic DeLorean DMC-12 that we all know and love.
