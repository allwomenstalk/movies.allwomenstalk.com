7 Hilarious Comedies to Rent ...

Just Go with It • Paul Blart: Mall Cop • The Proposal • She's the Man • Step Brothers • More ...

By Jordin

If you've ever felt like you could just use a good laugh, then I know some hilarious comedies to rent that will help you out! We all have plenty of down days but thanks to funny comedies in movie stores or libraries, you can have a chance to unwind and get in a good laugh after a stressful day at work. I've made a list of a few of my absolute favorite comedies, so if you're standing in front of the movie selection entirely stumped, you don't have to be for long! This list is a mixture with some oldies but goodies, some new releases, and a few comedies that are only a few years old. I'm sure you will find something suited to your personal taste from my list! Almost everything I've chosen is family friendly, with the exception of one or two, but mostly it is up to your discretion. Please keep reading for my list of 7 hilarious comedies to rent!

1. Just Go with It

My husband and I went to watch this comedy while it was in theaters, and we laughed so hard! We loved it so much that we rented it again when it came out on DVD. It has a bit of crude humor in it, but don't all comedies? I'm sure this will be one comedy that you will love to see over and over again! Make sure you add this to your list of must-see's if you haven't seen it yet.

2. Paul Blart: Mall Cop

I remember when this movie came out. Paul Blart is utterly hilarious in this comedy! My family and I rented this DVD and we all laughed so hard that we had tears in our eyes! It's full of funny scenes and jokes that will keep you smiling throughout the movie. It has a touching ending and it's a very family friendly and hilarious comedy to rent.

3. The Proposal

Sandra Bullock and Ryan Reynolds absolutely shine in this hilarious comedy to rent! I've always been a fan of Sandra, and she makes another touchdown with this classic. Full of awkward situations that result in hilarious happenings and sarcastic jokes, you will be rooting for this comedy to keep going! I rolled on the floor in laughter simply because of some of the dry humor jokes!

4. She's the Man

This comedy could be classified as a "teeny-bopper" movie, but I think adults can enjoy it too. It does appeal to our teenagers, but my husband and father were both able to sit through this one and enjoy it. In fact, they both had some pretty good laughs! It has a few "guy" moments in it, and all the girls are pretty so you shouldn't have a hard time getting your man to sit through this one with you. Give it a try! I think you will be glad you did!

5. Step Brothers

I only just recently saw this comedy, although I know it's been out on DVD for a while now. I laughed and laughed and laughed and I know you will too! I wouldn't recommend this one for children to see because it does have a lot of crude humor and adult moments and language in the film. It's a great one for you and your friends to enjoy though! Definitely more of a guy movie, but so totally funny, you won't even be thinking about that!

6. Are We There Yet?

This is a great hilarious comedy to rent! Very family friendly and kids will love it! Full of laughs, you will want to see the sequel the minute you finish this one! The sequel, "Are We Done Yet?" came out on DVD shortly after the first one. In my opinion this is one that your whole family can enjoy over and over again!

7. The Court Jester

"The Court Jester" is an older movie and may not appeal to all of you out there, but I found it quite funny and entertaining! It has a different approach to humor, but it's still funny nonetheless. If you love kings and queens and knights and jesters, you may find this to be funny! If you check out Julian Smith on YouTube.com, you will find that he has done a remake of one of the scenes from this movie. It's very funny!

These are but a very, very few of the hilarious comedies to rent these days. There are so many out there it's just hard to pick, but these comedies are at the top of my list. What about you-do you have any favorite comedies? Please comment below, we would love to hear from you!
