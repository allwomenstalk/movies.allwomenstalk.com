# 10 Disney Guys That Should Be Real ...

Almost every girl grew up pining over Disney guys, wishing they were real. They sing, they dance, and they would do anything for the girl of their dreams. What girl wouldn’t want that? So here are my top 10 Disney guys that should be real. That way I wouldn’t feel so weird about obsessing over fictional characters, but with some of these great Disney guys, I just can't help it!

## 1. Aladdin

He may be a street rat, but he is also one of my favorite Disney men. Aladdin may not be a real prince, but he certain knows how to charm a lady. I’d take a date on a flying carpet over dinner and a movie any day.

## 2. John Smith

John Smith is on this list of Disney guys that should be real because he was always the adventurous one. His constant curiosity is so attractive. And he is not afraid to go against the grain for the woman he loves.

## 3. Prince Eric

Even though he is the only Disney prince to never sing, he stole my heart. He may have been a bit quick to jump into a relationship with Ariel (I mean, he didn’t even know her name), but I could look past it for those eyes.

## 4. Tarzan

Who needs an awesome car when you can swing on vines with this jungle cutie? Sure, he may have been raised by monkeys, but who really cares when he is that good looking. Besides, he's a teddy bear at heart!

## 5. The Beast

Any guy who gives a girl an entire library automatically makes my list of Disney guys. My favorite thing about the Beast was that there was so much more to him than looks. You wouldn’t see just how beautiful he was until you got to know him. And that is the best kind of love.

## 6. Milo

This may be my inner geek coming out, but Milo is just so adorable. He was not the typical Disney guy with dashing good looks, but he still came out on top as the hero of his story. Plus, who doesn't love an underdog?

## 7. Shang

The resident badass from the Chinese army, Shang was always one of my favorites. He is not afraid to go for a girl who has accomplished more than him. And he is the reason that anytime someone says, “let’s get down to business,” I have to add “to defeat the Huns.”

## 8. Peter Pan

You may have to look past the fact that he wears tights, but I definitely wish that he were real. Pete Pan embraces his inner child and sees the fun in everything. And we could all use a guy who makes us feel like a kid again.

## 9. Hercules

So the fact that he is a demigod may help, but Hercules definitely deserves to be on this list of Disney guys that should be real. He may have started out as being arrogant, but he would do anything for his girl. That definitely earns him the right to be a real life Disney prince.

## 10. Prince Charming

It might be that I also associate him with shoes, but I personally love Prince Charming. He is the classic prince that every little girl dreams about. And every girl deserves to have her own Prince Charming.

So there's my top 10 take on Disney guys that should be real. Most girls grow up watching Disney movies, so it makes sense that you spend a lot of time thinking about the princes as well as the princesses. Let me know what you think of these Disney guys. Why would you want these Disney guys to be real? If not, tell me your favorites!
