# 7 Awesome Movies to Watch This Summer ...

There are so many fantastic movies to watch this summer. There are movies that'll make you laugh, cry, cry from laughing, etc. I don't know about you, but I'm a huge fan of movies. I'm always at the theater watching the latest releases! These are just a couple that I'm super excited about. Check out some movies to watch this summer and then let me know what you're excited for!

## 1. 22 Jump Street

21 Jump Street had me laughing from start to finish. You can't even understand how excited I was to learn that Jonah Hill and Channing Tatum were teaming up again to reprise their roles and hit the big screen together! The movie was just recently released (June 13) and, without giving away any spoilers, it was AWESOME! I definitely recommend seeing it. This is just one of the awesome movies to watch this summer.

## 2. Sex Tape

I'm always excited for any movie that Jason Segel is in. I think he's such a fantastic comedic actor! He's hitting the big screen with Cameron Diaz and naturally, you'll be in for some serious laughs. The movie is due out July 18th, so mark the date! Just the trailer alone makes me so excited for the film.

## 3. Think like a Man Too

When 'Think Like a Man' came out, I loved everything about it. It was funny, thought-provoking, and let's be real, featured some of the most handsome men! The sequel is no different. It's set in Vegas so, of course, wild things are bound to happen. The movie came out on June 20th, so get your tickets!

## 4. The Fault in Our Stars

Where do I even start with this movie? It made me laugh. It made me cry. It made me believe in the power of love! It was just released (June 6th) and will undoubtedly be one of the biggest movies of the summer. Did you read the book? What'd you think of the movie?

## 5. The Purge: Anarchy

This movie looks super disturbing but still, I can't wait to watch it. Is that weird? It's a sequel of sorts to 'The Purge,' but looks a lot more terrifying. Call up all your friends to join you in the theaters, July 18th! P.S. don't be that person in the theater that can't help but scream furiously at the movie screen. LOL!

## 6. Jupiter Ascending

Channing Tatum and Mila Kunis together on screen? Yes please! The two teamed up for an action packed movie. They're both from different planets but have the same mission. To be honest, I think they're the only ones who'd make me want to watch a movie like this. The movie hits theaters on July 18th!

## 7. Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

I know there are tons of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles fans that have been waiting for this day for what seems like forever. Well, the wait is almost over. The Ninja Turtles will be returning to the big screen this summer, on August 8th. I'm super excited for this film - the trailer looks like it won't disappoint!

Well, there you have it! There are SO many awesome movies out this summer - these are just a few that I'm really excited about. If you've seen any of the movies on the list, which was your favorite? What movie are you really excited for that didn't make the list?
