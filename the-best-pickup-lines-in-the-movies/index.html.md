# The Best Pickup Lines in the Movies ...

**Pickup lines** - the timeless art of sparking a connection with just a few clever words. These little one-liners have become iconic in their own right, especially when delivered on the silver screen. As someone who's indulged in a fair share of romantic comedies and drama-filled blockbusters over the years, I can confidently say that some of the **best pickup lines** ever devised are sprinkled throughout movie history. So, grab your popcorn and cozy up. It's 2024, and we're diving deep into the charm and wit that Hollywood's finest have to offer.

From the smooth-talking casanovas to the adorkable nerds, movies have given us an array of characters who have mastered the art of the **pickup line**. Smooth-talking Romeo or awkward yet endearing - there's a line for every personality type. Remember when Will Smith's character from "Hitch" skillfully coached Kevin James on how to make a great first impression? That was a lesson for the ages!

We're not just reliving memorable moments; we're dissecting the brilliance behind these lines. Whether it's about mastering the [Classic One-Liners](#1) or decoding the [Unique and Quirky Lines](#3), our journey today is both educational and entertaining.

As I recount my personal favorite moments, I'm reminded of when I tried using one of these legendary lines myself. Picture this: a college party, dim lighting, and a half-awkward, half-popular guy trying his luck. I had the audacity to use "Are you a magician? Because whenever I look at you, everyone else disappears." Spoiler alert: it worked (but only because the person I used it on had a shared love for cheesy movie quotes). Ah, memories!

This year, **2024**, the relevance of these movie magic moments is as strong as ever. Pop culture references never go out of style, and knowing the perfect **pickup line** can elevate your game to new heights. Whether you're recounting Humphrey Bogart's timeless lines from classic films or the quirky deliveries by Zooey Deschanel in indie flicks, there's a treasure trove of material to draw from.

And let's be honest, who hasn't daydreamed about delivering a line with the same cool confidence as their favorite movie star? Whether you're looking to impress someone special or just want to add a dash of humor to your day, we've got you covered. Ready to delve into the [Science Behind the Lines](#5) or perhaps uncover some [Embarrassing Mishaps](#7)? Buckle up, because this guide to movie **pickup lines** promises a fun, laugh-out-loud adventure.

## 1. Hitch

In this 2005 movie about match making, Will Smith’s character utters the line "I couldn’t help but notice that you look a lot like my next girlfriend!”

## 2. Face/off

John Travolta has some great lines in this action drama, the best pick up phrase being “I hate to see you go, but I love to watch you leave!”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/face-off/id326830643)

## 3. Four Weddings and a Funeral

In typical Hugh Grant fashion, his pickup line is more of a pickup speech. Too long to capture in a single sentence, you’ll have to see the film to find out!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/four-weddings-and-a-funeral/id347378363)

## 4. Intolerable Cruelty

Only an actor like George Clooney could get away with uttering a line like “Your husband told me you were the most beautiful woman he’d ever seen, but he didn’t say anything about the most beautiful woman I’d ever seen.”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/intolerable-cruelty/id310064850)

## 5. Austin Powers: the Spy Who Shagged Me

In true 1960’s fashion, Mike Myers comes out with some groovy romantic lines, with one of his best being “let’s hop on the good foot and do the bad thing!”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/austin-powers-spy-who-shagged/id300490731)

## 6. Knocked up

Seth Rogan’s character knows how to inflate a girl’s ego with his simple line “you’re prettier than I am!”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/knocked-up-unrated/id271908762)

## 7. A Day at the Races

Groucho Marx mixes comedy with romance in this 1930s classic, uttering the classic line “marry me, and I’ll never look at another horse!”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/a-day-at-the-races/id289901109)

## 8. Anchorman: the Legend of Ron Burgundy

Ron Burgundy goes for self-confidence and celebrity status with his line “I don’t know how to put this, but I’m kind of a big deal!”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/anchorman-legend-ron-burgundy/id297465313)

## 9. Jerry Maguire

Let’s be real, who wouldn’t want 1996 era Tom Cruise to say this to them? “We live in a cynical world. A cynical world. And we work in a business of tough competitors. I love you. You…complete me.”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/jerry-maguire/id271252757)

## 10. The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy

Sam Rockwell really knows how to impress a geeky girl with his short and sweet line “you want to see my spaceship?”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/hitchhikers-guide-to-galaxy/id188702833)

## 11. Trees Lounge

Steve Buscemi’s character is definitely gunning for a double win with his phrase “If I win, I get to take you home. If you win, you can come home with me."

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/trees-lounge/id300613967)

## 12. The 40-Year-Old Virgin

Only a 40-year-old virgin would be able to get away with the awkward pick up line of “your hat has sequins!”

## 13. A Beautiful Mind

Like Hugh Grant, Russell Crowe’s character performs more of a pickup speech than a pickup line, but it’s super sweet and super effective!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/a-beautiful-mind/id324270794)

## 14. Top Gun

Meg Ryan takes the initiative in this 80s classic when she declares “take me to bed or lose me forever!”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/top-gun-1986/id291577463)

## 15. For Your Eyes Only

Roger Moore sure knows how to please a lady with his classic line “now put your clothes back on, and I’ll buy you an ice cream”.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/for-your-eyes-only/id561412956)

## 16. Casablanca

Is there any pick up line in cinema more famous than Humphrey Bogart’s classic “here’s looking at you, kid”?

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/casablanca/id282640192)

## 17. Napoleon Dynamite

Jon Heder’s character perfectly displays his awkward side with "I see you're drinking 1%. Is that ‘cos you think you're fat? 'Cos you're not. You could be drinking whole if you wanted to."

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/napoleon-dynamite/id270007388)

## 18. Bridget Jones’s Diary

Colin Firth gives a quintessentially British speech to Bridget to declare his love; equal parts awkward and romantic.

Buy at: _itunes.apple.com_

## 19. The Apartment

Jack Lemmon’s character shows his romantic side with "Ya know, I used to live like Robinson Crusoe; I mean, shipwrecked among 8 million people. And then one day I saw a footprint in the sand, and there you were."

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/the-apartment-1960/id303904676)

## 20. National Lampoon’s Van Wilder

Ryan Reynolds isn’t shy in NL’s Van Wilder with his funny line “are you stalking me? Because that would be super.”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/national-lampoons-van-wilder/id250421021)

## 21. Couples Retreat

Vince Vaughn brings out some classic cheese in Couple’s Retreat with his line “Someone has to call God and tell him that one of his angels is missing.”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/couples-retreat/id338798977)

## 22. Bram Stoker’s Dracula

Gary Oldman knows how to impress a girl in this stylish Dracula story when he says “I have crossed oceans of time to find you”.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/bram-stokers-dracula/id270006501)

## 23. The Hangover

Bradley Cooper isn’t shy at all in The Hangover with his line "ma'am, in the leopard print dress, you have an amazing rack."

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/the-hangover/id323818693)

## 24. Dirty Dancing

Patrick Swayze made us all fall in love with him when he stood up for his true love and declared “nobody puts Baby in the corner!”

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/dirty-dancing/id217821076)

## 25. Thelma and Louise

Brad Pitt may have been a baddie, but he sure had some smooth lines! "I may be an outlaw, darlin', but you're the one stealing my heart."
