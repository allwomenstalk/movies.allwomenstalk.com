# 7 Best Movie Kisses ...

Actors smooch on the big screen all the time, but only some of those kisses actually end up being the best movie kisses of all time. You know what kisses I’m talking about now! The ones that make your eyes water despite the fact that you’re not too much of a cry baby, take your breath away and even inspire your dreams. Yup, some movie kisses are indeed spectacular and that’s why I’ve decided to make my own list of best movie kisses of all time.

## 1. Jack and Kate (Titanic)

![Jack and Kate (Titanic)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tf/ya/eoi8mc7354c93876457d4306665054_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $27.97 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Best Movie Kisses? Well, we definitely have to mention this one! You’re free to say Titanic was waaay too cheesy for your taste but you simply can’t argue the “WOW” effect of this kiss! Three ounces of romance, a pitcher of tension, five spoons of radiating passion and a pinch of great scenery. Wondering what the hell am I talking about? Well, you can go ask James Cameron, I bet he was using this recipe to whip up that perfect, memorable kiss.

## 2. Josie and Sam (Never Been Kissed)

![Josie and Sam (Never Been Kissed)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nf/s7/xx7yebbz54c9387758835131441605_282x400.jpg)

**Price:** $8.48 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The tension and the “oh-he’s-not-going-to-show-up” thing definitely adds a lot to the kiss, but the fact that she has never been kissed is what makes the picture perfect. Just think of it – a brave girl, ready to tell her life story to the world and risk public embarrassment for the prospect of getting the guy she likes. Now, wasn’t that kiss well-deserved? I’d say yes!

## 3. Lady and Tramp (Lady and the Tramp)

![Lady and Tramp (Lady and the Tramp)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pn/cy/ao4tf9y354c93878831ac147345197_287x400.jpg)

**Price:** $48.95 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
They are not human,s but they have shared one of the best movie kisses ever, and that makes them great candidates for my list. Remember the cute, shy spaghetti kiss and the way Tramp gave Lady the last meatball? Aww! I could watch it over and over again and my reaction will always be the same. I doubt something so adorable ever happens in real life.

## 4. Scarlett and Rhett (Gone with the Wind)

![Scarlett and Rhett (Gone with the Wind)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/ug/s6o394g454c93879ca216968906445_347x400.jpg)

**Price:** $9.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This list simply wouldn’t be complete without at least one old movie and I’ve, quite logically, picked Gone With the Wind. It’s really hard to say which kiss was the best one as there are so many romantic, breath-taking ones, but let’s go with the popular opinion this time and just name the last one Scarlett gives to Rhett before he leaves to fight the war.

## 5. Peter and Mary Jane (Spiderman)

![Peter and Mary Jane (Spiderman)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nl/h9/hr2oko1e54c9387b2ade3195361794_483x398.jpg)

**Price:** $11.49 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A superhero upside-down kiss, you just got to love that! Definitely one of the best movie kisses of modern times and a really interesting one too. I mean, come on, a romantic scene featuring a superhero and his lady is always great and, since I’m not really into Spiderman, you can’t accuse me of not being objective.

## 6. Elizabeth and Mr. Darcy (Pride and Prejudice)

![Elizabeth and Mr. Darcy (Pride and Prejudice)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/zv/r6wpn46n54c9387c41208196626658_279x400.jpg)

**Price:** $10.49 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
One of my favorite novels, favorite movies and favorite TV shows. Regardless of the way this amazing and not-so-typical retro love story is presented, I’ve always ended up loving it and the kiss, these two stubborn and yet loveable characters finally end up sharing is just as great as the story itself.

## 7. Kelly and Suzie (Wild Things)

![Kelly and Suzie (Wild Things)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/co/f6/tbo04gb654c9387d5cc7b254958020_274x400.jpg)

**Price:** $7.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Two gorgeous babes exchanging a passionate kiss is the most popular male fantasy but it’s also a recipe for an on-screen kiss people will remember. Denise Richards and Neve Campbell have really brought their A game into that scene in the pool and, boy, those were two damn hot chicks.

But, enough about my favorite movie kisses! Tell me, which ones are truly the best movie kisses of all times? Would you agree with me completely, only partially or disagree, disagree and disagree?
