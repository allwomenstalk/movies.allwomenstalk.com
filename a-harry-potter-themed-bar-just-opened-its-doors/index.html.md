# A Harry Potter Themed Bar Just Opened Its Doors ...

According to [Popsugar.com](https://www.popsugar.com/smart-living/Lockhart-Harry-Potter-Bar-Toronto-38351866), there's a Harry Potter themed bar in Toronto! It's called The Lockhart, which is a reference to Professor Gilderoy Lockhart, who was a the Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher.

Of course, the references don't stop there! The entire menu is composed of aptly named brews, like "The Befuddlement Brew," "The Shacklebolt," and "Ludo's Debt." There are even trivia nights and Halloween parties scheduled, where you know customers will be dressing up as their favorite Hogwart's students.
