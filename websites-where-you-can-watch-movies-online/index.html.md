# 7 Websites Where You Can Watch Movies Online ...

There are many reasons why you’d want to watch movies online. You might have misplaced that DVD you’ve been saving for a quiet night in and so disrupts your plans, or you might need to show some friends a film you’ve been talking about for ages. The answer is to watch movies online. Sometimes you just want to watch a film instantly, but then get online and discover it takes you ages to find a website that lets you stream or download movies quickly. Here is a list of 7 easy to access Websites Where You Can Watch Movies Online at your convenience.

## 1. Amazon Instant Video

![Amazon Instant Video](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/05/1_amazon-instant-video_600x303.jpg)

Here you can instantly watch popular movies and TV shows, and at no extra cost with Amazon Prime. You have unlimited streaming and watch movies online on your computer or on your TV with a connecting streaming device. It costs $3.99 for a 48 hour rental, or is at no additional cost if you have an Amazon Prime account (at $79 a year).

## 2. Apple TV

![Apple TV](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/05/2_apple-tv_600x304.jpg)

The Apple TV device enables you to stream thousands of movies and TV shows straight to your TV. You can watch movies online as soon as they are released on DVD and there’s a great choice. All you need is the device, iTunes and a computer with recent operating systems, such as Mac OS X or Windows 7 to Windows XP Home. The device costs $99, then its 99c for TV rentals and $2.99 for movies.

## 3. Blockbuster

![Blockbuster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/05/3_blockbuster_600x303.jpg)

You can choose from more than a whopping 100,000 titles, and Blockbuster can deliver your movie choices to your door. It costs $4.99 for a 7-day rental. But, if you want to watch movies online, you can go to Blockbuster On Demand and get movies that range in price between FREE and $3.99, depending on the movie.

## 4. Hulu

![Hulu](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/05/4_hulu_600x304.jpg)

Hulu is usually known for being able to stream hundreds of TV shows online. But you can also stream and hundreds movies online for free. Be warned though, their database of movies is rather limited. However, you can purchase Hulu Plus membership for $7.99 a month and access even more movies.

## 5. MoviesDataCenter

![MoviesDataCenter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/05/5_moviesdatacenter_600x412.jpg)

This is a large online movie community which enables you to watch movies online instantly and for free. It has over 35,000 titles, including the latest blockbusters. You are recommended to register for a free account to start watching. As the films are streamed online at no cost, use the website at your own risk.

## 6. Vudu

![Vudu](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/05/6_vudu_600x304.jpg)

With Vudu you can rent a movie for $2 for 2 nights. There is no monthly subscription as you just pay as you go. They have HD movies on demand so you can watch movies in high quality instantly. New films are available the day they are released on DVD, and you can stream them to your HD TVs, Blu-ray players, computer, iPad or even PlayStation 3. This is one of the cheapest options available, and the most flexible.

## 7. Wow-mov.com

![Wow-mov.com](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/movies/2012/05/7_wow-mov-com_600x304.jpg)

Here you can download movies straight to your computer after payment. It is recommended to download software, such as FlashGet, to speed up your downloading time. The website has over 10,000 titles available, old and new. Pricing is stated on the website’s updated catalogue.

So there are plenty of options available to you if you want to watch movies online. They are cheap and some extremely useful with their instant service. With the internet becoming faster and more readily available, there are always more options to choose from. Those are just 7 Websites Where You Can Watch Movies Online – perhaps you know of more? Which do you think is the best?
