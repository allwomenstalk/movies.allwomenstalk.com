# 9 Amazing Quotes from Movies That I Can Relate to ...

Quotes from Movies can be relatable, interesting and funny! Personally, I love quotes from movies, especially anything with Jason Segel. With that being said, below are my top quotes from movies that I can relate to and that actually mean something to me.

## 1. The Sandlot

“You're killin' me, Smalls.” The Sandlot is one of those movies that I love. It brings me right back to my childhood and this is one of the quotes from movies that everyone seems to know. This quote works for everything! Whether I am frustrated with someone or just poking fun, it’s honestly an awesome quote! What’s your favorite quote from The Sandlot?

## 2. Harry Potter

"It takes a great deal of bravery to stand up to your enemies, but a great deal more to stand up to your friends." Harry Potter is another favorite and it doesn’t even matter what movie I’m watching, I can find quotes in each one. This quote though, it’s so true. Dumbledore was so right when he said these words to Neville after he stood up to his pals.

## 3. Easy a

“Just once, I want my life to be an 80s movie. But no, no, John Hughes did not direct my life.” Easy A is easily one of the funniest movies I’ve ever seen. Emma Stone is my favorite actresses now and it’s all because of this movie. This quote though? It’s so relatable! It’s one of the few quotes from movies that works for almost everyone – after all, wouldn’t you like your life to be an 80s movie?

## 4. Brokeback Mountain

"Tell you what. The truth is... sometimes I miss you so much I can hardly stand it." Brokeback Mountain made me bawl. Plain and simple, it is the saddest movie I have ever seen. With that being said, this is one of the quotes from movies that we can all relate to right?

## 5. Mean Girls

“I have a fifth sense, it's like I have ESPN or something!” We’ve all had those moments where we aren’t the smartest bulb in the bunch right? Well, this is one of those quotes that we can use when we aren’t all that clever. Also, Mean Girls is by far the best movie for one-liners!

## 6. I Love You, Man

“Why does everything I do sound like a leprechaun?” Paul Rudd. Jason Segel. Man, just put those two in any movie and I swear that you can make it great, but this movie? It’s all about the bromance! It’s funny, quirky and totally sweet! So, when you do accents, do they all sound like a leprechaun?

## 7. Forgetting Sarah Marshall

“You're like one of those women from Flavor of Love. 'I'm gonna kill you.'” This is another Paul Rudd and Jason Segel movie that I absolutely love! It’s got so many good quotes, but this one is one of my favorite. First off, I love trashy reality TV shows like Flavor of Love and second off, who doesn’t know women like that?

## 8. All over the Guy

“Oh my God, I'm gonna go to the bathroom now and hope to God that I can overdose on Tums, or Maalox, or pine-scented Glade.” Have you ever had a wicked embarrassing moment that you wish you could hide from? Well, that’s what this quote is all about! Eli, the lovely main character in this romantic comedy has an embarrassing call from his mother and this was what he said. I love this quote and I can so relate to it!

## 9. The Hangover

"Next week's no good for me. The Jonas Brothers are in town." Sarcasm. I friggin’ love sarcasm and that’s what this quote is all about. Does one of your friends who always cancels on you now want to make plans? You can’t, ‘cause the Jonas Brothers are in town!

Finding the right quotes from movies that work in all situations can be fun! These are all movies that mean something to me and the quotes from movies are so relatable for me. So ladies, fuss up, what movie quotes do you love? Give it up!
