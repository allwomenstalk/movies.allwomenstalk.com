# 8 Fabulously Awesome Ways to Get Ready for Breaking Dawn Part 1 ...

**Breaking Dawn Part 1** has probably been one of the most anticipated movies of the year. The good news? On November 18th, 2011, it finally arrived! Twilight fans need to remember the date for posterity: **Breaking Dawn Part 1** hit the theaters. Those of us who are absolute stalkers of the Twilight Series have patiently awaited this moment since Eclipse entered theaters. Some people still haven't gotten to see it, while super fans are gearing up to see it again and again. No matter which category you fall into, here are 8 ways to get ready for **Breaking Dawn Part 1**.

## 1. Read the Book

Most true Twilight fans have read the entire seires. However, there are still a few who proclaim to be the biggest fans ever that have yet to take on the task of reading the series. I suggest you get started now. Even if you can’t finish before you can't stand it anymore and rush out to see Breaking Dawn Part 1, at least you got a start.

## 2. Buy Your Tickets

Did you see the midnight premier of Breaking Dawn? I sure did! No matter when you go see the movie, you need to get your tickets ahead of time, because it definitely is that popular. Head to your local box office or online to purchase your tickets beforehand. Lines are sure to be long with crazed Twilight fans. Beat the crowds and avoid the headache with advance tickets.

## 3. Glitter up!

I plan to show my support for Edward and my eagerness to see him shirtless with by covering my skin in body glitter. When Edward Cullen sparkles in Breaking Dawn Part 1, I want to sparkle too. Even better, talk your boyfriend into wearing a little body glitter too or just sneak in a nice big full body hug after you’ve covered yourself in glitter. Then you can have your very own sparkly “Edward.”

## 4. Plan Your Outfit

Unfortunately body glitter won’t be enough. The movie theater is a public place and clothes are required to see the movie. The question is, what are you wearing? Your favorite Team Edward or Team Jake clothing might work. I’ve seen some awesome Breaking Dawn shirts and other apparel in stores too.

## 5. Buy the Ring

Has there ever been a more perfect occasion to wear a replica of Bella Swan’s engagement ring since the starting night of Eclipse? No! If you already have the ring, wear it to see Breaking Dawn. Wear it proud! If you don’t have it, you are going to need. Every Twilight Series stalker needs her own ring.

## 6. Mentally Prepare Yourself

We watched the sexual tension and excitement build up between Bella and Edward through the last 3 movies. Finally in Breaking Dawn Part 1, all their passion and sexual frustrationwill be released, leaving a broken headboard and a feathery mess. We know it’s coming and can’t wait to see how it all plays out.

## 7. Make Plans

Who will you see Breaking Dawn with? Now is the time to get your best girlfriends together and work out the details. Where are you meeting, what time are you getting there, do you want to have dinner first? See? There is just so much to find out. Don’t wait until the last minute, make your plans now!

## 8. Be Patient

You’ve already waited this long. Don’t mess it up now when Breaking Dawn Part 1 is finally in theaters. Take a deep breath. Count to 10 and wait. Just wait. As the same goes, “Good things come to those who wait,” and I am sure this movie is one of those things.

**Breaking Dawn Part 1** did amazingly not just on its opening weekend, but on its follow up, too! Stalkers, I don’t know about you, but I am so excited to see **Breaking Dawn Part 1 **again and again! It has been on my mind constantly. If you have anything I forgot off my list of 8 Ways to Get Ready for **Breaking Dawn Part 1**, let me know.
