# 7 Guilty Pleasure Reality Shows We Cant Resist ...

No matter how repulsive and distasteful we might think reality TV can be, we all watch it and religiously enjoy our guilty pleasure reality shows. It’s almost like fast food, we know it’s bad for us but yet we are completely addicted! The crux of the popularity of reality TV is that there is an appeal to the viewers, who get the chance to relate to the stars and observe them as they face their struggles. It’s entertaining! So here are some of the most addicting guilty pleasure reality shows that we can’t get enough of.

## 1. Keeping up with the Kardashians

Sunday nights are never the same without the Kardashians! Their family dynamic is unique and fun and the episodes are never boring. They have everything that viewers want to see: glamorous life, drama, and scandals, but through it all they always manage come out strong with a tight family bond! There is no denying that this family have one of the most interesting lives, which is the reason why it’s one of the top guilty pleasure reality shows with some of the highest reality TV ratings.

## 2. Big Brother

One of my favorite things about summer is getting the chance to enjoy a brand new season of Big Brother.There is nothing more exciting than watching a group of housemates compete for half a million dollars for the entire summer through a series of mental and physical challenges. Despite the fact that the recent season of Big Brother turned out to be a little disappointing, it still remains one of my favorite reality shows. Certain players are astonishing and watching them mastermind their game moves is more than satisfying.

## 3. America’s Next Top Model

Some people may think that ANTM is a very shallow reality show that advocates beauty in the unhealthy sort of way, but it’s no secret that the show has given everyone - the short and the plus size models - the equal opportunity to compete. Even if you don’t agree, reality shows should never be taken seriously. They are just shows after all and you should just have fun with them. Nevertheless, America’s Next Top Model is an addicting show worth keeping up with.

## 4. The Voice

The best thing about reality TV is that we get the chance to see real talent and observe normal people go from ordinary life to stardom. The Voice especially has a wonderful twist which coerces the judges to solely focus on the talent and not let appearance cloud their judgments. It’s definitely refreshing to watch.

## 5. Cake Boss

To be honest I would never purposely tune in to watch Cake Boss, but hey if I stumble upon it while watching TV I would probably keep watching it. There is something really interesting to see beautiful cakes come to life starting from scratch. The whole show is family friendly and it’s actually really entertaining for a food show.

## 6. So You Think You Can Dance

So You Think You Can Dance definitely makes me feel self-conscious about having two left feet, but nevertheless it’s one of my favorite reality shows. On the show, dancers compete through a series of live performances for a chance to become the last one standing. The show is filled with talented dancers accompanied by eminent personalities that everyone can enjoy.

## 7. The X Factor

One of the most exciting parts of the X Factor is the audition period, during which you can get the chance to see some of the most humorous performances and discover some ridiculously shocking talent. It’s a fun show to follow and it allows talented singers with a lot of potential to actually make it in the singing business.

Reality TV is one of the best forms of entertainment because viewers tend to become emotionally invested in them. What are some of your favorite reality TV shows? Admit to your guilty pleasures!
