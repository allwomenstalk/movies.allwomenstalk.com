# Binge Watch These 7 Movies over Your Winter Break ...

You worked hard all semester, now your brain needs a break! It’s time to crash on the couch with a bowl of popcorn and binge-watch the latest and greatest films on Netflix! But you’ve been living with your nose in a book all semester, how will you know what to watch? Don’t worry, here’s your list:

## 1. Stuck in Love

A lot can happen in one year, more than you could believe! Follow this family's journey from Thanksgiving of one year to Thanksgiving the next and see how the characters transform. It's a beautiful story about love- but definitely not a love story.

## 2. Marie’s Story

This movie is a beautiful story of triumph. If Marie can overcome all of the obstacles in her life, you can definitely conquer next semester! Plus, it's French, so when you go back to school you can brag to your friends about how you're cultured and watch French films. Warning: this movie might make you cry!

## 3. Not Another Happy Ending

This is definitely one of my favorite movies! But it's a hidden gem on Netflix, so you might not have seen it. It's quirky, funny, and uplifting. It might even become your new favorite!

## 4. Silver Lining’s Playbook

This movie was a classic the day it came out! If you still haven't seen it, make sure you add it to your list. Even if you've already seen it, it never hurts to see this film another time! Jennifer Lawrence and Bradley Cooper? You really can't go wrong!

## 5. Chocolat

While you're sipping hot chocolate by the fire, you should watch Chocolat. It's a fantasy for children and adults alike, as well as those of us who live in between both! It teaches us that anything can be cured by either chocolate or love-or both!

## 6. Love Actually

This movie is a holiday classic! It has a storyline in it for everyone, which is how I know you'll enjoy it. Plus, it will inspire you to take risks and make leaps of faith, a great mindset to have going into the new year.

## 7. Kate and Leopold

He’s handsome, a gentleman, classy, what could go wrong? Oh right, he's from the year 1876. But 2003 is where he'll find true love! Make sure you watch this modern-day classic love story over winter break.

Winter break is the perfect time to veg out and watch insane amounts of Netflix! What movies are you adding to your list?
