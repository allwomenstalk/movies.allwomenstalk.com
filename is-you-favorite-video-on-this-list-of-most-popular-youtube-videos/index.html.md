# Is You Favorite Video on This List of Most Popular YouTube Videos?

Ever wonder what the most popular YouTube videos are? Well, you can stop wondering, because we've got all the deets. Here are the 10 most viewed YouTube videos of all time!

## 1. Psy - Gangnam Style

It has been over 2 years and Psy's Gangnam style is still going strong with 2,163,825,098 views when this post was written. That's over 2.15 billion views! That's almost as many views as the number of people who have internet access! In fact, Google had never anticipated that a video would exceed 2 billion views and had to upgrade to a 64-bit integer encoding from a 32-bit one. For the record Kim K, THIS is how you break the internet!

## 2. Justin Bieber - Baby

Beliebers, rejoice! Justin Bieber's "Baby" is the second most popular video on YouTube with 1.12 billion views. Sadly, it has many more thumbs down than thumbs up BUT I guess there is no such thing as bad publicity.

## 3. Charlie Bit My Finger - Again!

Love this video! Can't stop watching it! With over 805 million views, this is the third most popular Youtube video of all time! Aww.

## 4. Jennifer Lopez - on the Floor

I can't believe this video has 796 million views! Sure it's catchy but I had no idea JLo had so many fans.

## 5. LMFAO - Party Rock Anthem

No party is complete without this song playing at least once. Something about it just makes you want to get up and dance....even if you're not the dancing kind. With over 783 million views, this is the fifth most viewed Youtube video of all time!

## 6. Shakira- Waka Waka

Shakira's Waka Waka is synonymous with the 2010 Fifa World Cup. With over 779 million views, this catchy song still seems to be wildly popular!

## 7. Eminem - Love the Way You Lie

With over 777 million views, this song perfectly captures the essence of every dysfunctional relationship you've ever been in...

## 8. Psy - Gentleman

"Gentleman" doesn't quite have the same charm as "Gangnam style", nor is it remotely as popular at 765 million views, but you've got to admire the fact that he's the only person to be featured twice on this list!

## 9. Miley Cyrus - Wrecking Ball

This controversial video has about 729 million views! I don't hate the song, but as far as the video is concerned, I much prefer the spoof versions.

## 10. Katy Perry - Roar

Katy Perry can do no wrong. With over 721 million views, I'm not the only one who thinks so. Now, I just have to spend the next 4 odd minutes singing along. I just HAVE to!

So there you go ladies, the ten most popular Youtube videos of all time! Which videos do you watch over and over?
