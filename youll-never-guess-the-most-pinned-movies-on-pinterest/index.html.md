# Youll Never Guess the 20 Most Pinned Movies on Pinterest ...

Quotes, scene shots, advertising posters, reviews, tidbits about cast members, just because it’s loved – all reasons a movie might get pinned to Pinterest. Pinterest introduced its movie pins category in 2013 so there’s a couple of years’ worth of good data but what are the most pinned movies?

## 1. Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince

Of all the Harry Potter franchise movies, it’s the sixth installment that receives the most love on Pinterest. There are a few good reasons for this, but shh, no spoilers!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/harry-potter-half-blood-prince/id326568786)

## 2. Deadpool

Deadpool took the world by storm just last month, and this foul mouthed comic book hero is making big waves on Pinterest! Already!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/deadpool/id1078111961)

## 3. Gone Girl

Gone Girl became something of a phenomenon with the release of the original book and the subsequent hit movie. Pinterest users showed their love for it in a big way.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/gone-girl/id917874539)

## 4. The Shining

Arguably one of the most terrifying films of all time, The Shining is so much more than just a cult classic. One of Jack Nicholson’s very best performances.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/the-shining/id522395870)

## 5. A Clockwork Orange

A strong cult classic that has millions of avid fans all over the world. Pinterest is just an indicator of who beloved Stanley Kubrick’s weird and wonderful movie is.

Buy at:[itunes.apple.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/a-clockwork-orange/id422094672)

## 6. Sleepless in Seattle

A classic slice of 1990s romantic comedy starring two of the decade’s biggest stars, Meg Ryan and Tom Hanks.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/sleepless-in-seattle/id271817742)

## 7. Disturbia

Perhaps a bit of a surprise as Disturbia wasn’t the best movie, but something about the tense thriller obviously hit a nerve with Pinterest users.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/disturbia/id271408025)

## 8. The Martian

Another recent movie starring Matt Damon as an astronaut who gets left behind on Mars. Not seen this yet – must remedy that.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/7oF414)

## 9. The Sound of Music

An all time musical classic that has just as many fans today as it did when it first became a huge hit. Julie Andrews will always be our favorite nun/nanny/captain’s wife!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/QnD9M4)

## 10. Inception

It is surprising just how popular Inception became when it was released, because I think only about 5 percent of us actually understood the confusing movie!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/inception/id400763833)

## 11. Legally Blonde

Legally Blonde is the reason that Reese Witherspoon found a permanent place in all of our hearts. Who doesn’t love a story about a girl surpassing all expectations and kicking ass?

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/legally-blonde/id261128362)

## 12. Psycho

There is a sub section on Pinterest that has a lot of love for classic cinema, and Alfred Hitchcock’s Psycho is the most pinned of them all.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/psycho-1960/id284289301)

## 13. The Godfather

Quite rightly, the king of all gangster movies holds a proud place on the list of most pinned movies. How could it not be in the list with such an amazing cast?

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/godfather-coppola-restoration/id285494571)

## 14. Mad Max: Fury Road

The Mad Max universe came back to us in a huge way in 2015, and many other Pinterest users agree that it was one of the most insane and groundbreaking movies of the last decade! Its wins in the techie categories at the 2016 Oscars speaks volumes.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/mad-max-fury-road/id990549112)

## 15. Planes, Trains and Automobiles

There is clearly still a lot of online love for John Hughes’ classic tale of holiday travel gone so very wrong!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/planes-trains-automobiles/id314510037)

## 16. Chef

It’s no surprise that Chef is on this list as it incorporates two of Pinterest’s most popular things: great movies and food porn!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/chef/id877741061)

## 17. Blood Diamond

Perhaps a surprise inclusion because it isn’t the first Leo Dicaprio movie to come to mind, but he gives a customary brilliant performances and the film is great.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/blood-diamond/id293311376)

## 18. Star Wars: the Force Awakens

Does this one really need an explanation? Let’s just say that it makes sense for the most anticipated movie in history to be pretty popular in the world of Pinterest!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/AMdn37)

## 19. Jurassic World

Everybody celebrated when the latest Jurassic universe movie came out, and thank god it was actually good! We had been disappointed with Jurassic sequels before!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/jurassic-world/id973589977)

## 20. Up

Some people argue about whether Up is Pixar’s best movie, but something that cannot be argued is that it packed an emotional punch and it’s super popular on Pinterest as a result!

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://itunes.apple.com/us/movie/up/id322447599)
