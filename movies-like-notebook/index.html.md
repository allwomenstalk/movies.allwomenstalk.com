# Movies like the Notebook Hardcore Romantics Will Fall in Love with ...

[The Notebook](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0332280/) is no doubt one of the best romantic movies of all time. If you want to watch more movies like that, the movies on this list are not going to disappoint. They will make you wonder why we don’t make big romantic gestures anymore; dancing in the middle of the street, passionate kissing in the rain, building a big beautiful house, hanging from a Ferris wheel, although the last one is probably not a good idea…This list is a compilation of romantic movies like the Notebook that will make you all mushy inside…

## 1. Me before You (2016)

This movie stars Emilia Clarke and Sam Claflin where Clarke plays a girl who is hired for a job involving taking care of a recently paralyzed man (Claflin) but soon they form a beautiful romantic bond. Both actors have acted really well in this movie. It is an absolute must watch for romantic drama fans.

## 2. Letters to Juliet (2010)

Sophie, an aspiring writer goes to Italy with her fiancé on a pre-honeymoon where she meets the ‘secretaries of Juliet’ and ends up taking a journey to reconnect a woman with her lost love. This is such a sweet movie and shot in beautiful Italy. It will definitely want you to take a romantic trip with your BAE.

## 3. Titanic (1997)

It is impossible to complete a list of romantic movies without mentioning this one! Two people from different worlds fall in love with each other while on a ship. Most of us know how this movie ends but it still makes you cry every time you watch it.

## 4. The Fault in Our Stars (2014)

This movie is based on one of his most popular novels by John Green. It’s about two teenage cancer patients who become very close and go on a journey to Amsterdam to fulfill a wish. I read the book when it came out and absolutely loved it, and the movie certainly does it justice.

## 5. The Last Song (2010)

A girl at odds with her father is sent to live with him for the summer where she strikes up a romance with one of the locals and reconnects with her father with the help of music. The movie stars Miley Cyrus, and Liam Hemsworth who are a real-life married couple now. Did you know they actually met on the set of this movie? How romantic is that!

## 6. A Walk to Remember (2002)

A story about two teens in school who run in very different social circles but unexpectedly become friends, and slowly that friendship turns into a beautiful romantic relationship. This is such a cute movie; it will definitely win your heart.

## 7. The Vow (2012)

This is a true story about a husband and a wife. Rachel McAdams (wife) wakes up from a coma after a car accident losing most of her memory. She forgets that she is married and has a husband (Channing Tatum) but he is not going to let her forget him that easily. He works to win her heart again.

## 8. The Best of Me (2014)

Two high school sweethearts return back to their hometown after 20 years and get to know each other again. A movie based on the bestselling novel by Nicholas Sparks.

## 9. Dear John (2010)

A romantic drama Starring Channing Tatum and Amanda Seyfried. Tatum is a soldier on leave who falls in love with Seyfried, a college student on her spring vacation. When John (Tatum) goes back to work, they stay in touch by sending each other love letters.

## 10. P.S. I Love You (2007)

A widow finds out that her late husband has left letters for her that will change her life. It’s a beautiful story about a woman figuring out what she wants to do in life. I find this movie super inspirational. We all experience tragedy in our lives at some point, but we become stronger when we refuse to give up and fight back.

Whichever movie you decide to watch, make sure to grab loads of tissues because these are all beautiful romantic movies that will bring tears to your eyes...
