# 7 of the Best Boy Meets World Quotes ...

If there's one thing we took away from the Cory Matthews era, it's the famous Boy Meets World quotes. There are so many to choose from! Every episode is hilarious and I think this show is one of the most "quotable" shows I've seen. Boy Meets World quotes are the best because they always leave an impression on you. No matter if it's a funny or serious quote, it always resonates with you. Check out some of my favorite quotes from the show. Keep in mind, these are just seven!

## 1. Topanga Lawrence

![Topanga Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/25023233_3637_500x280.jpg)

Who didn't love Topanga? She had perfect hair, an adorable smile, and she definitely had the brains. Even in the early years of the show, Topanga provided many of my favorite Boy Meets World quotes. My favorite one has to be when she's talking to Cory in the school hallway. She says to him, "I do my thing and you do your thing. You are you and I am I. And if in the end, we end up together, it's beautiful." Preach, Topanga! Those are some wise words.

## 2. Shawn Hunter

![Shawn Hunter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/25023232_5784_467x342.jpg)

Shawn Hunter was my childhood crush. He was hilarious, loyal, and seemed like a blast to be around. Topanga asks Shawn, "Have you ever fallen in love?" Shawn replies with, "Five times a day." This quote isn't deep or anything, but it does teach you something. It teaches you to keep your heart open. Fall in love with as many things as possible, love everything. After all, it's better to have loved and lost than to not have loved at all, right? Shawn was definitely on to something here.

## 3. Cory Matthews

![Cory Matthews](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/25023232_3832_500x382.jpg)

This quote is great because it's so relatable. When does anyone understand anything about their life? Cory managed to get it together as he grew older, so maybe we should rewatch the series and find out how he did it! In this episode, Cory sits down for a tea party with his little sister and says, "I don't understand anything about my entire life." Well said, Matthews.

## 4. Mr. Feeny

![Mr. Feeny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/25023232_9691_500x375.jpg)

Words have never been more true. Whenever anyone in the show was feeling down or lost, Feeny was there to help guide them back to the right track. In this episode, he tells Shawn, "You don't have to be blood to be family." It's a great quote because he's 100% true. We all have that best friend who we consider family. It's all about the people who stick with you because they want to, not because they think they have to.

## 5. Eric Matthews

![Eric Matthews](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/25023232_8346_500x374.jpg)

There's been countless times where I've used this quote on my sister. Whenever she's complaining about something, I pull an Eric, and yell out, "Life's tough. Get a helmet." It's a great quote because it doesn't sugarcoat anything. Life's hard and to get through it, we need to be hard too. We need to surround ourselves with good people and hope that it's enough. Wise words, Eric.

## 6. Cory Matthews

![Cory Matthews](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/25023232_4421_500x266.jpg)

How precious is this? Cory and Shawn will always be my favorite bromance. I love this quote because it's relatable to us all. If you love someone, hug them. Let them know that they're not alone in whatever it is that they might be going through.

## 7. Mr. Feeny

![Mr. Feeny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/25023233_9821_500x399.jpg)

This is way too true. I'm telling you, Mr. Feeny always said the wisest things. The quote reads, "Unfortunately we live in a society where they tell us we have to look a certain way so we're all under pressure to live up to unrealistic expectations." Can you relate? I know I can.

There you have it! These are seven of the best Boy Meets World quotes. There are so many more out there, but these are my absolute faves. Which one was your favorite?
