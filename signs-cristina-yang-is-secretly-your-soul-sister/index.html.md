# 17 Signs Cristina Yang is Secretly Your Soul Sister ...

Cristina Yang is my soul sister. She is my favorite character in all of TV history and I wish I could grow up to be exactly like her. I don't care if she's a fictional character, either. Sandra Oh made her real. If I could be friends with Sandra Oh, I would probably promptly ruin our friendship by asking if we could have Cristina Yang Hour every single day. If Yang is your soul sister, too, you will feel all the feels by the end of this. Ready, set, DANCE IT OUT!

## 1. She Knows What's Really Important

![She Knows What's Really Important](https://img.allw.mn/content/ra/iz/o31myy55570e7b5c6c866474928036_500x278.gif)

This is probably my favorite Yang quote ever. She delivers so much sass when she says it, too – my sister knows she is beyond smart. She is a genius. She is a superstar.

## 2. When She Lets Go, She Lets Go

![When She Lets Go, She Lets Go](https://img.allw.mn/content/ao/bp/o7c5rar1570e7b5d121ca447015058_500x326.gif)

I bet you do the same thing, don't you? You hold all your stuff together for so long, and then everything comes out in a torrent.

## 3. She's Bluntly Honest

![She's Bluntly Honest](https://img.allw.mn/content/za/wb/odm6pmxu570e7b60004f7934925584_500x220.gif)

Just like you, she's not afraid to ask the hard questions. Or the inappropriate ones.

## 4. Just Way Too Honest

![Just Way Too Honest](https://img.allw.mn/content/x7/ko/ey3czfuu570e7b626c710156542790_500x271.gif)

But it's charming, right? When you're too honest? Whatever, girls like us just don't have time for the BS.

## 5. She's Always Thinking, All the Time

![She's Always Thinking, All the Time](https://img.allw.mn/content/s9/ow/bfgj4052570e7b5da70c5156852084_500x270.gif)

You know how that feels, right? I always feel incapable of turning off my mind. I need a McDreamy to take me fishing.

## 6. She Doesn't Fit into Boxes

![She Doesn't Fit into Boxes](https://img.allw.mn/content/d5/wq/wwbevbh1570e7b5d7193d845438975_500x220.gif)

You don't, either. Trying to fit a butterfly into a box will just suffocate it. You'll feel freer without it – just like Cristina!

![She Has No Time for Foolishness](https://img.allw.mn/content/yy/i4/l7ibd5yc570e7b5f22ed3348702138_473x265.gif)
