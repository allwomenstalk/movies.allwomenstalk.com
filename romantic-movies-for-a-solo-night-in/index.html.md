# 7 Romantic Movies for a Solo Night in ...

After a long day’s work sometimes, we just want to sit and relax and watch a good movie alone. That's why you need this list of **romantic movies for a solo night in**. Sometimes we want to have a cozy night in curled up in a blanket. What better way to do that with a good romance or romantic comedy film? Here are seven great movies to relax and watch! These films are in no order, but are definitely some of my favorite **romantic movies for a solo night in**.

## 1. The Note Book (2004)

A poor yet passionate young man falls in love with a rich young woman. This is a film infused with romance, comedy and passion, and is one of the best **romantic movies for a solo night in**.

## 2. The Proposal (2009)

A pushy boss forces her assistance to marry her to keep her visa status in the U.S. This film has comedy and romance with a star-studded cast.

## 3. Where the Heart is (2000)

A pregnant 17 year-old rebuilds her life after being abandoned by her boyfriend at a Walmart in Oklahoma.

## 4. Definitely, Maybe (2008)

A political consultant tries to explain his impending divorce and past relationships to his 11-year-old daughter. This film is sprinkled with humor and romance.

## 5. Stardust (2007)

A young man makes a promise to his beloved that he’ll retrieve a fallen star by taking on an adventure in a magical land that borders their village. This film is filled with fantasy, magic and romance.

## 6. About Time (2013)

Tim discovers he can travel in time and changes what happens and has happened in his own life. He decides to make his life better by getting a girlfriend. This film has comedy and romance.

## 7. Titanic (1992)

A seventeen-year-old aristocrat falls in love with a kind but poor artist aboard the luxurious ill-fated R.M.S Titanic. This movie shows all emotions and takes you on a full journey.
