# 7 Frighteningly Haunted Shows to Watch Preferably with Your Partner ...

It's almost Halloween and with Halloween comes some of the best haunted shows to watch right now. If you've been dying to be a little scared, to grab onto your partner's arm and hide your eyes – this is the list for you! I've got the top 7 haunted shows to watch right now! While some of them might not be around anymore, that's okay – Netflix is there for a reason. So, you ready to see what you should be watching for Halloween?

## 1. American Horror Story

Of course, at the top of the list of haunted shows to watch is going to be American Horror Story. This show actually freaks me out so much, I have to cover my eyes sometimes! The Coven is completely insane and it's honestly, a great show. It's scary, it's creepy, it's totally amazing and it's ideal for Halloween – or any time if you are looking to be scared.

## 2. The Walking Dead

This is a show that I watch all of the time but it still freaks me out! It's a show that is all about zombies, all about the walkers, all about living with them and trying to survive. It's a fantastic show, the plot is so original and just when you think you've figured it all out – they change it up on you. Are you ready for the new season?

## 3. American Gothic

While this show might not be around anymore, it is a show that is still amazing – and lives on forever! It's a show that Is super creepy, has a great plot line and the characters are seriously amazing. If you've never seen an episode of American Gothic, it's time to grab some popcorn and grab the couch.

## 4. A Haunting

This show freaks me out because it's all about possession, all about exorcism and all about ghostly encounters, which of course is something that creeps me out in general. It's a great show if you are really looking for confirmation that ghosts exist and so you'll be able to see people that have had these encounters.

## 5. Masters of Horror

Now, this is cheating a little bit because these are all just mini films, but to be honest, I think that these are amazing. Each short film is amazing and oh-so-different and I have to tell you, the story lines are incredible. If you want to be freaked out, you've got to watch Masters of Horror.

## 6. Supernatural

Now, I actually love this particular show. The fact Dean from Gilmore Girls is in it makes it even better! I love the brothers, I love the story lines and I love the plots of every single episode. If you want something twisty and freaky at the same time, this is the show you want to watch.

## 7. In the Flesh

Finally, this is a new show and I absolutely love it! It's again, all about zombies, but it's a unique, different type of story line. If you want to be freaked out in a different kind of way, you'll love this show – and you'll get very attached to each and every character.

Now that you know all about some of the best haunted TV shows out there, which one is your favorite? Do you have any more that you watch? Which shows are your favorite? Share 'em!
