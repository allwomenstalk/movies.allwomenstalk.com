# The Backstreet Boys Documentary Trailer is Here & Its Everything ...

Backstreet's back, alright! (Is that joke getting too old?!)

Anyways, the first trailer for the boy band's upcoming documentary "Backstreet Boys: Show Em What You're Made Of" has finally arrived! It's good stuff.

The 2-minute teaser opens with a few flashback scenes of A.J. McLean, Howie Dorough, Nick Carter, Kevin Richardson and Brian Littrell in their glory days, the late '90s. "From 1999 to about 2002, we were the biggest band in the world. Nobody thought it was going to be as big it was," they said.

The trailer then hops over to present-day BSB and gives fans an inside look at their London album recording and more. From the looks of it, fans can expect lots of heart-warming scenes, and some drama too! Watch the trailer:
